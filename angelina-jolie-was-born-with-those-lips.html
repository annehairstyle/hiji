Angelina Jolie was born with those lips

Posted by Artie Phelan on Monday, June 10, 2024


From the cover of Hello! 2/10/09 edition. People and Hello have more photos from the series this week

People magazine and the UK’s Hello! magazine have exclusive pictures of Angelina Jolie when she was a kid. After viewing some of the old photos, I can say without a doubt that Angelina was born with those lips, and that she really takes after her mother in the looks department. See this week’s issue of People for more.

Angelina also gave an interview to Good Morning America yesterday morning. She talked about her experience winning an Oscar, and how much she’s rooting for Brad come Oscar night. She also said that Brad’s movies are the only movies she really likes to watch. Interesting.

Outside her own family, Angelina Jolie is an Oscar-winning superstar of an actress. But the Changeling star, who’s nominated for another Oscar for her role in that film, definitely puts her family with Brad Pitt first, including her man’s movies!

“I feel very very lucky that I have won in the past,” she told Good Morning America this morning (Angie won Best Supporting Actress for Girl, Interrupted in 1999). “Listen, it’s lovely to be nominated. But I’m hoping Benjamin Button does really good on the night…. I’m rooting for Brad.” Awards aside, Angie even prefers watching Brad’s work over her own, admitting, “I like the process of doing them more than watching them. No, I just, I don’t really like to watch movies. I love to watch Brad’s movies.”

Despite her successful acting career her six kids – Maddox, Pax, Zahara, Shiloh, Knox and Vivienne – will always take precedence over everything else in her life. After her kids, Angie says her priorities are being a partner to Brad, followed by her philanthropic work around the globe.

“[I’m] trying to educate myself and trying to learn about the world, and trying to do some good things while I’m alive,” she reveals.

From Ok! Magazine

I think Angie’s being genuine. She knows that she really doesn’t have a shot at winning this year, going up against Meryl Streep and Kate Winslet. Brad doesn’t really have a shot either, much as I’d like to see him pull it out in the end. But out of the two of them, I think Brad and Benjamin Button are closer to taking home gold come Oscar night.
