Halsey Parents: Who Are Chris Frangipane And Nicole Frangipane?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, June 8, 2024

Chris Frangipane and Nicole Frangipane are the parents of popular American singer, Halsey. According to reports, Halsey did not have a really good relationship with her parents until she became very popular. It is believed that they now have a healthy relationship.

Chris Frangipane

Who is Chris Frangipane? Chris Frangipane is the father of American singer, Halsey.

Chris Frangipane children

How many children does Chris Frangipane have? Chris Frangipane has one child aside from Halsey. The other child is called Dante Frangipane.

Nicole Frangipane

Who is Nicole Frangipane? Nicole Frangipane is the mother of popular American singer, Halsey.

Nicole Frangipane age

How old is Nicole Frangipane? Nicole Frangipane is 50 years old; born on July 20, 1973.

Nicole Frangipane children

How many children does Nicole Frangipane have? Nicole Frangipane has three children. They are Halsey, Dante Frangipane and Sevian Frangipane.

Halsey age

How old is Halsey? Halsey is 27 years old; born on September 29, 1994.

Halsey songs

What are some of the popular songs by Halsey? Halsey released her debut EP Room 93 after she got signed by Astralwerks in 2014. In 2015, she released her first album dubbed Badlands. She has since released quite a lot of songs. Some of them include;

    • Without Me.
    • Him & I.
    • Eastside.
  • Could Have Been Me.
  • Bad at Love.
  • Gasoline.
  • Nightmare.
