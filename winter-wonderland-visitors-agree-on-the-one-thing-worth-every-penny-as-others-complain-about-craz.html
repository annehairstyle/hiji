Winter Wonderland visitors agree on the one thing worth every penny as others complain about craz

Posted by Kelle Repass on Saturday, June 29, 2024

Winter Wonderland has returned to Hyde Park for the festive season.

This year's Christmas attraction has once again left Londoners and tourists alike outraged at the 'crazy' prices.

However, there's one thing there that reviews on TripAdvisor and Google all seem to agree is worth the money.

READ MORE: 'I went to Winter Wonderland with just £20 and I couldn't believe what I got for my money'

Winter Wonderland currently has a Tripadvisor rating of four stars and 4.4 on Google and while many complain about the high costs, many visitors have recently been raving about the circus.

In the Circus Megadome, you can find performances of both Zippos Christmas Circus and Cirque Berserk.

Inside the UK's largest circus tent, Zippos Christmas Circus is aimed at young families with performances at 12:00, 14:00 and 16:00.

Cirque Berserk is aimed at those over 12-years-old, and is on at 18:00, 19:00 and 20:00.

Recent reviews praise the attraction, saying: "Cirque Berserk was incredible."

Other visitors called the circus "fabulous", "amazing" and "brilliant".

One said that Zippos Circus "made the day" and another hailed it as "great fun".

Off-peak tickets for Zippos cost £11 for adults and £8 for kids. Cirque Birserk starts at £12 for adults and £9 for children.

Many reviews slam Winter Wonderland as "unnecessarily expensive", "extortionate" and one wrote "be prepared to splash some money". Another even said that 'Disneyland charges less".

While one visitor said Winter Wonderland "is very expensive", they also wrote: "The circus was absolutely brilliant and worth every penny we paid."

Similarly, another slated the place as: ""Money gauging chaos where staff are utterly miserable."

They went on to describe it as a "rip off" but praised the popular attraction: "Circus was great though and provided relief from the happenings outside of the tent in the rest of the park."

Zippos Christmas Circus lasts 50 minutes and features acrobatics, laugh-out-loud humour for the family and giant teddy bears.

Cirque Berserk runs for 45 minutes with dare-devil tricks and 'mind-blowing' stunts. A reviewer even wrote that they "just wish the circus was longer".
