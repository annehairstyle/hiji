Kaley Cuoco in Bathing Suit Shows Off Baby Bump Celebwell

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, June 14, 2024

Kaley Cuoco is showing off her baby bump in a bathing suit! The Flight Attendant star, who recently announced she was expecting her first child (a baby girl!) with actor Tom Pelphry, showed off her growing belly in a two-piece swimsuit via her Instagram Stories this week. "Boop," she captioned the snap, taken while she was lounging by the pool surrounded by mountains. The 36-year-old recently revealed some of the rules she follows when it comes to eating. Read on to see 7 ways Kaley Cuoco stays in shape and the photos that prove they work—and to get beach-ready yourself, don't miss these essential 30 Best-Ever Celebrity Bathing Suit Photos!

Kaley recently became less restrictive about her diet, which was a total game changer. "I'm much healthier now than I used to be. And I feel better than I ever have, but I also probably eat more than I ever have, which is interesting," she told Women's Health. "I'm actually eating smaller meals and drinking more water and less alcohol." She added that it all boils down to knowledge. "I think I used to be afraid to eat, and now I just know the right things to eat. There's a huge difference."

Kaley starts the day with a piece of sourdough toast slathered in peanut butter. For lunch, she always has something with tuna. "I love tuna, [I] love it on a salad, love it in a wrap, love it in its own scoop, love it with chips. I'm like obsessed with tuna," she told WH. Dinner usually consists of protein, like grilled salmon with baked potato and veggies. "If I had to say what I eat most, it would be salmon," she said.df44d9eab23ea271ddde7545ae2c09ec

"I learned over the past few years that I have to eat every two hours," Kaley told WH. "I just do, and I'm not talking about giant meals, but I literally start to fall apart, especially when I'm shooting…there has to be at least a snack or something that I'm eating because I just lose energy so quickly."

"I love a smoothie," Kaley told WH. "I think it's a great way to get a nice meal in there, and it's quite easy." Her "favorite fruit in the whole world"? Pears. 

Kaley has a go-to rule she learned from her nutritionist, who she has been working with since she was 15. "If I go to dinner, she immediately wants me to cut my plate in half," she told WH. After eating half she will "take a little break" and then keep eating if she is still hungry. However, "nine times out of 10," she realizes she is full. "Our eyes are definitely bigger than our stomach," she said. 

Kaley is "candy obsessed" but also loves salty snacks. "I love the act of [eating] popcorn and watching a movie," she told WH. However, she avoids sweets at night. "I just don't feel good after, and I don't feel good when I wake up."

"I no longer watch TV while I eat," Kaley revealed to WH. "I think that's a big distraction 'cause you're not thinking about what's going on…so it's just being very mindful."
