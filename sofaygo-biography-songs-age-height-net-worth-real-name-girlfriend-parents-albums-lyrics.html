Biography

SoFaygo (born October 3, 2001), whose real name is Andre Dontrel Burt Jr., is a rising star in the American music industry. He is a gifted rapper, songwriter, and singer whose distinctive style has garnered widespread acclaim.

In 2020, SoFaygo released his mixtape, Angelic 7, which included his breakout single from the previous year, Knock Knock. The song quickly gained traction and went viral on TikTok, catapulting him to newfound fame. Since then, Knock Knock has amassed over 200 million plays on Spotify, cementing his position as a force to be reckoned with in hip-hop.

What sets SoFaygo apart from his peers is his ability to seamlessly blend different genres, creating a unique, catchy, and thought-provoking sound. His music proves his creativity and artistic vision, as he fearlessly experiments with different beats and rhythms, all while staying true to his signature style.

Early Life & Education

Andre Dontrel Burt Jr., the rapper extraordinaire who goes by the stage name SoFaygo, was born on October 3, 2001, in Grand Rapids, Michigan. He was raised in an American family with African heritage, although details regarding his parents and siblings are scanty. His mother and father were both deeply religious individuals, with the former serving as an evangelist and the latter as a pastor.

While still young, SoFaygo relocated to Atlanta, Georgia, where he spent the rest of his childhood. There, he first got bitten by the music bug, recording his first solo track at the tender age of nine in the basement of a friend’s house.

During his high school years, SoFaygo went by the name Trvllinese. He was an ambitious young man, relentlessly striving for success both academically and musically. He poured his heart and soul into achieving his goals, but no indication of whether he pursued further education after graduating from Etowah High School.

It is not uncommon for celebrities to keep their personal lives under wraps to maintain their privacy and avoid unnecessary scrutiny. Nevertheless, fans and followers of SoFaygo continue to be curious about his love life and eagerly await any updates or announcements from the artist himself.

Career

SoFaygo has been steadily climbing the ladder of musical success since 2019. He debuted with his mixtape War, quickly gaining a local following. But it was his second mixtape, Angelic 7, that caught the attention of Lil Tecca, and the two formed a collaborative bond. The mixtape’s single, Knock Knock, went viral on TikTok, bringing him international acclaim.

In 2021, SoFaygo released his third mixtape, After Me, featuring Off the Map and Every Day. This album led to him signing to Travis Scott‘s Cactus Jack Records alongside fellow rappers Sheck Wes and Don Toliver. In July of that year, he announced that his debut album Pink Heartz was almost ready.

The anticipation grew when his track MP5 was included on Trippie Redd‘s Trip at Knight album in August. The lead single from his album, Let’s Lose Our Minds, was released on September 1, 2021, and marked his first release with Cactus Jack.

He continued to make headlines throughout 2022, releasing the B4PINK EP in preparation for Pink Heartz and being named as part of XXL’s 2022 Freshman Class. In July, an EP titled Babyjack, reportedly leaked by SoFaygo, was exclusively released on SoundCloud under the account prettyboyarchive.

Finally, on November 11th, 2022, SoFaygo‘s highly anticipated debut album, Pink Heartz, was released, featuring guest appearances from Ken Carson and other notable artists. His music continues to evolve and push boundaries, cementing his place in the music industry as a force to be reckoned with.
