Bill Hader And Rachel Bilson Split After 1 Year

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, June 12, 2024

Bill Hader and Rachel Bilson are over. People Magazine reported today that the 42-year-old actor and the 38-year-old actress first made their red carpet debut a few months ago while at the 2020 Golden Globe awards.

The makeup artist, Amy Nadine, dubbed them as the “cutest couple ever” at the time. Insiders who spoke with the outlet claimed their breakup was on great terms, however, their official representatives had said they are remaining silent on the news.

As most know, Rachel and Bill’s romance has been outside of the public eye for the most part, but it changed after they were first seen at a Tulsa, Oklahoma, Starbucks, together back in December of 2019. Around a month later, they were spotted hanging out at a Ralph’s in LA.

Around that time, sources said their relationship looked to be serious, but it was noted that they were just having a good time together. Ironically, the news comes not long after it was reported that their romance was going as strong as ever.

Back in February of this year, sources who spoke with People again claimed Bill and Rachel were getting along well. Moreover, the insider stated that The OC alum and the SNL star were looking very cozy as a couple.

Rachel reportedly loved Bill’s sense of humor, and they were having so much fun together. Bilson considered Bill as very compassionate, sweet, and considerate of her emotions, which she found refreshing and calming.

As it was noted above, they first made their relationship official, at least in the public’s eye, when they appeared on the red carpet together while at the Golden Globe Awards on the 4th of January. According to People, Bill and Rachel have known each other for years.
