NeNe Leakes Shows Porsha Williams Love On Social Media And Fans Are Surprised

Posted by Artie Phelan on Sunday, June 30, 2024

Porsha Williams shared a couple of pics on her social media account in which she’s with her mother, Diane, and baby girl, Pilar Jhena McKinley. The pics are a throwback to the RHOA ladies’ Greece vacay.

‘Off to Greece!! So blessed to take them with me to one of my bucket list vacays! @msdianeofficial @pilarjhena #Rhoa #ThrowBack’ Porsha captioned her post.

Fans are extremely surprised to see NeNe Leakes hopping in the comments section to show Porsha and her family some love.
