Biography

Channing Frye was born in White Plains, NY on May 17, 1983. NBA forward/center known for his outside shooting. He was drafted 8th overall by the New York Knicks in 2005, then went on to play with the Portland Trail Blazers, Phoenix Suns, Orlando Magic and Cleveland Cavaliers. He won an NBA Championship with Cleveland in 2016. He played alongside star NBA point guard Steve Nash on the Suns from 2009 to 2012. He played four years of college basketball at Arizona from 2001 to 2005.
