Kuma - Dog With a Blog (Dog)

Kuma was the starring dog in the Disney Channel series Dog With a Blog that aired from October 12, 2012 to September 25, 2015. Peyton Meyer played the role of Wes, one of the family members with the dog in the series. Kuma did not belong to Peyton outside the show and unfortunately passed away in 2018.
