Chris Evans wore his wedding ring to NY Comic Con, calls his wedding really great

Posted by Billy Koelling on Wednesday, June 5, 2024

Embed from Getty Images

Here are some photos of Chris Evans at the New York Comic Con on Saturday. Which begs the question: why the hell is Chris Evans doing appearances at any Comic Con during the SAG-AFTRA strike? The people who attended his discussion say that Chris avoided all talk of his previous or upcoming projects, and instead talked about his life, his dog, and his marriage. So that’s probably how he got around strike rules – it’s not specifically promotional for any work which does not have a waiver. As for the topics he could discuss, the man wore his wedding ring and his fans are basically in a full meltdown. You see, some of them believed that his wedding was an elaborate hoax or that Alba Baptista “trapped” him and he’s unhappy. That man was glowing and wearing his ring proudly.

Chris Evans is in marital bliss. The “Captain America” star, 42, confirmed on Saturday during an appearance at New York Comic Con that he’s officially off the market after getting married to Portuguese actress Alba Baptista.

“I got married,” Evans told Marvel fans, who responded with mass applause. “It was really, really great.”

The actor revealed he and Baptista, 26, had two ceremonies, with one taking place in Portugal and another on the East Coast. “They were wonderful and beautiful,” he said.

Evans admitted to the crowd at NYCC that planning two weddings was “a lot,” but since the wedding, he and the “Warrior Nun” actress have been “relaxing and enjoying life and reflecting” as a newlywed couple.

[From USA Today]

His light was on and his biological clock was ticking, and I bet you anything that Chris will become a father within the next year. And good for him! He knew what he wanted, he fell in love, he got married and they seemingly plan to live a quiet life in Portugal and Boston. I hope they’re very happy.
