Shabir Ahluwalia is an Indian actor, producer, and host who is best known for having been cast as Abhishek Mehra in the television drama series, Kumkum Bhagya. In addition to that, he is also known for his on-screen roles in projects such as Shootout at Lokhandwala, Kasamh Se, Kasautii Zindagii Kay, Laagi Tumse Lagan, Kya Hadsaa Kya Haqeeqat, Kyunki Saas Bhi Kabhi Bahu Thi, Kahi To Milenge, Kayamath, Kahiin To Hoga, and Fixerr.

    Shabir Ahluwalia Facts

  • In the year 2010, he appeared as a contestant on the 3rd season of the stunt reality television series, Fear Factor: Khatron Ke Khiladi, alongside Ritwik Bhattacharya, Dino Morea, Karan Singh Grover, Angad Bedi, and Milind Soman. Shabir Ahluwalia wrapped up his journey on the show as the winner.
  • He started appearing as Abhishek Prem Mehra in Kumkum Bhagya in 2014 and this role won him the 2015 Gold Award for “Best Actor in a Lead Role (Critics)”, the 2016 Indian Television Academy Award for “Best Actor – Popular”, and the 2018 Gold Award for “Best Actor in Lead Role (Popular)”.
  • In 2019, he was seen as Jaiveer Maalik in the Hindi action drama web series, Fixerr, in which he co-starred alongside Mahie Gill, Karishma Sharma, Tigmanshu Dhulia, and Varun Badola.
  • Shabir Ahluwalia has served as a host on shows such as Nach Baliye, Guinness World Records – Ab India Todega, and Dancing Queen.
