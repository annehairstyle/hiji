Meet Too Hot To Handle Beauty Christine Obanor

Christine Obanor (age: 26, April 20, 1997) is a  businesswoman and owner of Beautifaux Nails. Known for her impressive height of 6’1″, Christine describes herself as a “Houston Hottie.”

She has made a name for herself in the fashion industry, having worked with numerous fashion brands and even graced the runway of FG New York Fashion Week Bridal 2020.

With a strong presence on social media, Christine has amassed an impressive following on Instagram, boasting over 34.2 K followers. She frequently shares stunning photos that showcase her beauty and fashion sense.

However, in 2023 Christine truly catapulted into the limelight when she joined the famous American-British reality television dating game show, “Too Hot To Handle,” for its fifth season.

Christine’s participation in “Too Hot To Handle” has further propelled her into the spotlight, allowing her to expand her fanbase and gain even more recognition.

Christine Obanor Early Life and Parents

Christine Obanor was born on April 20, 1997, to her mother, Johanna Obanor, and father, Ken Obanor, in Houston City, Texas, and grew up alongside her siblings Kenneth, Kevin, and Kelly Obanor. Her parents, Johanna and Ken Obanor, provided a supportive environment for Christine and her siblings to thrive.

Her siblings, particularly her brother Kevin Obanor, have also achieved fame in their respective fields. Kevin is a professional basketball player, currently representing the Toronto Raptors in the National Basketball Association (NBA).

During her formative years, Christine displayed a strong passion for education and personal growth. She pursued her higher education at the University of North Texas, where she earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Fashion Merchandising.

Additionally, she pursued a double minor in Business and Marketing while at the university. Christine’s dedication and hard work during her undergraduate studies allowed her to excel academically.

Christine Obanor Career

Before joining the University of North Texas, In September 2014, Christine started her career as a Hostess at TGI Fridays, where she worked until November 2014.

Simultaneously, in August 2014, She embarked on her modeling journey by joining the esteemed Neal Hamil Agency. During her time there, she had the opportunity to learn about the behind-the-scenes aspects and planning tactics of fashion shows.

While pursuing her college education, Christine also worked at Joe’s Crab Shack, gaining additional experience in the service industry. In 2017, she secured a position as a seasonal sales associate at Victoria’s Secret, further enhancing her customer service skills and product knowledge.

Building on her passion for fashion, Christine transitioned into the role of a Fashion Stylist at Stitch Fix. This opportunity allowed her to apply her expertise in curating stylish and personalized outfits for clients.

However, Her entrepreneurial spirit led her to venture into her own business. In 2020, she launched her own company called Beautifaux Nails, focusing on providing high-quality nail products and services.
