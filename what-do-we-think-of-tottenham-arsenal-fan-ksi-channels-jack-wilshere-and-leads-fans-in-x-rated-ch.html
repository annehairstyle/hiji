'What do we think of Tottenham?' Arsenal fan KSI channels Jack Wilshere and leads fans in X-rated ch

Posted by Kelle Repass on Wednesday, June 19, 2024

YouTuber-turned boxer KSI did his best Jack Wilshere impression to get a large crowd in Australia trolling Tottenham.

The die-hard Arsenal fan and his Prime business partner, Logan Paul, made a TV appearance on the Australian 'Today Show' before hundreds of their fans in Sydney.

Presented with a megaphone on a balcony outside their studios, KSI made no mistake taking the opportunity to show his love for the Premier League leaders.

"What do we think of Tottenham?" he cried to the fans below, before being greeted with the classic response of: "S***!"

He replied with: "What do we think of s***?", and his fans stuck to the fun as they bellowed: "Tottenham!"

"Thank you," said KSI, and his fans responded: "That's alright! We hate Tottenham, we hate Tottenham!"

It was reminiscent of Wilshere conducting Gooners in north London to celebrate an FA Cup win in 2015.

Whilst KSI and Paul received a lot of love from their fans in Sydney, they weren't given the best of welcomes at UFC 284 on the other side of the country in Perth.

The big screen camera panned to the duo sitting together in their front-two seats for Islam Makhachev's win against Alexander Volkanovski.

The business partners were hilariously booed by the crowd inside the RAC Arena, though they seemed to relish their roles as heels.

Prime was recently named the official drink of the UFC, with their branding being displayed in the octagon.

Away from the UFC, KSI will be pleased to see that his beloved Arsenal remain top of the Premier League table, although they dropped points at the weekend with a surprise draw at home to Brentford.
