BLACKPINK are coming back with mini album 'Kill This Love'

Posted by Artie Phelan on Friday, June 7, 2024

BLINKS get ready, because BLACKPINK are coming back in our area with their new mini album ‘Kill This Love!’

YG Entertainment confirmed the long awaited comeback of the fab four-piece with a gorgeous teaser poster of Lisa, also telling fans that ‘Kill This Love’ will be released April 5th.

This will be the girls first release since their record-breaking comeback last June with ‘Square Up’ lead by the banger that was ‘DDU-DU DDU-DU’, which broke records becoming the first Korean girl group to chart on the Billboard Hot 100 and UK Official charts with a full Korean song. This is also the girls first comeback of since signing to UMG and Interscope last year.

YG Entertainment explained “The choreography is expected to be more dynamic than the choreography of any song that BLACKPINK has introduced and will meet the expectation and eye level of fans around the world.” The label further stated that the group have spent four days filming the music video, hinting it is going to be incredibly epic.

The new music will arrive just over a month before their two-date stint in the UK, at the Manchester Arena on May 21st and their sold out show at Wembley Arena on May 22nd.
