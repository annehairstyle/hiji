Ed Sheeran Rocks Out With Newest Releases 'Blow' and 'Best Part of Me'

Posted by Artie Phelan on Thursday, June 6, 2024

Global pop sensation Ed Sheeran has been releasing songs from his forthcoming album “No. 6 Collaborations Project” which is set to drop soon on July 12 and has been teasing us with a number of high-profile musical collaborations. Ahead of the record’s release next week, Ed Sheeran continues to release some of his fireworks and expand his musical palette by releasing two new songs featuring Bruno Mars, Chris Stapleton, and Yebba.

The pop star recently dropped “Blow” featuring Bruno Mars and Chris Stapleton and another song titled “Best Part of Me” featuring Yebba, both of which will appear on his upcoming album titled “No. 6 Collaborations Project”. “Blow” is produced by Bruno Mars and “Best Part of Me” is produced by Benny Blanco, Joe Rubel and Ed Sheeran. Both singles are accompanied by lyric videos. (Watch below)

This upcoming record ‘No. 6 Collaborations Project’ by Ed Sheeran is a great mix of all styles featuring major artists from the industry and shows off his ability while exploring almost every musical genre on the new tracks. Ed Sheeran expressed his thoughts stating, “I loved making both records. Yebba is phenomenal, she’s onto massive things. Bruno, Chris and I had a lot of fun recording. Play them loud, tell your mates, enjoy!”

Previously, Ed released the tracks- “I Don’t Care” with Justin Bieber, “Beautiful People” with Khalid and “Cross Me” with Chance the Rapper and PnB Rock, which all appear on the upcoming album. Ed recently shared the star-studded tracklist for the album on his official Instagram account and it’s one hell of a lineup. This 15-track record is going to feature more artists such as Camila Cabello, Cardi B, Travis Scott, Eminem, Young Thug, Ella Mai, Stormzy and many more.

We’re absolutely thrilled to listen to these new songs by Ed Sheeran and can’t wait for the upcoming release. Stay tuned for more latest updates!
