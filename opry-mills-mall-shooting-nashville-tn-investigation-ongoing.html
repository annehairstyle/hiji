Opry Mills Mall Shooting Nashville TN, Investigation Ongoing

Posted by Kelle Repass on Tuesday, June 18, 2024
0SHARESShareTweet

Opry Mills Mall Shooting – Because there is a potential that a gunman is present at the Opry Mills Shopping Mall on Wednesday evening, there is a considerable presence of law enforcement officers there. Since six o’clock in the evening, people have begun departing the retail complex. This is the bare minimum that has occurred. The initial statement made by dispatch was that they did have officers responding to the event,

despite the fact that there was a call concerning guns fired. On the other hand, they did not possess any additional information. During a chat that took place between one of our reporters and a law enforcement officer who was present at the scene, the officer claimed that they did not find anything that might be construed as suspicious.

It has been claimed by the Metro Police Department that the event that took place was not at the movie theater but rather at a Haagen-Dazs establishment. From the beginning, we stated that there was a brawl that took place at the movie theater.

During the course of the investigation, it was discovered that there was no proof of a shooting or gunfire, and that no one had been injured. It has been reported that individuals got the sound of metal chairs hitting the floor confused with the sound of gunfire, as stated by the police. An effort has been made to clean up the shopping mall.
