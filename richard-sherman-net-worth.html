Richard Sherman Wiki Biography

Richard Kevin Sherman was born on 30 March 1988, in Compton, California. USA, and is an American Football player in the position of corner-back, playing for the NFL’s Seattle Seahawks. Since he entered the league, he has managed to become a player with the third most interceptions at 25 and defended passes at 75.

Are you wondering what the net worth of Richard Sherman is, as of early 2016? Sherman is estimated to have a net worth of about $12 million, accumulated during his football career since 2011. He is considered by many analysts as one of the best corner-backs in the NFL, having helped Seattle Seahawks to many victories. Reports state that he earns an impressive $11.5 million with the team, making him one of the highest paid football players in the league.

Richard Sherman went to Dominguez High School, where he played football and ran track.
