What is JT the Bigga Figga's Net Worth?

JT the Bigga Figga is an American rapper and music producer who has a net worth of $5 million. JT the Bigga Figga was born in San Francisco, California in November 1971. He has produced more than one hundred albums for artists such as Master P, Daz Dillinger, San Quinn, The Game, and Messy Marv. JT the Bigga Figga founded Get Low Recordz in 1991. His debut studio album Don't Stop til We Major was released in 1992 and he has released a total of 15 solo albums. JT the Bigga Figga has also released albums as part of the Bay Area Bosses and has made appearances on songs by artists such as Rappin' 4-Tay, The Gamblaz, and Young Buck. In 2002 he released the E-book The CEO Manual. As an actor he has appeared in the 2004 Spike Lee film Sucker Free City. In 2015 JT the Bigga Figga started an on demand service called Trapflix which focuses on urban films, concert footage, music, and documentaries.
