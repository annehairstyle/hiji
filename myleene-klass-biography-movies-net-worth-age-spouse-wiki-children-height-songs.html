Early Life & Education

Myleene Klass, now 45 years old, was born on April 6, 1978, in Great Yarmouth, England. She has a mixed heritage of Austrian and Filipino.

Her father, Oscar, was a Royal Navy submariner, and her mother, Bong, was a nurse. She has a younger sister, Jessie, and a younger brother, Don.

She grew up in Gorleston, Norfolk, where she attended St Mary’s Roman Catholic Primary School and Cliff Park High School.

She showed early musical talent and learned to play the piano, violin, and harp. She also sang in the school choir and participated in local musical productions.

She won a scholarship to study at the prestigious Royal Academy of Music in London, where she graduated with honors in 2000. She also studied musical theatre at the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art.

She signed a five-album deal with Universal Classics and Jazz and released her first album, Moving On, in 2003.

The album was a critical and commercial success, reaching number two on the classical charts and 32 on the UK charts. It also earned her a nomination for the Classical BRIT Award for Album of the Year.

She followed it up with her second album, Myleene’s Music for Romance, in 2007, which also topped the classical charts and reached number 43 on the UK charts.

She also performed at various events and venues, such as the Royal Albert Hall, the Classical BRIT Awards, the Live 8, and the Nobel Peace Prize concert.

Klass also pursued a career in television, where she proved to be a popular and versatile presenter. She hosted various shows, including CD: UK, The National Lottery, The All-Star Talent Show, The X Factor, Popstar to Operastar, BBQ Champ, The One Show, and Loose Women.

She also appeared as a contestant on the reality show I’m a Celebrity…Get Me Out of Here! in 2006, where she impressed the viewers with her courage, charm, and bikini body. She finished second behind Matt Willis and boosted her profile and popularity.

She later returned to the show as a presenter for the spin-off show I’m a Celebrity…Get Me Out of Here! NOW! in 2007 and 2008. She also participated in the South African version of the show in 2023, where she emerged as the winner.

Klass also ventured into modeling, becoming one of the UK’s most sought-after and highest-paid models.

She signed a lucrative deal with Marks & Spencer in 2007 and became the face of their lingerie, swimwear, and clothing ranges. She also modeled for Littlewoods, Pantene, La Senza, Next, and Mothercare brands.

She also launched her range of products, such as nail polish, jewelry, perfume, fitness DVDs, books, and baby clothes.

She also posed for various magazines, such as FHM, Maxim, Cosmopolitan, and Hello!, and was voted as one of the sexiest women in the world by several polls.
