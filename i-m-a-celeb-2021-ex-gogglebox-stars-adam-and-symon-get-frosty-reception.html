Im A Celeb 2021: Ex-Gogglebox stars Adam and Symon get frosty reception

Posted by Kelle Repass on Sunday, June 23, 2024

Former Gogglebox stars Adam and Symon received a very frosty reception when they entered the I’m A Celeb camp.

In an episode that’s set to air tonight, the reality stars entered the camp the morning after Jack Vidgen’s “violent” eviction.

The other camp mates were visibly shocked to see the two intruders walk in, and they made it clear they weren’t thrilled.

“Not these two idiots,” Colin Fassnidge said loudly.

Some of the celebs seemed a tad confused as to who Adam and Symon were.

“You’re the Googlebox boys?” Dipper hilariously guessed.

A few stars went up to greet the intruders but Grant Denyer, Paulini and Pettifleur Berenger didn’t even get up from their beds to say hello.

The boys received the frostiest reception from Toni Pearen who turned to Jess Eva after the pair walked in and said: “What the f**k is going on?”

Toni later burst into tears and told Colin: “I can’t handle anymore people.”

Speaking to the camera later in the episode, Toni said her “brain exploded” when she saw Adam and Symon enter.

“I was seriously lost for words,” she said. “I just didn’t think that this late in the game, two more people would be introduced to the camp.

“We’ve got our thing … (But now) we’ve got these two new people who haven’t lived it like we’ve lived it,” she said.

Fellow camp mate Abbie Chatfield said about Adam and Symon: “I do feel bad for them because everyone here has just been through so much together and we’ve done the hard yards and we’re just sort of like, ‘Hi, I’m Abbie, nice to meet you … I’m going to have a nap.’

“We’re just not all there at the moment, unfortunately for them,” Abbie added.

Adam and Symon were well aware some of the celebs weren’t happy to see them, saying to the camera later on: “They were a bit flat! We came during the middle of lunchtime so everyone was like, ‘We’re eating, sorry, can you come back in an hour?’”

Adam and Symon appeared on 10 seasons of Gogglebox before they quit the show in 2019.

“We wanted to do things a bit more creative,” Symon told TV Week last year. “It was just the right time in our lives.”

Check out Adam and Symon’s awkward unveiling on I’m A Celeb tonight at 7.30pm on Channel 10
