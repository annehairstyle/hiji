Who is India Love?

India Love is an American social media celebrity and reality television personality. She is known for her Tumblr blogging and the reality show 'The Westbrooks'.

Who are India Love's parents?

The future star was born on February 3, 1996 in Compton, California. She once admitted that she came from a wealthy family. India's parents run several successful businesses together, including a cigar company. Her father's name is Warren Westbrooks. The star said that he always did his best to provide for the family and be present in his kids' lives. India's mother's name is Candy Westbrooks. The famous blogger has 4 sisters and 2 brothers. India's mother, father, sisters, and brothers also star in the reality series 'The Westbrooks' that follows her family life. The reality star was close to her grandmother, who died from cancer. After that, India donated money to cancer research.

Blogging career

When India Love was 15 years old, she created a Tumblr blog, where she uploaded pictures of herself. To her surprise, she gained a decent number of followers pretty fast. After getting a start on the blogging platform, she created popular Instagram and Twitter pages. You can also find her on YouTube.
