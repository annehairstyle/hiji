Candace Hiltz Murder: Who Killed Her? Where is Daughter, Paige Hiltz?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, June 27, 2024

The murder of Candace Hiltz brought chills to the spine of many people hence many people were seeking justice for the young mother but it remains unserved till now

Candace Hiltz was a young mother who was brutally murdered and the Oxygen True Crime series has shared the story. In this article, we’re going to learn more about her and the updates that have come up so far.

Who killed Candace Hiltz?

In 2018, the murder case of Candace Hiltz attracted some hope after some crucial evidence was revealed but the murderer remains a mystery.

Candace Hiltz was a brilliant student who used to do Calculus at the age of 11 years and even dreamt of becoming a Supreme Court Justice. She was accepted in Standford Law School but her dreams got shattered.

All that she wanted to become went with her to her grave after she was murdered at her home. Candace Hiltz was shot multiple times with three different guns which were wrapped in a green comforter. She was shoved beneath a bed when her mother discovered her dead body.

The murder of Candace Hiltz happened in 2006 but now the murderer is still not found. Robert Dodd was however convicted in 2018 for abusing public records and served 18 months of jail term plus a $5,000 fine but was not found guilty of murdering the young girl.

Where is Candace Hiltz’s Daughter, Paige Hiltz?

Candace Hiltz’s incident came as a blow to her family as her brother was announced to be mentally sick hence was sent to Colorado Mental Health institution.

Her infant daughter, Paige Hiltz was born with an abnormal accumulation of cerebrospinal fluid. After the murder of Candace Hiltz, her daughter used to live with his grandmother in Cotopaxi but she is currently no more.
