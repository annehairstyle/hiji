Drew Pinsky Biography | Wikipedia

Drew Pinsky is an American TV personality, internist and specialist of addiction medicine currently serving as a regular medical analyst for CNN News. He is also recognized as the host of his own syndicated radio talk show known as Loveline from 1984 to 2016. Drew even hosted an HLN/CNN talk show known as Dr. Drew On Call in 2016. Previously, he served as a medical analyst and presenter for a Sunday Night news program on KROQ-FM in Los Angeles, California.
