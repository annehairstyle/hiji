Bachelor In Paradise 2015 Finale Spoilers: Kirk DeWindt Breaks Up With Carly Waddell in Shocking Spl

Posted by Artie Phelan on Sunday, June 9, 2024

Bachelor In Paradise 2015 spoilers tease that there will be shocking break-up during the Season 2 finale on ABC. The promo video for the final episode of Bachelor In Paradise implies that Jade Roper and Tanner Tolbert break up, but that was just some creative editing on behalf of the Bachelor producers. Bachelor In Paradise 2015 spoilers tease that the couple that breaks up during the finale and leaves everyone in Mexico shocked and devastated – is actually Carly Waddell and Kirk DeWindt.

Carly Waddell and Krik DeWind hit it off in paradise basically on night one – and aside from Jade and Tanner, they were probably the most solid couple in paradise. If any couples on the summer reality TV show really fell in love and were going to get married, we all would have assumed that Carly and Kirk were one of them. But, according to Bachelor In Paradise 2015 spoilers, Kirk blindsides Carly during the finale and dumps her!

A new report from US Weekly Magazine reveals that Carly thought she was getting a Neil Lane engagement ring from Kirk – instead she got a broken heart and publicly dumped. An insider dished to US Weekly, “Carly said she thought she’d found her soul mate and was talking about having babies with him. But Kirk totally blindsided her and said he was far from being on the same page. It was so dramatic and real. Carly was totally shocked when Kirk broke up with her. She’s still very upset.” The insider went on to say that while Kirk was breaking up with her Carly had a total meltdown and ran off sobbing – she informed the producers she wanted to go home ASAP.

We are still baffled by what exactly when wrong between Kirk and Carly – they seemed so happy. But, Kirk may have just been telling Carly what he wanted to hear so that he had an excuse to stay in paradise and be on television. Carly bounces back fast though, word on the Internet is that she is dating her fellow BIP cast member Dan Cox, they have been seen together since the show ended and are reportedly really in to each other.

Why do you think Kirk dumped Carly? Was he ever that in to her? Did he just want to be on television? Let us know what you think in the comments below!
