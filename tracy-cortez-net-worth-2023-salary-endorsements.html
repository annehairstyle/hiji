Tracy Cortez is a 29-year-old professional fighter from America competing in the women’s flyweight division of the Ultimate Fighting Championship. She is currently the No.14 ranked contender in the weight class.

Making her octagon debut in 2019, Cortez has had only four fights in the promotion so far and still managed to get herself a spot in the rankings table. This was because of her professional record of 10 wins and 1 loss. She is currently on a nine-fight win streak.

What is Tracy Cortez’s Net Worth in 2023?

According to multiple sources, Tracy Cortez’s net worth 2023 is approximately $100,000.

Coming from a family of Mexican immigrants in America, the primary source of her income comes from her performance inside the octagon.

Apart from her UFC fights, Tracy Cortez is also seen promoting ‘Ghost Lifestyle products via her Instagram account, which ranges from apparel to energy supplements.

Tracy Cortez’s Salary

For her last outing at UFC 274, Tracy Cortez earned a lump sum of $40K, which includes $18K(base salary), 18K(win bonus), and $4K(via sponsorships).

At UFC 274, Cortez fought Melissa Gatto and won the bout via a unanimous decision on May 7, 2022, in Phoenix, Arizona, United States.

What nationality is Tracy Cortez?

Tracy Cortez's nationality is American with Mexican descent.

Her family is of Mexican immigrants, and hence, Cortez is often seen supporting both countries while she is up for a fight inside the octagon.

While talking to the media before her fight at UFC Fight Night: Grasso vs. Shevchenko 2, Cortez stated that it is her honor to be Mexican-American and to know that she has the support of the ‘Raza’(which means people).

Who does Tracy Cortez have tattooed on her arm?

Tracy Cortez has a portrait tattoo of her late brother Jose Cortez in his remembrance.

Jose Cortez is Tracy Cortez’s eldest brother, who dreamt of becoming a UFC fighter, which he did achieve in 2008. For his debut, he had a decision win over former UFC veteran Drew Fickett, and this was Cortez’s first time watching an MMA fight.

However, Cortez could not continue to fight after his debut due to his cardiovascular problems, which later turned out to be cancer. In 2011, he passed away, and it was then Tracy Cortez got her tattoo.
