Who Is Miss Universe Spain Angela Ponce? Whats Her Relationship Status?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, June 20, 2024

Every now and then, a Miss Universe arises after competing with several contestants along with lots of hard work.

Later this year, Miss Universe pageant features the first transgender woman who made a history. Transgender Angela Ponce won Spain’s Miss Universe last month after beating nearly two dozen contestants.

And she will be the first transgender woman to take part in the worldwide competition. Until the year 2013, Transgender woman were not allowed to compete when Canada’s Jenna Talackova questioned the rules.

After winning the competition, transgender Angela Ponce wrote on her Instagram account,

“Bringing the name and colors of Spain before the universe is my great dream,”

“My goal is to be a spokesperson for a message of inclusion, respect, and diversity not only for the LGBTQ+ community but also for the entire world.”

Besides, it’s not the first time that Angela Ponce tried for the beauty pageant. Angela Ponce competed in the year 2015 on the Miss World Stage but lost to Miss Barcelona Mireia Lalaguna.

At that time, Angela Ponce told the Spanish newspaper 20 Minutos,

“Society is not educated for diversity, it’s what made me go public, I mean, here I am and I’m not weird,”

“I just have a different story, a woman who came to life differently but I’m a woman.”

With this let’s get some details on who is Miss Universe Spain Angela Ponce, who was she before? Also is this gorgeous transgender Angela Ponce dating or is she single? Explore Angela Ponce wiki facts, her parents info, and her future plans after being crowned as Miss Universe Spain!

Angela Ponce Wiki Facts

Spanish model and beauty pageant titleholder, Angela Ponce (born 1991-1992) won Miss Spain 2018. Currently, Angela Ponce age is 27, and Ponce is originally from Seville, Spain with a tall height of 175cm.

Angela Ponce parents Maria Jose Camacho Notario (mother) and Eustaquio Ponce Moreno (father) are professional restaurateurs. Much details of  Angela Ponce parents are not revealed in the media yet.

Angela Ponce also has a sister named Amanda Ponce Camacho. As for Angela Ponce her boyfriend, she is not dating anyone now.

Born as a male, Ponce realized what transsexuality was and at age 11, she started the journey of transitioning into a female. She revealed that as a child she was different from other boys.

To complete her transition from a male to a female, Ponce underwent surgery in the year 2014. But it wasn’t that easy, it took years of hormone therapy. As of now, Angela Ponce has turned out to become one hell of a gorgeous lady who won the most acclaimed beauty pageant, Miss Universe Spain.
