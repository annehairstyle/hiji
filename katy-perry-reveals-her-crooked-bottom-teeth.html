She is one of the biggest pop stars of all time, selling a whopping 11 million records worldwide and worth an estimated $44m fortune.

However, perennially preened Katy Perry has surprisingly not yet got around to fixing her crooked teeth.

While many of her famous peers have splashed out on expensive veneers to correct their troublesome molars, the 27-year-old has left her bottom row of teeth in their natural state.

Not so perfect smile: Katy Perry revealed her crooked teeth on a Perez Hilton All Access: Katy Perry special last night

The Teenage Dream star revealed her not-so-perfect smile on Perez Hilton All Access: Katy Perry special, last night.

Chatting to the blogger, she protruded her bottom choppers out of her mouth and pointed at them with her pink painted talons. 

In the special, Hilton goes behind-the-scenes of her music video for The One That Got Away.

Straight talking: Katy's top teeth are meanwhile perfectly symmetrical

He also travels with Katy on her U.K. tour, which was at the start of the year, and talks to her about her career and her personal life, in which she opens up about her marriage with Russell Brand

It comes as Brand this week said he had no regrets about his union with the pop star.

In fact, the British actor and comedian admitted he still loves his estranged wife in an interview on the Ellen DeGeneres show.

Opening up: Perez talks to her about her career and her personal life, in which she opens up about her marriage with Russell Brand

The 36-year-old Get Him To The Greek star said: 'I still love her as a human being.

'But, sometimes when you’re in a relationship I suppose it doesn’t work out, does it?

'But that doesn’t mean I regret it or anything. I was very happy to be married with her.'

Practice run: The blogger accompanied Katy on her U.K tour and went behind-the-scenes of rehearsals for her California Dreams extravaganza

Brand appeared on the U.S. talkshow, alongside fellow British exports and YouTube sensations Sophia Grace Brownlee and Rosie McClelland.

Russell went on, to cheers from the audience: 'She’s such a beautiful human being and I just have only love and positivity for her,.'

Last December Brand filed for divorce from Katy. It was granted by a California judge in February and will become effective in July.

Both stars have new love interests now - most recently Katy who has been linked to Florence + The Machine guitarist Robert Ackroyd.

Candid: Perez documented Katy's every move

Pink lady: At the time of the interview Katy sported a pink hair colour

Russell, meanwhile, has been linked to a string of women, including model-artist Oriela Medellin and Hungarian model Nikolett Barabas.

During the interview, Russell reflected on how things had not turned out as he had expected between him and Katy.

'You can’t absolutely make everything the way you want it to be in life.

'Sometimes thing are just different and then you do just move with that and try and remain in contact with what is beautiful about yourself and each other … with any situation.'
