Maddie Corman

Posted by Billy Koelling on Monday, June 10, 2024

01/12/2015 – Maddie Corman – 3rd Annual Paul Rudd All-Star Bowling Benefit in New York City on January 12, 2015 – Lucky Strikes Lanes – New York City, NY, USA – Photo Credit: MJ Photos / PRPhotos.com

Birth Name: Madeleine Arbi Cornman

Place of Birth: Manhattan, New York City, New York, U.S.

Date of Birth: August 15, 1969

Ethnicity: Ashkenazi Jewish

Maddie Corman is an American actress.

She is married to film and television director Jace Alexander, with whom she has three children. Jace is the son of actress Jane Alexander.

Maddie’s paternal grandfather was named Noah Cornman (the son of Charles Cornman and Jennie). Noah was born in New York, to Russian Jewish parents. Charles was the son of Joseph Cornman and Rachel Katz.

Maddie’s paternal grandmother was named Jessie.

Maddie’s maternal grandfather was Murry Kerman (the son of Louis David Kerman and Yetta Haker). Louis was the son of Moses Kerman and Annie Eioler. Yetta was the daughter of Baruch Haker and Lea Traner.

Maddie’s maternal grandmother was Lillian Shapiro (the daughter of Sam Shapiro and Freda Rashkin). Lillian was born in New York. Sam was the son of Selig Shapiro and Bluma Gold. Freda was the daughter of Israel Rashkin and Jenny.
