Is Khloe Kardashian Pregnant With Tristan Thompsons Baby Again?

Posted by Kelle Repass on Saturday, June 22, 2024

Is Khloe Kardashian pregnant with Tristan Thompson’s baby again? Fans are buzzing that Khloe Kardashian has the pregnancy glow and has been covering her stomach in photos, making many think that she’s pregnant and ready to give True Thompson a sibling. Those who watch Keeping Up With The Kardashians (KUWK) saw the storyline play out where Khloe asked Tristan Thompson to father a second child with her. Though the show made it appear that Tristan would be a sperm donor, many people believe that it is just a cover-up as Khloe doesn’t want to face more backlash for taking Tristan back.

Tristan Thompson was photographed spending time with other women while Khloe was pregnant and their relationship came to an abrupt halt in Feb. 2019, after he was reportedly kissing Kylie Jenner’s friend Jordyn Woods. The kiss ruined Kylie’s friendship and Khloe called it quits with Tristan.

When Khloe Kardashian and Tristan Thompson were photographed with their two-year-old daughter True Thompson at Christmas, fans immediately suspected the two had reunited. They are currently quarantining throughout the Coronavirus pandemic and celebrated True Thompson’s second birthday and Easter on April 12, 2020.

Now, a source is speaking to Star magazine in an upcoming article for their May 4, 2020, issue and dished that Khloe may already be pregnant.

The source stated the following.

“Khloe’s giving off hints that she could possibly be expecting. She’s got a certain glow and is really embracing her relationship with Tristan. He’s stepped up to be a partner and a dad, and they’re closer than before. Khloe was never going to stop at one baby and she really wants True to have a sibling close in age.”
