Victor Wembanyama's jaw dropping new hair style: Teammates react to Victor Wembanyama's jaw dropping

Posted by Kelle Repass on Monday, June 10, 2024

Victor Wembanyama's new hair is setting social media on fire.

It seemed like the French rookie had escaped the spotlight. It was a quiet few weeks after the San Antonio Spurs shut down their top draft pick for the end of the NBA 2K24 Summer League. Taking time for introspection and self-gathering seemed fitting after a whirlwind period that included going No. 1 overall in the 2023 NBA Draft, drama with Britney Spears and two memorable summer league appearances.

As training camp approaches next month, Wembanyama has made his return, displaying a completely transformed appearance. In a striking departure from his previous look, Wembanyama has embraced a vibrant, dyed hairstyle. It appears inspired by his teammate Jeremy Sochan, who posted the video of Wemby's new look on Instagram.

The colorful hair sported by Wembanyama and Sochan adding an interesting touch of style to the frontcourt duo, who hope to take the league by storm this year.
