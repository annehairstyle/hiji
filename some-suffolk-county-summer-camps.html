Some Suffolk County Summer Camps

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, June 13, 2024
Here are a list of just some camps that I found that are located in Suffolk County. Contact them or visit their websites for additional information and tuition costs. Please feel free to share this with friends. Also if you know of a camp that is not on the list, please feel free to comment below and share with everyone. 

Not all registration is available at this time, so you will have to contact them and see when registration starts.

CENTEREACH
MAD SCIENCE OF LONG ISLAND – CENTEREACH
PO Box 401, Woodmere, NY 11598 Phone 866-331-2493. www.madscienceli.com  info@madscienceli.com
Ages pre-K – 6th grade. Half-day (9 am – noon) or other (1 – 4 pm); 4 or 5 days in length. Fees: Starting at $180. Up to 12 weekly themes to choose from. Register for up to 10 themes. Daily science take-homes and tons of hands-on science activities per theme. Transportation not provided.

CORAM
TOWN OF BROOKHAVEN SUMMER FUN
1130 Old Town Rd., Coram, NY 11727. (This is the registration address. There are many camp locations.)
631-451-6106/6113. www.brookhaven.org  For children who have completed kindergarten through 6th grade. Half-day (9 am – 12 pm). Fees: Call for rates. Offers arts and crafts, games, gymnasium games and special events. Transportation and meals not available. Note: Mail-in only after school-budget vote in May.

DIX HILLS
PARK SHORE COUNTRY DAY CAMP
450 Deer Park Rd., Dix Hills, NY 11746 (631)-499-8580. www.parkshoredaycamp.com info@parkshoredaycamp.com  Ages 2 – 14. Full-day, 4-, 6-, or 8-weeks; also mid-day for preschoolers. Fees: Call for rates. Three on-site swimming pools with slides. Offers Red Cross swim instruction, sports track program, excellent athletic program, batting cages, electronic scoreboards, athletic conditioning, high ropes course, rock wall, bungee trampoline, inline skate park, arts and crafts, music, dance, special events.

EAST ISLIP
ISLIP TOWN SUMMER PLAYGROUND CAMPS 50 Irish Ln., East Islip, NY 11730 (631)224-5406. www.townofislip-ny.gov  All ages and various programs. Fees: Call for fees. On-site swimming at certain locations. Offers arts and crafts, outdoor games, trips, sports. Registration: As of this month. Note: Various locations available. For Town of Islip residents only. Sessions run for two weeks.

HAUPPAUGE
HIDDEN POND DAY CAMP AT THE RINX
660 Terry Rd., Hauppauge, NY 11788 (63)232-3222, Ext. 201. www.therinx.com  hiddenpondcamp@therinx.com  Ages 4 – 15. Full-day. Fees: Call or visit website for fees. Minimum 3 weeks. On-site swimming (3 heated pools). Ice-skating, tennis, music, dance, high ropes, go carts, gaga ball and more. No transportation; meals included. Open house: Call for dates. Registration: Ongoing. Contact:

SOLUNA STUDIO THEATER CAMP
590 Old Willets Path, Hauppauge, NY 11788 631-708-9681;631-761-6601 (fax). www.solunastudiony.com solunastudioNY@gmail.com  Ages 5 - 14; co-ed. Full-day; half-day. Fee: $599 for five weeks. Offers acting, theater, music, dance and art. No on-site swimming. Soluna Studio is a performing arts studio with two full dance rooms, a rehearsal room and a professional stage. Grades K - 4: "Willy Wonka Junior" from 9:30 am - 12:30 pm. Grades 4 - 9: "High School Musical" from 12:30 - 3:30 pm. Transportation and meals not provided. Registration: Began in June. Contact: Megan Pietroforte.

HOLTSVILLE
HOLTSVILLE – PATCHOGUE Y SUMMER DAY CAMP
155 Buckley Rd., Holtsville, NY 11742 and 255 West Main St., Patchogue 11772 (631)289-4440; 61-891-1800. www.YMCAli.org  adamymca@yahoo.com  Ages 3 – 14. Fees: $835/3 weeks; $875 for Too Cool For Camp and Kiddie Camp. On-site swimming. Offers sports, arts and crafts, general activities. No transportation or meals provided. Open house: Call for dates. Registration: Call for information. Contact: Adam Contreras or Tina Lamendola.

MANORVILLE
CAMP ZOO AT THE LONG ISLAND GAME FARM
Camp Site: Chapman Blvd., Manorville, NY 11949 (631) 878-0202. www.longislandgamefarm.com campzoo@longislandgamefarm.com   Ages 4 – 11, co-ed. 10 am – 3 pm. Fees: Call for fees. Arts and crafts, informative animal tours, nature walks, sing-alongs. No transportation or meals provided. Registration: Begins Mar. 1. Contact: Melinda Novak.

RONKONKOMA
JUMP
151 Dominon Blvd. Ronkonkoma, NY 11779 (631)585-5867. www.jumpparties.com   Ages 6 – 10. 3 hours, 3 days a week. Fee: $75. Jumping, organized activities, arcade and lunch. No transportation. Meal provided. Open house and registration: Call for info. Contact: Kim Holland.

SMITHTOWN
ST PATRICK'S RC CHURCH SUMMER CAMP
280 East Main Street, Smithtown, New York 11787 Phone: (631) 360-0185 www.stpatsyouth.com/id5.html Kiddie Camp for children ages 3-5. Daily activities in- clude circle time, crafts, sports, games, music, as well as workbook time. Kiddie Camp also has field trips for full day campers. Saint Patrick’s Summer Camp’s main camp section is for all children ages 6-12. They are all put into same age groups and go through the day together with their counselors. Our main camp has days that are both on-site and off-site. The Teen Travel portion of our camp is for children ages 13-15. Our Teen Travel group goes on trips everyday including places in New York City, New Jersey, and Pennsylvania. Exclusively available to our Teen Travel group are our overnight trips. No applications will be accepted after March 17, 2013.


STONY BROOK
MY GYM CHILDREN’S FITNESS CENTER
1320 Stony Brook Rd. (just east of Smith Haven Mall) 631-751-KIDS. www.mygym.com  Three-hour summer fit and fun days ages 3 – 9; 90-minute mini-camp for ages 2 – 4. Gymnastics, sport skills, arts and crafts, board game time, yoga and most of all fun. 

THE SUMMER CAMPS AT STONY BROOK UNIVERSITY.  
Stony Brook University, 100 Nicolls Rd., Stony Brook, NY 11794 (631)632-4550; 631-632-4657(fax). www.stonybrook.edu/summercamps  summercamp@stonybrook.edu  Ages kindergarten - 10th grade, co-ed, June 24- Aug. 16. On-site Swimming. Offers a balanced program of recreational and educational activities. An hour of swimming in our indoor 6 lane swimming pool and a hot lunch buffet are included in the price. Camp Day runs from 9 am – 4 pm. Extended hours are available. Parents can enroll their child in as little as 2 weeks of camp, and we offer 3-, 4- or 5-day programs.

THE CAMP ACADEMIES AT STONY BROOK UNIVERSITY
Stony Brook University, 100 Nicolls Rd., Stony Brook, NY 11794
631-632-4550; 631-632-4657(fax). www.stonybrook.edu/summercamps  summercamp@stonybrook.edu
Kids Kindergarten - 11th grade.  The Academies are designed for the camper that has a specific interest in a particular program. Campers will spend half their day in the chosen program and the other half participating in day camp activities (unless otherwise noted). A hot lunch, snack, and one camp shirt are included. Enrollment is limited to 20 campers per academy, all on a first-come, first-served basis. All enrollment is based on the grade the camper is entering in Fall 2013. No early registration or faculty discounts will be given.: Acting; Chess; Chess/Medical; Culinary; Forensics; Game Builders Academies – Starburst; Video Game Design; Programming; Fashion, Robotics, 3-D Modeling and Animation. Medical; Natural Sciences and Band. New for 2012: Academies in Baking, Dance, Photography, Exploration of Space and VR Quest (a virtual reality game building experience). Hot lunch and snack are included.  Days will be announced in February.

ST. JAMES
HARBOR COUNTRY DAY SCHOOL SUMMER CAMP AND WORKSHOPS
17 Three Sisters Rd., St. James, NY 11780.
631-584-5555, 631-862-7664 (fax). www.campharbor.org
Ages 3 – 14, co-ed. Full and half-days; weekly sessions/flexible scheduling. On-site swimming, academic and specialty workshops, choice afternoon, contests and challenges, drama production, music, nature, arts and crafts, sports, archery, C.I.T. program. No transportation. Friday pizza lunch/snacks included. Mon-Thurs. lunch available for additional fee.  Starts June 24th - Aug. 9th

DESTINATION SCIENCE –  www.destinationscience.org info@destinationscience.org 
Ages 5 – 11, co-ed. Full-day (9 am – 3:30 pm), half-day (9 am – noon), extended care (8 – 9 am; 3:30 – 5:30 pm). Fees: Starting at $259 – $359/week. Enroll by Feb. 29 and save $90/week. No on-site swimming. Campers engage in 20 hands-on science activities each week.
