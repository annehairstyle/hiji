Prince Harry allegedly cheated on Meghan Markle: Who is Sarah Ann Macklin?

Posted by Billy Koelling on Tuesday, June 4, 2024

Prince Harry is believed to have had an affair with model Sarah Ann Macklin while dating his current wife, Meghan Markle. Angela Levin, who wrote Prince Harry's biography, revealed that Harry and Macklin began talking after meeting at a private party.

This quick romance took place just after Harry met Meghan, whom he married four years ago in St George's Chapel at Windsor Castle in the UK.

The pair went on dates

"During the very early stages of his relationship with Meghan, Harry was thought to have gone on dates with Burberry model Sarah Ann Macklin," Levin said.

"They met at a private party, he took her number and bombarded her with texts. But it turned out to be just a fling.

"A friend said: 'They go on but are quite different. She is very clean-living and barely drinks, and in that respect they were on a different wavelength. Harry was a bit noncommittal.'

"There has been some confusion too about whether or not Meghan had at the time broken up with her boyfriend of two years, celebrity chef Cory Vitiello."

Who is Sarah Ann Macklin?

While Prince Harry and Meghan have two kids and live in the United States, Sarah Ann Macklin continues with her career as a model and a nutritionist. She is also an influencer, sharing tips for a healthy lifestyle on social media.

Despite some bumps along their relationship, the pair seem as strong as ever, with Meghan admitting recently that Harry saved her by finding her a therapist.

"When I was really bad, my husband helped me by finding a trusted therapist. And I got in touch with this woman. The voicemail I left her showed how bad I was.

"I think it's crucial that we are all very honest about our needs. We shouldn't be afraid to seek outside help if it's needed."
