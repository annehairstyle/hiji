The Gold Country Pro Rodeo

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, June 15, 2024
Gold Country Fairgrounds1273 High StreetAuburn CA 95603
Google Map

Description

From event page:

The beautiful, historic city of Auburn has been hosting a rodeo for over 90 years and the tradition continues this year with 3 action packed performances! The Gold Country Pro Rodeo is co-produced by FlyingU Rodeo Company and EzEvents, Inc. The rodeo is held at the Gold Country Fairgrounds in Auburn, California, in the McCann Stadium. We kick off the Gold Country Pro Rodeo celebration at 6 pm on Wednesday night at Auburn Chrysler Dodge Jeep Ram with a dummy roping contest, FREE food, and fun, followed by this years first ever Friday night rodeo as well as the Official Rodeo Party Saturday night.
The rodeo runs 3 days this year – Friday-Sunday, rain or shine. You won’t want to miss the pro bull riding, bronc riding, Flyin' Cowboys, world class trick roping and MUCH more! Grab your family and friends and lets rodeo!
