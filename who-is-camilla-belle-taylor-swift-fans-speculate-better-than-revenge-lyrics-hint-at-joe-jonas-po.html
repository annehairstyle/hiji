Posted by Kelle Repass on Wednesday, June 5, 2024

LOS ANGELES, CALIFORNIA: Taylor Swift has just released the Taylor's Version of her 2010 studio album, 'Speak Now'. One notable change in the re-recorded version was the absence of a particular sentence in the song 'Better Than Revenge'. In the original version, Swift sang, "She's an actress, but she's better known for the things that she does on the mattress." However, in the re-recorded version, the lyrics have been modified to say, "She's an actress, he was a moth to the flame, she was holding the matches."

'Better Than Revenge' was written by Swift in the aftermath of her breakup with Joe Jonas in 2008. Following their split, Jonas started dating actress Camilla Belle, leading to speculation that the song was inspired by their relationship. With Swift's announcement to re-record her first six albums as a means to regain ownership of her master recordings, fans have been curious to know if she would retain the controversial sentence in the song.

Who is Camilla Belle?

Camilla Belle, 36, is an American actress. She has appeared in various films and television shows throughout her career. Some of her notable film credits include 'The Ballad of Jack and Rose', 'When a Stranger Calls', '10,000 BC' and 'Push'. Belle has also appeared in music videos for artists such as Enrique Iglesias and Maroon 5. Joe Jonas started dating Belle in 2008. Their relationship attracted media attention at the time, and Belle even appeared in the music video for the Jonas Brothers' song 'Lovebug'.
