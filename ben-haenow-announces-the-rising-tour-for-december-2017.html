Ben Haenow announces 'The Rising Tour' for December 2017

Posted by Kelle Repass on Saturday, June 29, 2024

By announcing a brand new tour, Ben Haenow has given his fans another reason to smile. The seasoned performer will be hitting the road again this December!

Ben will be showcasing his incredible talent at nine different venues and his brother Alex will, once again, be joining him on stage as the tour’s accomplished guitarist.

The Rising Tour will kick off in Birmingham on the 5th December and the final show will take place in Bath on the 16th December. For his second tour of the year, Ben will be venturing to various new locations. The ‘Alive’ hitmaker’s show dates are as follows:

5th December – Birmingham – Institute3
6th December – Guildford – The Boileroom
7th December – Oxford – The Bullingdon
9th December – Liverpool – Arts Club
10th December – Edinburgh – The Voodoo Rooms
12th December – Leeds – Brudenell
13th December – London – Borderline
15th December – Dover – The Booking Hall
16th December – Bath – Moles Club

Fans can expect to hear a live rendition of Ben’s latest single ‘Alive‘, new material from his forthcoming EP and a selection of tunes from his debut album. Due to the time of year, the Croydon-born singer may also throw in a cheeky Christmas classic.

General sale for The Rising Tour will begin at 9am on Monday 31st July. Standard and VIP tickets will also be available via a limited presale tomorrow morning. For a chance to grab tickets early, make sure you sign up to The MJR Group’s newsletter here online.

The lovable singer’s fans are clearly excited about his new tour and we can understand why. With phenomenal vocals and an electrifying stage presence, Ben always puts on an unforgettable string of shows. You seriously won’t want to miss out.
