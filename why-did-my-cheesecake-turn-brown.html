Why did my cheesecake turn brown?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, June 10, 2024

Cheesecakes that are overcooked, even slightly, can result in the top edges of the cake turning brown. In order to make cheesecake without browning the top, you will need to bake it one day ahead of time in order to allow it time to reach the perfect consistency.

How do you fix a ruined cheesecake?

How to Fix A Cracked Cheesecake

  • Chill your cheesecake. Fixing cracks works better when your cheesecake is cold.
  • Press the cracks closed as much as possible using clean fingers.
  • Dip an offset spatula in hot water so it gets nice and warm.
  • Repeat step 3, wiping the spatula between in time, until your crack is gone.
