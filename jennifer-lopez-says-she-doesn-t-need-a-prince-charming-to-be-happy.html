Jennifer Lopez says she doesn't need a Prince Charming to be happy!

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, June 2, 2024

Jennifer Lopez is one of the most powerful, beautiful and successful women in the entertainment industry….and whether she’s single or not, she’s perfectly content with her life as it is!

Despite admitting to rekindling her relationship with dancer Casper Smart, J Lo has said that after three divorces, marriage is no longer a priority in her life, and that she has learnt to find happiness in her single life.

In an inspirational and empowering interview with InStyle magazine, on which she adorns the cover this month, J Lo said:

“I need to be happy on my own. If someone fits into that, great. But if they don’t, that’s OK too.”

The mother of two then went on to explain why it took her such a long time to come to the realisation that she doesn’t need a man or a marriage to bring her happiness, explaining that ‘As girls, I think we are taught from a very young age that someone else is going to bring us our happiness”

“I was waiting for Prince Charming. Everybody I met was going to be the guy I was going to spend my life with”

“And then you realise, I’m strong .I’m my own keeper, my own saviour.”

“That’s been the journey for me.” 

With such a busy 2016 ahead of her, we’re surprised J Lo could even have time to fit a man in to her life right now, and we certainly see why with everything else she’s got going on we can see why she’s loving her life right now!

In January of this year alone, the singer and actress, 46, stars in NBC’s new TV show Shades of Blue, the final season of American Idol (On which she sits between country star Keith Urban and Harry Connick Jr as a judge), and kicking off her very own residency show in Las Vegas at Planet Hollywood! Combine that with her plans to produce and star in comedy film Mothers I’d like to… and her hopes of recording both a Christmas album and a second Spanish Language album at some point, the star is set for a very, very busy year!

“They come with me everywhere” said JLo, when asked how she balances such a full on career with motherhood. “I am busy, and they know it. And they know part of their job is helping me get sleep. If I’m sleeping in, they’re very quiet, like, ‘Mommy needs to sleep! She worked late last night!’ We’re a team.”
