Sigrid strips back 'Don't Feel Like Crying' for Vevo LIFT

Posted by Billy Koelling on Tuesday, June 11, 2024

Vevo LIFT’s latest addition, Sigrid, breaks down latest single ‘Don’t Feel Like Crying’.

Earlier this month CelebMix brought you news of Sigrid’s latest track ahead of the rising star’s debut album, Sucker Punch, on March 8th. Vevo LIFT shines a spotlight on some of the industry’s hottest rising talent, with artists such as Jorja Smith, Anne-Marie, Billie Eilish and Khalid all previously featuring.

Sigrid’s triumphant new single is co-written by Emily Warren and takes on a new life in the stripped back environment, where Sigrid thrives. Without doubt one of the best live vocalists in the game right now, the singer boils the track down to just herself, a piano and a backing singer.

The ‘Strangers’-singer also spoke to Vevo LIFT about her family on the road, songwriting in bed… and owning cats when she is older.
