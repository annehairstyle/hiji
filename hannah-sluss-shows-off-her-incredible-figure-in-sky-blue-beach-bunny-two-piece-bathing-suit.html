Hannah Sluss Shows Off Her Incredible Figure In Sky Blue Beach Bunny Two-Piece Bathing Suit

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, June 25, 2024

Hannah Sluss has been putting her fabulous figure on display in multiple photos that she’s shared on her official Instagram account where she has 1.4 million followers. Wearing a sky blue, Beach Bunny bikini, Hannah looked gorgeous as she posed in the swimwear. The light blue color brought out Hannah’s eyes and was the perfect choice to complement her natural beauty. She wore her hair swept to the side and it framed her face in soft, beachy waves. With plenty of chunky, blonde streaks, and a golden glow to her skin, Hannah was ready for the beach, a pool party, or a trip on a yacht.

The top and bottoms are sold separately and the top is called the Lexi Bralette and retails for approximately $130. The top features two, gold-tone ring front accents in a figure-eight shape.  The top would make a great solo piece as well as a bikini top and could easily be paired with an oversized shirt, under a blouse, or even alone with a blazer. The bikini is made of polyamide and elastane and should be hand washed for easy laundering.

The bikini bottoms are called the Nadia Skimpy Bikini Bottoms and also feature the gold-tone, figure-eight accents on the hips. The bathing suit is available in aqua and black and the bottoms cost approximately $130.

Hannah posed outside for the sunny photo and she geotagged the picture with Malibu, California. In addition to her gorgeous bathing suit, fans also commented on her necklace and many wanted to know who it was by.

In the photos that you may see below, Hannah is wearing a herringbone choker from Adina’s Jewels. The necklace is gold vermeil and costs approximately $68.
