Darby Allin Nearly Injures Himself Again After Dangerous Stunt

Posted by Billy Koelling on Thursday, June 27, 2024

Darby Allin is currently one of the top stars in AEW, who has made a name for himself as a thrill seeker and extreme daredevil. That is not a gimmick as he is exactly like that in real life. However, Allin almost injured himself again after performing a dangerous stunt on a tricycle.

Darby Allin also loves skateboarding and doing a lot of death-defying activities that truly make him feel alive. The former TNT Champion is an enigma and embraces that in every way.

Darby Allin injured himself at AEW WrestleDream after his match as he was spotted wearing an arm sling. Recent footage showed Darby Allin performing death-defying stunt on a tricycle during the recent Nitro Circus event, as he ended up botching the stunt and landing directly on his shoulder – nearly injuring himself again in the process.

‘‘I’m not doing too good, my shoulder I think is fractured. Two weeks ago I messed up my elbow pretty bad, last week I had to go train to climb Mt. Everest, so I haven’t really had time to give my shoulder any rest. When I heard Nitro Circus was coming through Texas, I had to come.”

Darby Allin also plans on Climbing Mt. Everest next year, so all of these dangerous stunts are not doing him any favors whatsoever. Regardless, we do hope Allin eventually stops doing such risky stunts for his own good.
