Biography

Dante Bowe (born May 10, 1993) is a renowned songwriter, performer, and recording artist who has succeeded dramatically. With a Grammy Award and multiple platinum-selling records, he has established himself as a prominent figure in the music industry. 

Notably, Dante Bowe was associated with Bethel Music Collective and Maverick City Music, showcasing his versatility and talent across different musical genres. Additionally, he has taken on the CEO role at TRUE Music, an independent music management company, further demonstrating his entrepreneurial skills and commitment to the industry.

Early Life & Education

Dante Bowe, a talented musician, was born on May 10, 1993, in the beautiful town of Rockingham, North Carolina. Growing up in a close-knit Christian community, he was fortunate to be surrounded by the guidance and wisdom of his Christian minister’s grandparents. 

It was within this nurturing environment that Bowe‘s passion for Music began to take root. He owes much of his early musical influences to his parents, who significantly fostered his love for singing. During a seventh-grade talent show, Bowe discovered his true calling and realized the immense joy and fulfillment of showcasing his vocal talents on stage. 

Dante Bowe Bowe boldly decided to forgo attending college and instead focus on his passion for music ministry. At the tender age of 16, he made the courageous choice to drop out of high school to pursue his dreams. His dedication and talent led him to become a prominent worship leader at Eddie James Ministries, where he undoubtedly left an indelible mark with his soul-stirring performances. 

Recognizing his immense potential, Dante Bowe was eventually welcomed into the esteemed Bethel Music Collective, where he continues to captivate audiences with unparalleled musicality and spiritual devotion.

Career

Dante Bowe began his musical journey in 2017 when he took his first steps into the world of singing. He debuted in the music industry with his first single, Potter and Friend, showcasing his distinct vocal abilities. Dante made a memorable debut in the industry by collaborating with Jesse Cline. 

Not satisfied with a single release, he self-released his debut album, Son of a Father, on March 31 of that year. This album was a testament to Dante‘s talent and passion for Music, solidifying his presence in the industry and leaving fans eager to see what he would bring to the table next.

In 2021, an artist named Dante Bowe released his second studio album, Circles. This album featured several collaborations, including songs with Jesse Cline, Amanda Cook, Steffany Gretzinger, and Chandler Moore. One of the album’s standout tracks was the hit single Joyful, which became a huge success.
