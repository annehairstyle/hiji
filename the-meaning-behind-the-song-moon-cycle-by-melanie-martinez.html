The song’s meaning is open to interpretation and can resonate with listeners in various ways. At its core, MOON CYCLE represents the cyclical nature of relationships and the constant ups and downs we experience. It depicts the emotional rollercoaster we embark on when we fall in love and the inevitable pain and renewal that follows.

Martinez’s lyrics draw upon vivid imagery and metaphors to convey the volatile nature of relationships. She compares the shifting dynamics of love to the cycles of the moon, which wax and wane. The moon becomes a symbol of change and unpredictability, mirroring the tumultuous nature of human connection.

The lyrics of MOON CYCLE also touch upon themes of vulnerability, betrayal, and the complexities of one’s own emotions. Martinez’s hauntingly beautiful voice adds a layer of depth to the song, allowing listeners to fully immerse themselves in the emotional journey it represents.
