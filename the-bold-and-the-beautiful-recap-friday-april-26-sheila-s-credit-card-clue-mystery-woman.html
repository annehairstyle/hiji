The Bold and the Beautiful Recap: Friday, April 26 Sheilas Credit Card Clue Mystery Woman

Posted by Billy Koelling on Monday, June 17, 2024

The Bold and the Beautiful (B&B) spoilers recap for Friday, April 26, reveals that Steffy Forrester (Jacqueline MacInnes Wood) noticed John “Finn” Finnegan (Tanner Novlan) was distracted, but she assumed it was about a patient and admired his dedication.

After Finn got another text from Deacon Sharpe (Sean Kanan) about meeting up again, he joined him at his place.

Finn admitted Deacon got him thinking despite how farfetched and improbable the “Sugar” aka Janet Webber (Kimberlin Brown) story sounded.

Deacon insisted he was right and that they were running out of time to find Sheila Carter (Kimberlin Brown).

Since Deacon was able to get his hands on Sheila’s credit card password, he used it to investigate Sheila’s purchases leading up to that night.

Once Finn and Deacon looked at the charges together, they found that Sheila visited a convenience store in a rough neighborhood.

Deacon and Finn immediately went there and found an employee named Michele (Juliet Vega) on her break outside.

After Deacon confirmed they weren’t cops and showed off a picture of Sheila, Michele insisted she’d never seen her before and went back to work.

Nearby, a homeless man named Tom (Clint Howard) piped up about seeing everyone who came in and out of that place.

Deacon gave Tom some cash and showed him Sheila’s pic, so Tom admitted he never forgot a face – especially one as pretty as hers.

Tom pointed out another building and offered updates on Sheila walking into it. That’s when Tom heard screaming, but he chose to mind his own business instead of investigating.

Finn said maybe there was something in that building that could lead them to Sheila, but he also raised the possibility that Sheila had been in that building all along. Deacon just hoped they weren’t too late!

Inside that rundown building, B&B offered a glimpse of a woman on a mattress and under some covers, but only her hand could be seen.

At Forrester Creations, Ridge Forrester (Thorsten Kaye) had a meeting with Carter Walton (Lawrence Saint-Victor) and hyped up what a great team Zende Forrester Dominguez (Delon de Metz) and RJ Forrester (Joshua Hoffman) were going to be at Hope for the Future.

Ridge noticed Carter’s skepticism and questioned it, but Carter didn’t let any news slip about what happened between Zende and Luna Nozawa (Lisa Yamada).

Afterward, Ridge talked to Steffy about how messed up Deacon’s claims about Sheila were. Steffy refused to let Finn get caught up in whatever was going on in Deacon’s twisted mind.

In the design office on Friday’s B&B episode, Brooke Logan (Katherine Kelly Lang) found Luna and asked if she was OK since she heard what happened.

After Brooke hugged Luna, she let her know that woman-to-woman, this was a safe place.

Brooke said she was taken advantage of a long time ago, but Luna assured her that Zende didn’t do that to her.

Although Brooke knew that, she still had a problem with Zende sleeping with Luna when she was in a serious relationship with RJ.

Brooke pointed out that RJ swore her to secrecy and listened as Luna recapped what went down that night. The last thing Luna wanted to do was cause pain to RJ and his family, especially since they’d all been so welcoming.

Brooke knew who Luna was as a person and assured her that this wasn’t her fault. After Brooke insisted she’d always be there for her, Luna offered her gratitude.

The Bold and the Beautiful spoilers say RJ and Luna will have to figure out what’s next, so we’ll bring you more predictions on what the future holds.
