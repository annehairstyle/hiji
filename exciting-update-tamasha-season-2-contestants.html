Introducing Tamasha Season 2

“Tamasha” Season 2 brings back the riveting Urdu television reality show inspired by the Bigg Boss franchise, airing on ARY Digital in Pakistan. Following the triumph of its inaugural season, “Tamasha” returns with an intriguing concept drawn from the original Dutch format of “Big Brother,” pioneered by John de Mol.

The show delves into the lives of a group of contestants known as “housemates,” who reside in a specially built house and are isolated from the outside world. Each week, they partake in the ritual of nominating fellow contestants for eviction, followed by public voting.

Viewers can expect a spectacle of drama, suspense, and surprising turns as contestants engage in strategic maneuvers and face challenges on their journey to claim victory.

Tamasha Season 2 Where To Watch

“Tamasha” Season 2, the popular Urdu television reality show inspired by the Big Brother franchise, is ready to enthrall viewers once again. Hosted by Adnan Siddiqui, the show follows the format of the Dutch reality game show, Big Brother, where contestants, called “housemates,” coexist in isolation, engaging in various challenges and social interactions.

The show involves weekly nominations and public voting, leading to the eviction of housemates. Season 2, airing from August 5, 2023, onwards, promises another exciting journey of drama, companionship, and competition. To catch the compelling episodes and witness the housemates navigate through their challenges, viewers can tune in to ARY Digital, the original network for “Tamasha” Season 2.
