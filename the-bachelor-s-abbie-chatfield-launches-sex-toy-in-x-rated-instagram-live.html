The Bachelors Abbie Chatfield launches sex toy in X-rated Instagram Live

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, June 23, 2024

Abbie Chatfield is known (and loved) for being vocal about taboo topics.

Now the former Bachelor star and current queen of the I’m A Celeb jungle is tackling another subject many people get uncomfortable discussing – orgasms, specifically the female kind.

The 25-year-old announced she’s partnered with adult brand Vush to produce her very own sex toy – a bright orange gadget that Abbie said she made “suuuuuper strong” to compensate for the amount of times she’s returned home from a date in need of “self care”.

But while Abbie was discussing the racy partnership in an Instagram Live on Tuesday, fans found themselves distracted by something dangling from her ears.

Dressed in all orange to match the $150 bedroom device, Abbie went one step further – wearing the X-rated item in her earlobes.

Now, let’s be clear – she didn’t have two massive sex toys dangling from her ears, but a mini version made into adorable earrings.

It didn’t take long for the cheeky item to cause a fuss with the star’s 255,000 followers, with many asking: “Where can I get the earrings?”

“Women empowering other women I’m all for it! PS love the mini vibrator earrings,” one person said.

“YES VIBRATOR EARRINGS,” another added.

While one person wrote: “The fact that her earrings are mini vibrators is giving me life.”

In the comments, Abbie replied telling fans the earrings would being released alongside her bedroom gadget on Sunday, April 11.

If you’re hoping they vibrate like the item they’ve been modelled on, sadly Abbie revealed “they do not”.

Since Abbie rose to fame on the 2019 season of The Bachelor she has become a prominent voice on topics, including sexuality, women’s rights and body image.
