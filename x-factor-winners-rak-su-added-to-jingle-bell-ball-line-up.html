X Factor Winners Rak-Su Added to Jingle Bell Ball Line-Up

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, June 19, 2024

The Capital FM Jingle Bell Ball is one of the most exciting music events of the year. Held annually at the O2 Arena in London the hit radio station regularly welcomes household names such as Cheryl, Will.I.Am, Shawn Mendes, and DNCE to the stage and tickets sell out in seconds.

This year’s line-up is one of the most exciting in recent years with Ed Sheeran and Taylor Swift both taking to the stage along with mega names such as Sam Smith, Niall Horan, The Script, Liam  Payne and many, many more.

And now it has been confirmed that the 2017 X Factor winners Rak-Su will be performing on night one of the Capital Jingle Bell Ball on Saturday, December 9th. They will be performing their massive winners single ‘Dimelo‘ and will no doubt perform some more fan favourites on the night.

The exciting news was announced by Capital FM this morning;

THIS. IS. BIG! @RakSuMusic are joining us for the #CapitalJBB this year. Who's ready to sing 'Dimelo'?! ??? https://t.co/Cr8RjkMKAW pic.twitter.com/xdtr5QORPD

— Capital (@CapitalOfficial) December 5, 2017

The full 2017 Capital FM Jingle Bell Ball line-up can be found on the hit music station’s website! The concert will take place over two nights on Saturday and Sunday, December 9th and 10th.

Make sure you follow Rak-Su on Twitter @RakSuMusic to keep up to date with all their latest music and tour news!
