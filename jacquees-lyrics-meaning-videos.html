Overall Meaning

The song "Trip" by Jacquees is about a girl who is playing games with him, making him feel confused and uncertain about their relationship. He feels like he has a lot of love to offer, but doesn't want to waste it on someone who isn't fully committed to him. He also acknowledges that he's been drinking to cope with the situation, but he's ready to work through it and be with her.


The chorus repeats the phrase "I think that I'm done trippin', I'm trip-trippin'" which could be interpreted in a few different ways. It could mean that he's done being confused and is ready to make a decision about their relationship, or it could mean that he's finally sobering up and seeing things more clearly. The line "Got a whole lot of love, ain't tryna waste it" emphasizes that he has a lot to offer and wants to make sure that it's not being taken for granted.


The rap section towards the end of the song addresses some of the specific ways that she's been playing games with him, such as telling him they're just friends one minute and then acting like they're more than that the next. Despite this, he's willing to put in the work to make it work, and he acknowledges that his feelings for her are too strong to just walk away.
