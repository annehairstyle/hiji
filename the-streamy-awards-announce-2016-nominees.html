The Streamy Awards Announce 2016 Nominees

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, June 16, 2024

The Streamy Awards honors the best content that the internet has to offer. YouTube personality, GloZell Green, will host the sixth annual awards show, while also being a nominee herself.

Tubefilter and Dick Clark Productions announced this year’s round of nominees. There are 42 categories. In addition, the nominees for the two fan-voted awards – Entertainer of the Year and Show of the Year – will be announced later on. Fans can vote for their favorites in these two categories until the Streamy Awards premiere. The rest of the awards will be chosen by a group of experts in the online video industry.

The Streamy Awards will take place on Tuesday, October 4th.
