Who is Emme Maribel Muñiz?

American Emme Maribel Muñiz is a singer. Maribel is renowned as the daughter of the singing sensation, Jennifer Lopez.

Recently, she shared the stage with her mother and the Colombian singer, Shakira in Super Bowl LIV Halftime Show.

Emme Maribel Muñiz- Birth Age, Family

Emme Maribel Muñiz was born on the 22nd of February 2008 in Long Island, NY. Her mother, Jennifer Lopez is a Billboard Icon Award winner singer.

She is of Puerto Rican ethnicity. Her father, Marc Anthony is also a singer by profession.

She shares the same birthday with Maximillian David Muñiz. She also has three half-siblings, Ryan Adrian Muñiz, Cristian Marcus Muñiz, and Ariana Anthony.

Family History

Her parents were married in 2004. However, they ended their 10 years of marriage with a divorce in 2014. But the separation between them was already announced in July 2011.

At the time, she was only 3 years old. The divorce was on mutual understanding and trust.

Before that, Marc Anthony was married to the pop singer, Dayanara Torres in 2000. Later, Mac was married to the Venezuelan model, Shannon De Lima.

Before Marc, Jennifer was married to the American actor, Cris Judd in 2001. Currently, Jennifer is engaged to Alex Rodriguez. Soon, they are planning their wedding in the summer of 2020.
