Toya Wright, Rasheeda Frost, Kandi Burruss And More Are Mourning After The Death Of Nipsey Hussle

Posted by Billy Koelling on Tuesday, June 11, 2024

It seems like time stands still today after the tragedy that managed to shake the community to the core. Nipsey Hussle has been killed, and people just don’t understand the reasons because the man was so loved by everyone.

Various celebrities have honored him by posting pictures, messages, and prayers for the family and friends on social media.

No one seems to care about anything today, other than this and how/why could such a thing happen.

Tiny Harris and T.I. recently posted their thoughts and Toya Wright, Rasheeda Forst and Kandi Burruss are also heartbroken.

Rasheeda wrote ‘This is so damnnnn sad & heartbreaking mannnnnn…. sending prayers to @nipseyhussle entire family & friends may he #RIP. 🙏🏽🙏🏽.’
