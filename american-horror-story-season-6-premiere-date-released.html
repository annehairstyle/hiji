American Horror Story Season 6 Premiere Date Released!

Posted by Artie Phelan on Thursday, June 27, 2024

After a long wait and many rumours, FX have finally released the premiere date for Season 6 of American Horror Story! Mark your calendars everybody as Wednesday, September 14th will be the start of a brand new horror story. Let the haunting begin!

Much is still unknown about the upcoming season of American Horror Story, however, the new logo has recently been released. No plot details have been released but rumours have been floating through social media – Anyone up for a space or apocalypse themed AHS? Anyone up for a reunion with the demonic baby from Season 1? Those are just some of the rumours but an apocalypse theme has been a really big hit! We can’t wait to find out what they’ve created this time round.

It has been confirmed that Lady Gaga will be returning for Season 6 which is great news, as she did an outstanding job in Season 5!

There’s not much more to say at this moment in time… All we can say is mark September 14th in your diaries right now! We’ll keep all you AHS fans updated when an official theme has been announced and once again, we look forward to this new era.
