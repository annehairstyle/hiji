Flyana Boss is Here With The Ultimate Summer Soundtrack

Posted by Artie Phelan on Friday, June 28, 2024

Flyana Boss is back again with a double shot of serotonin on latest EP Make It A Double. It seems the stars have aligned for this best friend duo as this new release follows a streak of viral attention on TikTok. This two track collection will give you that extra pep in your step with it-girl lyricism and sugar-coated sonics.

Make It A Double pulls together a robust range of beats, vocals that bounce off each other, and unexpected lyricism for a joyride with new surprises at every turn. The opening track, “You Wish”, is an intense hitter about finding your strength as a female goddess and keeping your sense of humor intact on the way. Flyana Boss sings, “I know what you want, so get in my belly.” The closing track, “Trashboi” is a slower, vocally sensational single that reminds women of their worth. Make It A Double is ready to set your summer in motion with two fresh bangers set to pump your girl gang up.

Flyana Boss is a duo made up of besties Bobbi Lanea and Folayan Kunerede. The two multi-instrumentalists met in college and have since evolved into Flyana Boss as we know them today. Make It A Double is their latest release for the summer.
