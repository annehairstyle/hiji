Stars of social media Nick began his YouTube channel with the help of his pal Pouya. They routinely posted videos of themselves performing music on YouTube. Personal Life of Fat Nick

Fat Nick was born in Miami, Florida, on September 6, 1994. Nicholas Minucci is his real name. He grew up in Miami, Florida, and has always been interested in music. He stated in an interview that he aspired to be a model in the future. Nick has a sister, whose name has yet to be revealed. Maria Minucci is his mother’s name, and his father abandoned them when he was a child. When it comes to Nick’s early years, he had a difficult upbringing. He had a lot of troubles growing up and ended up dropping out of high school. There was a lot going on in his life, and none of it was good. The 26-year-old actor did everything he could to stay alive. Nick turned to drugs to address his financial issues and began selling them to make ends meet. While he was doing this and earning some money, he understood that what he was doing was dangerous, so he eventually quit selling drugs.
