Gigi Hadid And Zayn Malik Reportedly Spending Time Apart

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, June 27, 2024

Are Zayn Malik and Gigi Hadid over for good this time? Due to the fact that the pair hasn’t been photographed together in quite some time, fans have started to assume that they were no longer together and it looks like they might’ve been right.

One insider tells E! News that the two have been spending time apart ever since the beginning of November.

Apparently, both Gigi and Zayn agree that is the best thing for their relationship at the moment.

Furthermore, they have ‘both been really focused on themselves and their separate careers,’ and that naturally caused them to drift apart.

Zayn has been in the studio working on his new music while the model has apparently been focusing on herself a lot.

Hadid’s schedule had supposedly ‘slowed down after the Victoria’s Secret Fashion Show.’

That is also the time when the rumors that they had split again started going around since Zayn was not in attendance at the event.

But a source told E! News at the time that him skipping it had nothing to do with their romance, claiming that ‘Everything is going smoothly, they are just trying to keep it more under the radar. It is no surprise that Zayn did not attend the VS show. He isn’t one for public outings, and usually skips out on going.’

The same insider also told the news outlet that they were keeping their relationship ‘more private.’

The singer does not even follow the supermodel on Instagram anymore, which fans have taken as another sign that they are no longer dating.
