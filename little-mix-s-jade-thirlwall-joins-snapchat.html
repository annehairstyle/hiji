Little Mix's Jade Thirlwall joins Snapchat

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, June 30, 2024

What’s that you ask? Has Jade Thirlwall finally blessed us all by creating a personal Snapchat account?

The simple answer to that question is ‘yes!’

Thirlwall becomes the third member of the chart topping girl band to create a personal account on the social media platform. You can view her snaps by adding the username ‘justjadeamelia.’

This comes just days after Leigh-Anne Pinnock also made her account public, her username being ‘leigh-annepinn.’

Perrie Edwards was the first of the band to go solo on Snapchat several months ago while Jesy Nelson is yet to make a personal account for her fans to follow.

The record breaking girl group are currently embarking on the MASSIVE ‘Get Weird Tour’ and they never fail to post hilarious daily snaps onto their social media accounts. We almost feel as if we’re on tour with them!

You can add the members of Little Mix on Snapchat using the following usernames:

  • Band account – littlemix_offic
  • Perrie Edwards – perriesnap
  • Leigh-Anne Pinnock – leigh-annepinn
  • Jade Thirlwall – justjadeamelia

Now all we have to do is patiently wait for Jesy Nelson to hop on the personal Snapchat bandwagon. Girl, we’re begging you!

Just while we’re on the topic, be sure to add ‘CelebMix’ on Snapchat for concert videos, celeb news and much more!
