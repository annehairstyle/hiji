Teen Mom Alum Jenelle Evans Files Restraining Order Against Her Mom

Posted by Kelle Repass on Thursday, June 6, 2024

Teen Mom alum Jenelle Evans Eason is on the outs with her mom, Barbara Evans again and she filed a restraining order against her.

It apparently relates to something her mother said to TMZ about her son, Jace. So, what is going on with these two women now? They have a long history of fighting with each other. Read on to find out.

Teen Mom: Jenelle Seems Angry With Barbara After Jace Ran Away

In August, Jace ran away from home. He’d been seen at school but never made it home. So, the cops got involved. MTV fans noticed that it coincided with Jenelle and David fighting it out again on social media.

Naturally, MTV fans assumed that Jace had tried to get away from an uncomfortable situation at home.

Bear in mind, he only recently went to live with his birth mother after Barbara gave up custody of him,

The Teen Mom alum slammed people who suggested that she and David had caused Jace to run away because of tension in the house. In fact, she denied they ever fought in front of the kids.

Asking for privacy, she claimed that Jace had just had his phone taken away after he misbehaved.

However, just days later, he went missing again. Fortunately, he was found safe and returned home. How does that tie into the current friction with her mom?

Teen Mom: Restraining Order Filed By Jenelle Evans

When the mom of three kids fled her restraining order, it came after Barbara had accused her daughter of taking her son off his meds for “ADHD.” TMZ cited her as saying that she and Jenelle are not taking but still, she suspects that the alleged lack of medication might be causing behavioral problems.

The outlet also noted that there is a lot of tension between the two women.

This week, The Ashley’s Reality Roundup reported that a restraining order was filed by Jenelle against her mom.

Apparently, she went for “a domestic violence protective order” against the older former Teen Mom star.

 

However, she later reapplied for a “civil restraining order,” citing the comments that her mom made to TMZ. So, it seems that the judge responded negatively to her original domestic order.

The Two MTV Alums Often Clashed

Teen Mom fans are well aware that the two women clashed multiple times. Barbara raised Jace, but he apparently started acting out and in her later years of life, Barbs couldn’t deal with it. Reportedly, Barbara will be attending court “next week.”
