The Tide, Sigma and DJ Fresh announced for Fusion Festival

Posted by Billy Koelling on Tuesday, June 18, 2024

The Tide, Sigma and DJ Fresh are among the new acts which were announced for Fusion Festival today.

Sigma, who’s latest track is ‘Cry’ featuring Take That, will be performing on the Saturday. They will be joined by Blonde, Anne Marie and Imani who were also added today. They will be performing alongside Jason Derulo, Tinie Tempah, Labrinth, Craig David, Sigala, Fleur East, and Krept and Konan.

Over on the Sunday, The Tide will be taking a day off from their ‘Click My Fingers’ tour to perform at Fusion Festival. Also added to the line up today were DJ Fresh, Becky Hill, and Club Drive. Joining them are the previously announced Olly Murs, Busted, The Vamps, Ella Henderson, Nathan Sykes, Jamie Lawson and Lawson.

This year, there’ll also be a second stage sponsored by Koppaberg. The ‘Urban Forest’ stage will see a variety of artists perform over the two days, including home grown Liverpool talent Mic Lowry and Louis Berry.

Fusion Festival takes place on the 3rd and 4th of September in Otterspool Prom, Liverpool. Tickets for the event are still available here.
