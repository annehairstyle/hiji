5 After Midnight Release Music Video For Debut Single 'Up In Here'

Posted by Kelle Repass on Thursday, June 20, 2024

5 After Midnight – made up of members Kieran Alleyne, Jordan Lee and Nathan Lewis – have released the music video for their debut single ‘Up In Here.’

The official video for #UpInHere is out RIGHT NOW on @Vevo_UK! Break those replay buttons guys ? #UpInHereMusicVideo https://t.co/BMi8TAu34L

— 5AM (@5AfterMidnight) June 22, 2017

Right from the beginning of the video, there is an upbeat, positive and fun theme running throughout. There is also the opportunity for the band to show off some of their incredible dance skills – and we love it! The video portrays people having a good time, which is always great to see in a music video.

In the bridge of the song, this segment is remixed and it fits extremely well into the video, also allowing for an incredible dance break from the trio.

You can watch the video for ‘Up In Here’ below:

5 After Midnight are best known for finishing in third place on ‘The X Factor’ in 2016, impressing the Judges and viewers with their incredible vocals and phenomenal dance moves week after week. With performances including ‘Crazy In Love’ and ‘Can’t Stop The Feeling,’ they repeatedly proved why they deserved a place in the Live Shows.

Following their time on the show, the band were part of ‘The X Factor Live Tour,’ performing in arenas across the UK and Ireland. Most recently, the band debuted their single on ‘Britain’s Got Talent’ and featured as part of the Capital FM SummerTime Ball line-up!

Now we’ve had a preview of what they’ve been working on, we now can’t wait for the debut 5 After Midnight album – which will no doubt be incredible!
