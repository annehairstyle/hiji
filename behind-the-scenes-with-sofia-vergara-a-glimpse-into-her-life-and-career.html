Behind the Scenes with Sofia Vergara: A Glimpse into Her Life and Career

Posted by Artie Phelan on Thursday, June 20, 2024

Sofia Vergara is one of the most popular and successful actresses in Hollywood. From her iconic role as Gloria Delgado-Pritchett on Modern Family to her many film appearances, she has established herself as a force to be reckoned with in the entertainment industry. But what goes on behind-the-scenes of this star’s life? 

In this article, we take a look at some of Sofia Vergara’s lesser-known facts about her career and personal life. We will also take a look at her campaign with SciPlay. Get ready for an inside scoop into the world of Sofia Vergara! 

About Sofia in a Nutshell

Sofia Vergara began her career in the entertainment world as a model, while strolling along the sandy shores of Colombia, a photographer spotted her and immediately showered her with opportunities to model and star in television programs.

Appearing on the covers of numerous magazines, she then moved into television, making appearances on shows like Baywatch and As The World Turns. In 2009, Vergara landed her breakthrough role as Gloria Delgado-Pritchett on Modern Family. She also took part in many movies like The Smurfs, New Year’s Eve, Hot Pursuit, and more. 

Aside from being a successful actress, Vergara is also a businesswoman and entrepreneur. She has her own production company, and she has launched successful lines of clothing, jewelry, beauty products, and furniture. 

Personal Life

Outside of her work in the entertainment world, Vergara is also a devoted wife and mother. She married Joe Manganiello in 2015, after nearly two years together. The couple welcomed their first child together in 2016, a son named Manolo. In addition to Manolo, Vergara has a son from her first marriage, Manolo’s half-brother. After the birth of Manolo, Vergara said she “felt complete” and dedicated her life to her children.
