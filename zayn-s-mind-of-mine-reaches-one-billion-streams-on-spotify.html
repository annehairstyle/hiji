Zayn's &quot;Mind of Mine&quot; reaches one billion streams on Spotify

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, January 1, 0001

Zayn Malik has achieved a major feat this week. The “Still Got Time” hitmaker has experienced a monumental personal record on the streaming service Spotify.

The singer’s album “Mind of Mine” has reached a billion streams on Spotify

A fan account Zayn Daily News tweeted the news out. It was then retweeted by the singer himself.

Mind of Mine has reached one billion streams on @Spotify! Congratulations @zaynmalik ? #MOM1Billion

? https://t.co/WWBhhlEsy1 pic.twitter.com/XKxvqcgOd7

— Zayn Daily News (@zayndailynews) June 8, 2017

Zayn’s debut album was released last year and treated fans with some really good tracks including “Pillowtalk” that stayed on top of the charts for weeks.

Malik was recently nominated as Top New Artist at BBMAs. The singer eventually went on to win the award. He tweeted that he is making some new music and couldn’t be there in person, but that he’d be back very soon.

Can't make it to the #BBMAs tonight and know I've been away for a bit .. Finishing up these new songs for you. Be back soon ?

— zayn (@zaynmalik) May 21, 2017

He has also announced the second collection of his Zayn x Versus Versace line. It seems the singer is quite busy. It’s definitely worth the wait to see what he has got in store for his fans next. We can’t wait to hear his second album and see what he took with him from his first and what he changed to make the second one it’s own.
