The Beyonce: Renaissance World Tour film will be released on December 1st

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, June 17, 2024

Beyonce’s Renaissance Tour was a global spectacle which dominated headlines and social media buzz for months. The Beyhive was flying all around the world to catch the concerts and between this tour and Taylor Swift’s Eras tour, we likely saw the two largest-grossing tours by female artists of all time. Taylor put together an Eras Tour concert film which will probably gross eleventy billion dollars too. Now it looks like Beyonce directed herself in a Renaissance tour film!!

Capitalizing on the anticipated big screen fever of Taylor Swift: The Eras Tour, we are hearing heavy buzz this morning that AMC’s next big concert film is Beyonce: Renaissance World Tour, which has been another hard-to-get-live event ticket this past year in addition to Swift. There’s been reports that Beyonce shot Renaissance World Tour in her hometown of Houston, TX back in late September for two days.

Deadline has learned exclusively that the theatrical release date for Beyonce Renaissance World Tour is looking like Dec. 1, the post Thanksgiving/Black Friday weekend frame which is typically a dead zone at the box office. AMC didn’t respond to request for comment. Interesting that AMC is looking to hit a grand slam in 2023 since the 2024 theatrical release calendar will greatly be shaken up by films pushing due to actors strike.

[From Deadline]

Yeah, Beyonce announced the Beyonce: Renaissance World Tour film this morning and she released the trailer too. I will… actually go to the theater to watch this? It looks like so much fun. It actually gave me goosebumps. It’s a great option for all the Beyhive members who couldn’t afford tickets. The only thing which made me chuckle is Beyonce’s little speech about how she wanted to create a tour where everyone would feel accepted and loved. While she did do that, she also invented the Mute Challenge, and I know some of those people screaming out in the middle of “look around, everybody on mute” got JUMPED during or after the show.
