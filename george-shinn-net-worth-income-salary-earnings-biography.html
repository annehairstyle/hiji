George Shinn Net Worth, Income, Salary, Earnings, Biography

Posted by Artie Phelan on Tuesday, June 4, 2024
George Shinn (born May 11, 1941) is a former owner of the Charlotte/New Orleans Hornets, the Charlotte Knights, and the Gastonia Rangers minor league baseball clubs, as well as the World League of American Football’s Raleigh–Durham Skyhawks. In 1987, he paid $32,500,000 for the Hornets. He was unsuccessful in his quest for a National Hockey League expansion club, the Hampton Roads Rhinos, in 1997.
