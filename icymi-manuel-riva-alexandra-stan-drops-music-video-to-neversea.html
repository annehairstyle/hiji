ICYMI: Manuel Riva &amp; Alexandra Stan Drops Music Video To &quot;Neversea&quot;

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, June 10, 2024

Did you miss the “Neversea” music video by Manuel Riva and Alexandra Stan? Well, fear not because it’s so good to have a visual for this catchy song that is the Neversea music festival’s theme for 2018. It is a new version of the previous track these artists collaborated on, titled “Miami“.

This music video easily promotes the Neversea music festival which is held in Constanta, Romania every year and boasts a load of highly respectable artists who have made their names known within the music industry as a whole. The music festival takes place in Alexandra Stan’s hometown.

Paired with the music video is this description: “NEVER, the biggest beach festival in Europe, which takes place between 5 and 8 July in Constanta, brings on the Romanian coast artists of the moment. The 7 scenes will feature: The Script, Scooter, John Newman, Armin Van Buuren, Hardwell, Alan Walker, Steve Angello, Jonas Blue, Steve Aoki, Redfoo, Nina Kraviz, Pendulum, Jamie Jones and many others. ‘For four days and four nights, the fans of the festival will have the most intense sunsets and sunrises accompanied by the music of the Black Sea waves and the music of the best artists of the world,’ says the organizers NEVER. The program (may be modified) will be as follows: JOI – Hardwell, Alan Walker, GTA, Yellow Claw, Modestep, NGHTMRE, Satra BENZ, DOC, Pauli Acid, Blond: ish, El Mundo, Nightmares on Wax and many others. VINERI – Steve Angello, Galantis, Jonas Blue, Kungs, Redfoo, Nina Kraviz, Lucy, Mumdance, DJ Premier, Pendulum DJ Set, Russia, Deliric & Silent Strike, Clapton, Lum, NU, Rampue, Yokoo and many others. SUN, CHAMIE & KROOKED, Chase & Status DJ Set, Dope DOD, Macanache, Parasites, Christian Loffler, Jan Blomquist & Band, Johnny Newman, Sunnery James & Ryan Marciano, Marwan and many others. SUMMER – The Script, Axwell & Ingrosso, Steve Aoki, Subcarpathians, Scooter, W & W, Jamie Jones, Eats Everything, Dan Andrei, Emi, Kozo, VincentIulian, Dub FX, Noisia DJ Set, Satori Live, Stavroz Live and many others.” (sic).

Within the visual, we watch festival-goers enjoying the Neversea festival. We presume the footage is from last year. We watch as everyone has a brilliant time at the festival, with quite a few scenic shots of the beach, to which Alexandra Stan sings about in the song. There’s also a fantastic firework display at the end. We so wish we could go to this music festival at the start of July – we hope to make it one of the years.
