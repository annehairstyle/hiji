How tall is Jeremy Allen White?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, June 7, 2024

American actor Jeremy Allen White stands 5 feet, 7 inches tall.

Jeremy Allen White was born on February 17, 1991. He is most widely recognized for portraying Phillip "Lip" Gallagher on the Showtime dramedy series “Shameless” for a protracted period of time, 2011–2021.

White has also made appearances in a number of films, including “Afterschool,” “Twelve, After Everything,” and “The Rental,” as well as in the 2018 thriller series “Homecoming”'s first season.

White won a Critics' Choice Television Award, a Golden Globe Award, and a Screen Actors Guild Award for his performance in the Hulu drama series “The Bear,” which he has starred in since 2022.

Where is Jeremy Allen White from?

Jeremy Allen White's parents, Richard and Eloise White, had both relocated to New York City before they met to pursue acting careers. Eloise, his mother, is a North Carolina native who earned a BFA from Virginia Commonwealth University.

After falling in love, spending many years on stage together, and getting married, the couple gave up acting and found jobs that would allow them to support their growing family.

White was raised in Brooklyn's Carroll Gardens. White was a dancer all through elementary school, specializing in ballet, jazz, and tap. He changed his mind and chose to pursue acting when he was 13 years old and enrolled in a middle school dance program.

The Professional Performing Arts School is where he attended high school, and after graduating, White was offered the role of Phillip "Lip" Gallagher on “Shameless.”
