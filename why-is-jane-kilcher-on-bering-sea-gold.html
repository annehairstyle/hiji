Why Is Jane Kilcher On Bering Sea Gold?

Posted by Billy Koelling on Sunday, June 23, 2024

Alaska native Jane Kilcher entered the sets of Bering Sea Gold after Eroica dredge captain Emily Riedel brought her on board as a guest. 

The discovery channel show will welcome its newest season on December 6th as the cast members battle the waters off Nome, Alaska. Kilcher is a new addition to the already thriving program as seekers enter the unpredictable Bering Sea in hopes of taking home gold. 

As someone native to the unforgiving climate, she and her family would be the perfect people to seek adventure.

Emily was the most ecstatic about her joining their experience, even stating they could not have made it without her there. 

On the other hand, Kilcher was not having the best time, going on Facebook to explain she was with the crew when Nome got struck by the worst storm ever.

Nothing is indisputable concerning mother nature as even the most seasoned miners cannot tell how quickly things go wrong, let alone average joes. 

But the waters hold a secret as the hypothesis says the money found at the bottom of the sea would make someone wealthy overnight.

Is Jane Kilcher Leaving Alaska: The Last Frontier?

There are rumors of Jane Kilcher leaving Alaska: The Last Frontier, but it is untrue. 

Jane is still a part of the cast even though she has reduced her screen time. 

Rumors of her leaving the show began after the airing of its eleventh season after they reduced the planned episodes to six. When her concerned fanciers asked about the change-up, she replied they had lots to deal with as Covid was underway. 

Her husband and kids, Etienne and Piper, do not partake in the show as they prefer to keep their privacy, but her family is more than happy to take the reign.

So it was not surprising when the eleventh season took off with her brother-in-law Otto, his daughter Etienne, and other children.

Even though she pacified her fans, they got enraged when she did not get much screen time in the October edition, taking to Twitter to ask about her whereabouts. She again tried to calm them down, saying she remained part of the team.

Besides, she has not been too happy with the creators, as they even blocked the greenhouse from the upcoming episodes. She constructed the house straight out of the pages of Better Homes and Gardens.

The Christmas lights give off a festive feeling with a table to sit at and chat. She was very proud of the creations and teased about showing the creative process through the upcoming episodes. 

But her dreams shattered when cuts got made unknown to her knowledge. The blatant disregard for her wishes makes the audience think things might not be as cordial as they seem between the producers and Jane.
