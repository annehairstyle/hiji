Are There Any Elizabeth Banks Movies We Dont Like?

Posted by Billy Koelling on Sunday, June 16, 2024

The odds were ever in her favor when Elizabeth Banks embarked upon a career in Hollywood. It’s a dream had by many and achieved by so few, but she made it happen. She went on to utter the words, “May the odds be ever in your favor,” repeatedly in her role in one of the biggest movie franchises in the world (The Hunger Games for those who don’t know) and she’s been in some seriously cool roles in her career. She’s been relevant in Hollywood since 1998 and Elizabeth Banks’ movies have been exceptional. From her roles in the Pitch Perfect Movies to Role Models to the Lego Movie, she always hits the nail right on the head and impresses her audience even more. We love her in everything she does, but her role as Effie in the Hunger Games won us over with that high-pitched voice – though we assume she had no choice considering the weight and tightness of those ridiculous ensembles.

Elizabeth Banks’ movies are not always the same. She’s not a character actress who portrays the same woman in every role, but rather an actress with a broad array of talent that allows her to encompass her role and take it on head first in a way that so few actors can do. It’s what makes her so good at what she does. And that’s probably what’s going to make us love her even more when Magic Mike XXL comes out, because it’s likely a role she’s never played before, which means she’s going to wow us all over again. But if we are really honest, it’s not her amazing movie performances that make her a star in our eyes; it’s her recurring on occasion role on Modern Family as Sal, Mitch and Cam’s perpetually drunken kind of slutty friend she plays so perfectly that makes us love Banks.
