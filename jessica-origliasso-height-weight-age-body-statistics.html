    Jessica Origliasso Facts

  • Jessica and her sister Lisa started performing at the age of 5. They used to perform at local events and award shows under the name of Origliasso Twins.
  • In their teen years, they changed their band’s name to Lisa & Jessica. They even released a couple of studio albums under that name.
  • In 2003, Jessica and her sister roped in two of their friends to form a new band, Teal. Soon after, their band released their single, Baby It’s Over.
  • In 2004, Jessica and Lisa were signed up as songwriters with Excalibur Productions Australia after having impressed music executive Hayden Bell with their demo tracks.
  • After they were signed up by the independent record label Engine Room Recordings, they traveled all over the world, writing and recording demos with other songwriters.
  • When Jessica and her sister signed a record deal worth $2 million with Sire Records, which is a subsidiary of Warner Musi, they decided to call themselves The Veronicas, which was inspired by a line in the film Heathers (1988).
  • The owner of Archie Comics filed a trademark infringement lawsuit against them as they argued that the band name was inspired by the comic character Veronica Lodge. They eventually reached a settlement, which saw the twin sisters being featured in the comics.
  • Along with her sister, Jessica started working with Steve Irwin‘s Wildlife Warriors Worldwide in 2006. The charity works to conserve natural habitats and protect animals.
  • In January 2009, she posed wearing nothing except a faux fur jacket for a photoshoot conducted by the animal rights group, PETA. The photoshoot aimed to hammer an anti-fur message.
  • In recognition of their work in raising awareness about animal protection and conservation, Jessica and her sister were declared Wildlife Warriors of 2010.
  • She was an active participant in the Wear it with Pride campaign that aimed to bring reformation of the same-sex laws in Australia and overturn discrimination faced by same-sex couples.
  • In August 2007, her music group released their exclusive fashion range at the Target Miranda store. The products from their range were sold all over Australia.
  • On June 25, 2021, The Veronicas released its 5th studio album Human and it includes songs like Without You, LA, Biting My Tongue, Human, Jealous, and Think of Me. The album peaked at number #5 on the Australian Albums (ARIA) chart.
