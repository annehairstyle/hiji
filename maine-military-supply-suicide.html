Maine Military Supply Suicide

Posted by Artie Phelan on Sunday, June 2, 2024

Maine Military Supply: A Hub for Outdoor Enthusiasts

On Tuesday, a tragic incident occurred at Maine Military Supply, a business specializing in military surplus items and outdoor equipment. A woman reportedly died by suicide after shooting herself inside the store on Route 1A. The Holden Police Chief, Eduardo Benjamin, confirmed that no other individuals were injured or involved in the incident. The victim's identity has not been released, and authorities are still investigating the matter, with an official report yet to be released[1].

The Incident and Response

The store was closed for the remainder of the day on Tuesday, but a sign outside indicated that it would reopen on Wednesday[1]. The Penobscot County Sheriff's Office, Holden Police Department, Holden Fire Department, and Brewer Police Department all responded to the scene to assist with the situation[1]. The incident has left the community in shock, and thoughts and condolences go out to the victim's family and friends during this difficult time.

Maine Military Supply: A Hub for Outdoor Enthusiasts

Maine Military Supply offers a wide range of products for outdoor enthusiasts, including military surplus items, tactical equipment, weapons, ammo, knives, and crossbows. The store also provides hunting, fishing, and camping equipment, making it a one-stop shop for all your outdoor needs[1]. Additionally, the business features a 4,000-square-foot shooting range with guns available for rent and a paintball course for those seeking a more adventurous experience[1].

For every season, Maine Military Supply carries a sizable range of clothes for men, women, and children, as well as hats, boots, gloves, and other accessories[1]. Whether you're looking for military surplus items or planning an outdoor excursion, the knowledgeable staff at Maine Military Supply is ready to assist you.

Safety First: Bringing Your Own Weapon or Renting One

If you choose to visit Maine Military Supply's shooting range, you have the option to bring your own weapon or rent one from the store. However, it is essential to ensure that your personal firearm is unloaded before entering the business, as safety is a top priority[1]. By following these guidelines, you can help create a secure environment for yourself and others.

Conclusion

The incident at Maine Military Supply is a tragic event that has deeply affected the community. As authorities continue to investigate, our thoughts are with the victim's family and friends. Maine Military Supply will reopen on Wednesday, offering its wide range of products and services to outdoor enthusiasts. Whether you're in need of military surplus items or planning an outdoor adventure, the store's knowledgeable staff is ready to assist you. Remember to prioritize safety when visiting the shooting range, either by bringing an unloaded personal firearm or renting one from the store.
