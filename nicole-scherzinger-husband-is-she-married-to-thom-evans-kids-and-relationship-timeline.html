Nicole Scherzinger Husband: Is She Married To Thom Evans? Kids And Relationship Timeline

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, June 24, 2024

The dating history of American singer-actress Nicole is highly anticipated by her admirers. People are interested in Nicole Scherzinger’s husband since she has dated a lot of guys since beginning her career in the entertainment world.

Having a career in singing, acting, and television series, Nicole Scherzinger is a well-known figure in the American entertainment business.

As the lead vocalist of the Pussycat Dolls, a female group that found popularity internationally in the early 2000s with hits including “Don’t Cha,” “Buttons,” and “When I Grow Up,” Nichole shot to fame.

Scherzinger began a solo music career after the Pussycat Dolls broke up in 2010 and released her first album, “Killer Love,” in 2011.

She has also starred in movies including “Men in Black 3” and “Dirty Dancing,” and she has served as a judge on many seasons of the television singing competition series “The X Factor” and “The Masked Singer.”

Since January 2020, Nicole, an actress and multi-talented vocalist, has been dating Thom Evans, a Scottish model and former rugby union player.

The pair have been dating for more than a year, but they haven’t gotten married yet. Thom and Nicole first crossed paths in 2019 when she was a judge and he was a competitor on the set of X Factor: Celebrity.

In spite of their business connection at first, the two quickly became attracted to one another romantically and started dating.

Nicole and Thom went on the red carpet together at a high-profile event in January 2020, only three months after they started dating, to officially announce their romance.

Since then, the pair has been photographed hanging together and supporting one another’s jobs on several occasions. They seem to love one other’s company.

Despite the COVID-19 epidemic and their hectic schedules, Nicole and Thom are still a supportive and loving marriage.

Nicole Scherzinger Kids: Who Are They?

Nicole, the well-known singer and actress, is childless as of 2023. She has, however, said that she wants to have a family with Thom Evans, her partner.

Nicole said that she wants to have a child and have a family with Thom in an interview with Mirror. Since January 2020, the pair has been in love and is committed to constructing a future together.

Nicole thinks she and Thom would be wonderful parents, therefore she also wants to wed him. She has said that she wants to have children when the moment is perfect and that she understands the significance of timing.

Nicole Scherzinger Relationship Timeline

Prior to falling in love with her present partner, Thom Evans, the gifted singer-songwriter had a wild dating past.

She has had romantic relationships with a number of well-known guys from various industries throughout the years. Her romantic relationships have often generated news, and her personal life has been the focus of significant media scrutiny.

Between 1999 and 2000, Scherzinger was in a relationship with comedian and actor Nick Cannon.

After that, she had prominent partnerships with the lead vocalist of the band 311 Nick Hexum from 2000 to 2004. At one time, the two even were engaged.

Scherzinger started dating reality personality Talan Torriero in 2006, and the two were together for a year.

After that, from March to October of 2007, she dated comedian Dane Cook. Later that year, she started dating Formula 1 driver Lewis Hamilton, whom she had met at the Munich MTV Europe Music Awards.

They broke up in February 2015, having been together for over nine years.

Scherzinger began a one-month relationship with Swiss soccer player Pajtim Kasami in June 2015.

Then, in 2016, she started dating Grigor Dimitrov, a tennis player from Bulgaria. The two of them reportedly parted up in July 2018 after dating for two years.

Scherzinger and well-known musician Ed Sheeran were said to have dated, however, this hasn’t been verified.

On the other hand, Scherzinger fell in love in 2019 with Thom Evans, someone she met on the set of X Factor. Since then, the pair have remained together, and their bond seems to be solid.
