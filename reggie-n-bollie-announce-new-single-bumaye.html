Reggie 'N' Bollie Announce New Single 'BUMAYE'

Posted by Kelle Repass on Friday, June 14, 2024

Reggie ‘N’ Bollie are back with a brand new single. The track is titled ‘BUMAYE’ and is the boys’ second release via their own record label F.R.O.D Music and their fourth single since coming second on the 2015 series of The X Factor where they were mentored by Geordie pop princess Cheryl.

The duo originally from Ghana launched F.R.O.D (Fearlessly Ruling Our Destinies) at the start of the year after parting ways with Simon Cowell’s label Syco. During their time at the Sony label they released two hit singles; ‘New Girl‘, and ‘Link Up‘. And their debut F.R.O.D release was the catchy ‘This Is The Life‘.

And now the lovable Reggie ‘N’ Bollie have announced that their brand new single ‘BUMAYE’ will be released later this month. It is the latest taste of what is to come from their upcoming debut album which will drop this summer.

Speaking exclusively to us about ‘BUMAYE’ the pair said: “BUMAYE is about that special killer thing, about someone that makes you love them [all] the more for no particular reason.”

Reggie ‘N’ Bollie’s new single ‘BUMAYE‘ will be released on July 21st and is available to pre-order now. Make sure you follow the duo on Twitter @ReggieNBollie to keep up to date with all their latest music and tour news!
