Who is Mina Bonino, Fede Valverde's wife? From the start of their relationship to the current contro

Posted by Billy Koelling on Wednesday, June 26, 2024

Like many other relationships, the love between Fede Valverde and Mina Bonino came about thanks to social media.

The Real Madrid player's current partner is now back in the social media limelight due to the controversy with Villarreal player Alex Baena, but the truth is that the Argentinian was already a well-known figure, especially in her native country, where she was a very charismatic journalist.

Born on October 14, 1993, Bonino is a media professional who, from a very young age, made a name for herself in Argentina thanks to her work in television where she was a regular reporter and used to interview different personalities.

The Argentinian is a fervent fan of one of the best teams in South America, River Plate, which is why she was also famous in her country.

How Fede Valverde met Mina Bonino

Valverde and his partner met online. Although they did not announce their relationship until March 2019, the truth is that they had already been having conversations for some time.

As she herself confessed in an interview with Infobae, they first met through Instagram and, by then, he was already in Madrid.

The journalist did not hesitate to get on a plane to Spain and meet Valverde.

"Everything went much better than I expected," said Bonino.

Just two months after their first date, the Argentinian moved in with the Uruguayan midfielder and they haven't been apart since.

Just one year later, in February 2020, the couple welcomed their first child into the world after a complicated 14-hour delivery in a Madrid hospital.

Bonino still works as a journalist and covers Real Madrid, a club she often shares her admiration for on social media. She watches every game, even when Valverde is not playing.
