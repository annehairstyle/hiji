Is Wendy Williams' Ex-Husband Kevin Hunter Remarried? An Update for Fans

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, June 26, 2024

Is Kevin Hunter remarried? This is the question Wendy Williams fans have been asking. And it turns out that he may have plans to jump the broom.

Ever since the release of the biopic Wendy Williams: The Movie, fans have been shocked, outraged, and sympathetic toward Wendy Williams. While there have been talks about what Wendy endured during her marriage, the biopic put a lot of those rumors into perspective. And it also made people look at the icon in a brand new light.

Her ex-husband, Kevin Hunter, has been subject to tons of criticism. And since it’s not news that he stepped out on Wendy multiple times, fans are wondering what exactly he’s up to these days and if he has remarried. Here’s the full scoop.

Wendy Williams' ex-husband Kevin Hunter is engaged to Sharina Hudson.

Whew chile, the disgust! It goes without saying that Kevin and Wendy's relationship faced a lot of highs and lows. And one of those lows that hurt their union was his cheating. While he did admit to cheating with various women throughout their marriage, there was one woman who stuck around — Sharina Hudson. And it turns out that Kevin and Sharina are reportedly engaged.

Sources, per Page Six, reveal that Kevin bought a ring, valued at $80,000, which Sharina has been seen wearing in various social media posts dating back to April 2020. It's still unclear when Kevin proposed to Sharina, but sources do share that the ring was purchased before Wendy's health issues came to light.

In case you've been MIA, Kevin has been with Sharina for 13 years — while married to Wendy. In fact, Sharina even gave birth to a daughter back in March 2019, according to Page Six. And that news was ultimately the final straw that set Kevin and Wendy's divorce in motion.

And of course, Wendy fans are not pleased with the engagement news. Many people took to social media to share their disapproval of the engagement and of his actions during and after his marriage to Wendy.

Some fans also didn't mince words about Sharina. From the beginning, a plethora of fans said that she's in for her share of karma for being with Kevin when he was married. And now that news of an engagement has come to light, social media users have been relentless with the shade.

Despite the drama, Kevin has been busy working on establishing multiple businesses.

While it's safe to say that the drama of his marriage to Wendy and mistress will follow him for the rest of his life, Kevin is focused on building his own empire. In February 2021, Page Six initially reported that he was becoming a restaurateur, and now, his downtown Brooklyn restaurant, Loreto, is officially open for service.

While Kevin is no stranger to the business world — since he reportedly has a barbershop in Florida and has launched the Hunter Publishing Company — this is the first time he is stepping into the food industry.

And since he has been successful with his business endeavors in the past, it's safe to say that this restaurant may follow suit. After all, no one can resist good food.

As for Kevin making the decision to remarry, it's not a huge surprise. Kevin and Sharina have been together for quite some time. And since his divorce with Wendy is officially old news, and since he has a child with Sharina, it makes sense that they take that next step in their relationship.
