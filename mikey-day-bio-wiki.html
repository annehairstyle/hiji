Mikey Day Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Kelle Repass on Tuesday, June 4, 2024

Who is Mikey Day?

Mikey Day is an American actor, comedian, and writer. People mostly recognize him as the cast member on ‘Saturday Night Live’ (SNL). Additionally, he has also appeared in ‘Brother Nature’, ‘Maya & Marty’ and ‘Robot Chicken’ among others.

Mikey Day: Early Life, Childhood, Ethnicity

Mikey day was born in Orange County, California on March 20, 1980. He became interested in drama in his childhood years and was involved in the drama program and the associated student body.

He is of American nationality. Furthermore, he belongs to a mixed (English, Scottish, Irish, German, and Sephardi Jewish).

In which school did Mikey Day attend?

Talking about his education, Mikey Day attended Panorama Elementary School around 1990. Later, he graduated from El Modena High School in Orange. Additionally, he graduated from the University of California, Los Angeles with a degree in theater.

Mikey Day: Career, Profession

Day initially began his career with the Groundlings. It is a Los Angeles-based improvisational theater group. In 1999, he appeared in the TV series ‘The Mike & Ben Show’. Next, in 2004, he appeared in ‘Angel’ as O’Shea. He portrayed the role of Charlie in ‘Totally Awesome’. Similarly, Day also appeared in the TV series ‘Faking the Video’. Since then, he has appeared in numerous other movies and television series. All in all, he has over 25 credits as an actor.

Some other movies and television series that Day has appeared in are ‘The David S. Pumpkins Halloween Special’, ‘Brother Nature’, ‘Idiotsitter’, ‘Mad’, ‘Animation Domination High-Def’, ‘Friends with Benefits, ‘Nathan vs. Nurture’, ‘Nick Cannon Presents Short Circuitz’, and ‘The Underground’ among others. Additionally, he has 17 credits as a writer and 6 credits as a producer. He joined ‘Saturday Night Live’ in 2013.

Day garnered the Primetime Emmy Award nomination in 2016. In addition, he won the WGA Award in 2017. All in all, he has 1 win and 5 nominations to his name.
