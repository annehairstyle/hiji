Mark Labbett

Posted by Artie Phelan on Sunday, June 2, 2024

Mark Andrew Labbett is a British television personality who is best known in the United Kingdom for his role as Chaser on ITV’s game program The Chase. He originally debuted on the show in 2009 and has subsequently played the sole Chaser in the American version in 2013 and one of the five Chasers in the Australian version since 2016. Mark received his Master of Arts degree in mathematics from Exeter College, Oxford, where he studied for four years. What does Mark Labbett do?

  • Mark began his career teaching in secondary schools, including Cove School in Farnborough, where he worked as a supply teacher in physical education and math. He grew interested in quizzing after that.

In the same year, Labbett joined ITV’s The Chase as one of the Chasers. In August 2013, he became the only chaser on the American version of the game show.
  • He then appeared in the ITV programme Sugar-Free Farm three years later. The National Lottery People’s Quiz, Are You an Egghead?, and University Challenge are among his other television performances. Along with UK Chaser Anne Hegerty, he is one of the five Chasers on the Australian edition of the game show The Chase.
