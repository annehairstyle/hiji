Drew Butler Career

Butler works as the Managing Director of i(x) investments. He brings his broad experience as a family office advisor who specializes in impact investments and real estate. Drew formed Butler Global Partners in 2012 to work directly for select families seeking customized solutions in asset and family management.

Butler began his career working at Merrill Lynch as a Registered Client Associate. He then joined Richman Asset Management where he worked as a Senior Capital Management Associate. While there, he provided affordable housing development and management of Low-Income Housing Tax Credit Funds. Butler has also worked in London as a researcher for the UK Parliament, House of Commons, and as an Investment Analyst at New York Life.
