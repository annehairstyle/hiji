Bec Hewitt is an Australian actor, dancer, presenter, and singer. She has worked on projects such as Home and Away, Water Rats, Dancing with the Stars, Police Rescue, and Roar. As a musician, she has released albums and tracks like Bec Cartwright, On the Borderline, All Seats Taken, and A Matter of Time.
