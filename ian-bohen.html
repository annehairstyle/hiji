Ian Bohen

Posted by Artie Phelan on Thursday, June 20, 2024

Bohen in 2015, photo by kathclick/Bigstock.com

Birth Name: Ian Stuart Bohen

Place of Birth: Carmel-by-the-Sea, Monterey, California, United States

Date of Birth: September 24, 1976

Ethnicity: Swiss-German, German, as well as Irish and English

Ian Bohen is an American actor.

Ian’s paternal grandfather was Harold Gilbert Bohen (the son of James Gilbert Bohen and Laura Elizabeth Fessey). Harold was born in Wisconsin. James was the son of Gilbert Bohen/Bohan, who was born in Rhode Island, to Irish parents, and of Bridget Walsh, who was born in Wisconsin, also to Irish parents. Laura was the daughter of Waldo J. Fessey, who was of English descent, and of Margaret A. Hurley.

Ian’s paternal grandmother was Margaret Osenroth (the daughter of Charles Frederick Osenroth and Vanelia M. Kleeber). Margaret was born in Illinois, and was of German descent.

Ian’s maternal grandfather was Julian Victor Oechslin (the son of Hans/John Ernest Oechslin and Julia Christen). Julian was born in Wisconsin. Hans was the son of Johann Gottfried Oechslin and Mary Barbara Portmann, both from Canton of Lucerne, Switzerland. Julia was born in Switzerland.

Ian’s maternal grandmother was Joyce L. Goodman (the daughter of Irving French Goodman and Ethel Fay Atter). Joyce was born in Wisconsin. Irving was the son of James Ulysses Goodman and Tyra Sweeney. Ethel was the daughter of Benjamin Thorp Atter and Matilda Augusta Bauermeister.
