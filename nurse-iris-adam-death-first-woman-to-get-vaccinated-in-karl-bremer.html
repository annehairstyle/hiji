Nurse Iris Adam Death, First Woman To Get Vaccinated In Karl Bremer

Posted by Artie Phelan on Friday, June 28, 2024

For your kind information, she was the first woman in Karl Bremer hospital of  South Africa who got vaccinated. The reason behind her unexpected death was told to be the covid virus.
