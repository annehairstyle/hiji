Raxstar Facts

  • He is the middle of 3 children born into a Sikh family that resided in Luton all through Raxstar’s childhood years.
  • Growing up, he would mostly listen to Punjabi and Bollywood music.
  • He credits pop singer and songwriter Michael Jackson to be his first real music inspiration. Thereafter, he began listening to various other artists such as Gurdas Maan, Nusrat Fateh Ali Khan, Warren G, Busta Rhymes, and Common.
  • It was during his high school years that he discovered his true passion for music.
  • In 2005, his hit debut single Keep It Undercover was played on BBC Radio 1 for the first time.
  • His stage name “Raxstar” came from a cousin of his who would call him “Rax” and he would add the word “star” to his nickname, hence forming the name “Raxstar”.
  • He released his debut studio album titled Glass Ceiling on September 1, 2018.
  • Raxstar has collaborated with various well-known artists including Manj Musik, Badshah, Raftaar, Bohemia, and Mumzy Stranger.
