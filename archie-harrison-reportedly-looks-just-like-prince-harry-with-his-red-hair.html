Archie Harrison Reportedly Looks Just Like Prince Harry With His Red Hair

Posted by Artie Phelan on Saturday, June 15, 2024

Archie Harrison Mountbatten-Windsor is already three months old, but royal fans haven’t had many opportunities to catch a glimpse of Prince Harry and Meghan Markle’s firstborn son yet. With the exception of his official introduction to the world, his christening photos, and a couple of paparazzi pics, Archie Harrison’s first few weeks on Earth have been extremely private. However, an insider has just given an update on the young royal and revealed that Archie Harrison has red hair just like his dad.

People Magazine is reporting that baby Archie is a “happy baby,” and he is growing fast.

“He has lovely, puffy little legs and tufts of reddish hair. He’s really adorable,” said the source.

When Prince Harry and Meghan Markle introduced Archie two days after his May 6th birth, the Duchess of Sussex said they were still trying to figure out which parent he resembled the most. Prince Harry added that everyone says babies change so much in their first two weeks, and they were going to monitor the changing process.

“His looks are changing every single day so who knows,” said Harry.

Harry, Meghan, and Archie Harrison recently took a secret trip to Ibiza on a private jet to celebrate the Duchess’ 38th birthday. They reportedly flew to the island on August 6th – two days after Markle’s birthday – and stayed in a private villa for six days before heading home.

The trio is also planning a trip to Africa this fall for their first official royal tour as a family. The Duke and Duchess plan to raise awareness of the work people are doing in the local communities across the commonwealth.

Harry and Meghan have already traveled to Africa together numerous times. For the Duchess’ 35th birthday in August 2016, Harry invited her to Botswana just weeks after they met
