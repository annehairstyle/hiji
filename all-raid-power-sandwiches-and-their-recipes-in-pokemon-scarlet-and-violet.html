All Raid Power Sandwiches and their recipes in Pokemon Scarlet and Violet

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, June 5, 2024

There is a lot of post-game content to be enjoyed by players seeking a competitive edge in Pokemon Scarlet and Violet. Participating and completing Tera Raid Battles all across the Paldea region is one of the best ways to grow stronger after the completion of the main storyline.

These Raid Battles will allow you to obtain large amounts of valuable resources such as Exp. Candy and Herba Mystica. Interestingly, by cooking and consuming Raid Power Sandwiches in Pokemon Scarlet and Violet, you can also increase the number of drops that these encounters bring.

Raid Power Sandwiches are some of the best items to help you maximize the rewards that can be obtained from Raid Battles in Pokemon Scarlet and Violet. You may come across a wide variety of Raid Power Sandwiches in the Gen IX, each with different recipes.
