Colin Farrell's Changing Looks

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, June 28, 2024

Colin Farrell's Changing Looks

2000

After appearing in the Irish TV series Ballykissangel, a young Farrell made his Hollywood debut in Tigerland.

2002

The actor hit a Photo Booth press conference with a head shaved for his role in Daredevil. "I never spend much time on my hair anyway," he told InStyle of the look.

2003

Farrell hid his Alexander bleached locks under a knit cap for the S.W.A.T. press conference.

2003

He flaunted his bleached ends at the premiere of Intermission. "It was outrageous how bad my hair was," he told InStyle of his flaxen Alexander locks. "To have the abomination of hairstyles I sported at the center of a $150 million film-it was terrible. The pieces...are clipins you can buy at any dollar store downtown."

2004

Back to his natural dark locks (and favored goatee) at an N.Y.C. screening of Alexander.

2006

Short and spiky for the world premiere of Miami Vice.

2008

Shoulder-length strands at a photo call for Pride and Glory.

2010

He sported a classic fedora at the Tribeca Film Festival premiere of Ondine.

2011

The Horrible Bosses star showed a little salt-and-pepper at the American Giving Awards.

2012

The Total Recall lead sported a pomaded style (and some carefully manicured facial hair) at a pre-Academy Awards party.
