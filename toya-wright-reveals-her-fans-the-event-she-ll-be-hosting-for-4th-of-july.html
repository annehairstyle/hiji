Toya Wright Reveals Her Fans The Event Shell Be Hosting For 4th Of July

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, June 26, 2024

If you were wondering what to do for the 4th of July and especially if you would like to meet Toya Wright, well, here’s your chance. She just revealed on her social media account the event that she has scheduled for that specific day.

Fans were happy to see that Toya has planned more events, and some of them even said that they would definitely be attending this one.

Other than this, Toya has been spending a lot of time with her family, but also working to help society.

https://www.instagram.com/p/ByZBWCvDZK8/

For example, she has been involved in this movement called Wight No More which aims to fight obesity and overweight.

Tomorrow, June 8, Toya is expecting everyone at the 5k walk/run event, and fans are going not only to support the important cause but also to meet Toya and her family as well.

‘4 more days Shreveport, la!!! grab your family & friends and join us this Saturday at Southern Hills park at 11 am. If you Register before Friday you will receive a free weight no more T-shirt. Link in bio #wnmshreveport #letsfightobesitytogether >> My boo @monyettashaw is also bringing her Keep It Classy co-parenting tour to Shreveport this Friday! Tickets available on Monyettashaw.com #friendsaupportfriends,’ Toya captioned a recent post.

As it was reported, people are attending these events not only to support the important movement but also to meet Toya and her family.
