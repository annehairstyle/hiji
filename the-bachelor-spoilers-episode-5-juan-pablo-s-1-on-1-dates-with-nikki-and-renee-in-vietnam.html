The Bachelor Spoilers Episode 5: Juan Pablos 1-on-1 Dates With Nikki And Renee In Vietnam

Posted by Billy Koelling on Thursday, June 20, 2024

Last night we watched Juan Pablo and the remaining bachelorettes explore the city of Seoul, South Korea. Next week on Episode 5 of The Bachelor, which is set to air on Monday February 3rd, Juan Pablo and his remaining 11 women will travel to the city of Da Nang, Vietnam.

While in Da Nang, Vietnam Juan Pablo will go on two 1-on-1 dates, and one group date. The first 1-on-1 date will go to single Mom, Renee Oteri. According to Reality Steve, Renee and Juan Pablo will spend some quality time exploring the Vietnamese city. Renee will get fitted for a custom dress, go shopping with Juan Pablo, and then the pair will head out to dinner.

The second 1-on-1 date Juan Pablo decides to spend with Nikki Ferrell. Although no one in the house seems to care for Nikki, Juan Pablo definitely does! In fact Nikki is supposed to be the winner of season 18. Hopefully he will see her true colors soon, Clare was dead on in Episode 4 when she accused Nikki of acting fake whenever Juan Pablo was around. On their 1-on-1 date Nikki and Juan Pablo will go cave-diving in Episode 5, and share a romantic dinner for two in an actual cave. Renee and Nikki both receive roses at the end of their 1-on-1 dates.

The other nine women, Cassandra, Andi, Kelly, Kat, Clare, Chelsie, Danielle, Sharleen, and Alli, spend a fun-filled day with Juan Pablo in Da Nang. The group goes out on the water for paddle boats, crashes an authentic Vietnamese dinner at one of the residents of Da Nang’s houses, and then ride some water buffalos. At the end of the group date Juan Pablo presents a rose to Clare.  Three women are eliminated on the February 3 episode, find out who by clicking here.
