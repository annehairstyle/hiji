The pilot episode will be written by Wandavision actor Randall Park and screenwriter Michael Golamco. The duo is known for their work on the 2019 Netflix romantic comedy, Always Be My Maybe.

As reported by The Hollywood Reporter, Condor will be playing a character named Iris.
