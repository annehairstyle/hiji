Hailey Baldwin Bieber Stuns In Christopher Esber Cut-Out Dress As She Dines With Kendall Jenner

Posted by Artie Phelan on Saturday, June 22, 2024

Hailey Baldwin Bieber was spotted enjoying dinner with best friend and fellow model Kendall Jenner on Sunday, July 19, 2020. The two went to Malibu’s Nobu restaurant and while both looked gorgeous, Hailey especially struck a stunning figure as she wore a Christopher Esber, maxi dress. Hailey recently went viral for her behavior after being accused of not being nice to a hostess, Hailey has since apologized, and now she’s going viral for her outfit instead of something negative.

Kendall Jenner was spotted earlier Sunday when she was at the beach with Kourtney Kardashian, Scott Disick, and friends, but by evening she and Hailey were hanging out and having a great time. Hailey and Kendall wore face masks (Kendall wore a Skims one) and the two were spotted outside holding hands. Both women showed off their flawless abs as Hailey wore the cut-out maxi dress and Kendall opted for a mini-skirt, with a black crop top that featured spaghetti straps.

Kendall wore a miniskirt by Mozh Mozh and carried a Baby Amber shoulder bag by By Far.

Hailey, however, wore the Christopher Esber, maxi cut-out dress in cream, and for footwear wore a pair of Vignette vegan mule sandals by Femme Shoes. She wore a white face mask and carried a kelly-green, Bottega Veneta chain purse called the Cassette Bag. You may see photos of Hailey and Kendall below.

She wore gold earrings and paired several necklaces together including a gold, herringbone chain necklace from Fallon Jewelry along with a medium, gold chain by the same designer. She wore the necklaces with an Anita Ko Sagitarrius pendant.
