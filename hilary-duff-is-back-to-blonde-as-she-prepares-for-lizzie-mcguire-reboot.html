Hilary Duff Is Back To Blonde As She Prepares For Lizzie McGuire Reboot

Posted by Artie Phelan on Tuesday, June 18, 2024

Hilary Duff is preparing for her upcoming role as she returns to the Disney show that made her a household name, Lizzie McGuire. Hilary, her boyfriend Matthew Koma and her two children Luca Cruz Comrie and Banks Violet Bair are often spotted around Los Angeles and Hilary is receiving renewed attention now that Disney announced Lizzie would be returning to airwaves. Though Hilary hasn’t begun filming yet, she is working with the show’s creator and has been speaking out about the vision for the reboot.

The 32-year-old’s acting career has continued to thrive since Lizzie McGuire ended in 2004. She currently stars as the character Kelsey Peters in the series Younger. Younger has been renewed for a seventh season and will return to Paramount networks and TV Land in 2020.

It’s unclear when the Lizzie McGuire reboot will air, but it is highly possible that Hilary Duff will appear in both television series simultaneously.

You may hear Hilary Duff speak about the new Lizzie McGuire reboot in the video player below.

Hillary and children Luca and Banks were spotted in Los Angeles over the weekend shopping for Halloween costumes. Luca is seven-years-old and Banks is one-year-old.  In the video above, Hilary spoke of how it wasn’t the right time to reboot Lizzie McGuire in the past but now is a perfect time.

She didn’t go into specifics as to what made the timing off, but it’s possible that it was due to Hilary’s role as a mother and putting her family first.

At 32-years-old, Hilary has done it all. In addition to her acting career, she has also become a professional singer, a producer, and a best-selling author.
