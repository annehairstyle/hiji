Ryan Lochte is an American professional swimmer who is known for being an Olympic medalist and for winning several tournaments such as the World Championship, Pan American Games, and Pan Pacific Championship. He has also participated in a variety of other tournaments such as World Aquatics Championship and FINA Short Course World Championship. He has been sponsored by brands like TYR Sport, Inc. During the course of his career, Ryan has been honored with several awards such as “Swammy Award”, “American Swimmer of the Year Award”, and “World Swimmer of the Year Award”.

  • As a kid, Lochte lived in Canandaigua, New York before his family decided to shift to Florida because of his father’s opportunity to work as a swimming trainer.

  • Ryan has constructed his own core workout video named “Lochte Hardcore” featuring fitness trainer Matt DeLancey.
  • While at college, Ryan was selected for the NCAA Swimmer of the Year award for two times. He was also a seven-time NCAA champion and seven-time SEC champion.
  • Lochte won six gold medals at the 2010 Pan Pacific Swimming Championship in California.
  • He qualified for the 2012 Summer Olympics in London by finishing first in the 400-meter individual medley and 200-meter backstroke. He also achieved second place in the 200-meter freestyle and 200-meter individual medley.
  • Lochte won his first gold medal at the 2012 Olympics in London after he managed to secure the first place in 400 meters individual medley with a total time of 4:05.
