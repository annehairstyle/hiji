Rihanna posts series of naked pregnancy photos: So beautiful

Posted by Artie Phelan on Friday, June 21, 2024

Rihanna has shared a series of never-before-seen photos from her first pregnancy as she prepares to welcome her second child.

The Barbadian pop star, 35, who unveiled her latest pregnancy while performing at the Super Bowl in February, took to Instagram with a carousel of jaw-dropping, naked photos, which were taken while she was pregnant with her son last year.

Rihanna could be seen wearing body jewellery in the tropical snaps, sporting wet hair as she posed by the ocean.

“Here’s a little series I call ‘Rub on ya titties’,” she wrote. “In honor (sic) of my first pregnancy, embracing motherhood like a g, and the magic that this body made!”

Rihanna, who welcomed son RZA Athelston Mayers in May last year with her partner, rapper A$AP Rocky, continued: “Baby RZA … he in there not having a clue how nuts his mama is, or how obsessed he was bout to make me.”

The billionaire cosmetics owner, who walked the red carpet at the Met Gala earlier this month, appeared to suggest she was pregnant during her 12-minute Super Bowl set earlier this year, but officially confirmed it after the show via her reps.

The music icon performed some of her greatest hits during a stunning halftime set in Arizona, which was widely lauded by critics and fans.

According to Rihanna, who reportedly did the show for free, her decision to perform in the iconic halftime show was driven by her baby boy.

She explained at a press conference with Apple Music: “It is important for me to do it this year, it is important for representation, it is important for my son to see that.”

More Coverage

While Rihanna hasn’t released a new album in seven years, Forbes reports she’s worth a whopping $2.2 billion thanks to her business venture with makeup and skincare line, Fenty Beauty, which has occupied much of her time over the past decade.

However, she told British Vogue she hoped to release her long-awaited ninth album before the end of 2023.

“I want it to be this year,” she said. “Like, honestly, it’d be ridiculous if it’s not this year. But I just want to have fun. I just want to make music and make videos.”
