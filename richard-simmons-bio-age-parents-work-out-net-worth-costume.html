Richard Simmons Bio, Age, Parents, Work out, Net Worth, Costume

Richard Simmons Biography | Wiki

Richard Simmons is a retired American fitness instructor, actor, and comedian. He’s known for his eccentric, flamboyant and energetic personality. He promoted weight-loss programs, most prominently to the Oldies line of aerobics videos through his Sweating.

Richard Simmons Age | Birthday

Simmons was born as Milton Teagle Simmons on 12 July 1948 in New Orleans, Louisiana, United States of America. He celebrates his birthday on 12th July every year. His zodiac sign is cancer. He is 71 years old as of 2019.

Richard Simmons Education

Simmons attended Cor Jesu High School, presently Brother Martin High School. He then joined the University of Southwestern Louisiana at Lafayette. Simmons later transferred to Florida State University and graduated with a degree in Art. After graduation, Simons worked briefly as a waiter and advertiser.

Richard Simmons Height

Richard Simmons Family | Parents

Milton Simmons was born to Leonard Douglas Simmons Sr. a Methodist (father) who worked as a master of ceremonies (MC) and later in thrift stores and Shirley May (mother) who was a Russian Jewish and was a traveling fan dancer and later a cosmetics store saleswoman. Richard has one older brother named Leonard Jr.

Richard Simmons Wife | Married

At 71 years old, Simmons has never been married and has no children. He lives a single life at his residence in Beverly Hills, California. His sexual orientation has been kept secret and many have speculated on whether or not he’s gay.

Richard Simmons Career

From his early childhood to adolescence, Simmons was obese. At the age of 15, he weighed 83 kilos and reached up to 122 kilos during his adolescence.

He began his professional life as a host for a restaurant called Derek’s after moving to Los Angeles in the 1970s. He started his own exercise studio called The Anatomy Asylum, which focused on the right diet and proper exercise in a supportive environment. The studio was renamed Simmons and continued to operate in Beverly Hills and he appeared on motivational classes and aerobics throughout the week.

He also runs its own membership-based website and has numerous official social media pages such as Facebook, Twitter, My Space, and Youtube. He has also appeared on numerous TV shows and in movies including TV shows like Whose Line is it Anyway? The Larry Sanders Show, Saturday Night Live. He also hosted Sirius Satellite Radio Channel 102 in an exercise program called Lighten Up With Richard Simmons. Suddenly he withdrew from his public appearances in 2014 and closed his gym in 2016. Although he and those close to him claim that he is doing great things, speculations are being made about his sudden withdrawal.

Richard Simmons Net Worth

Simmons has an estimated net worth of $15 million which he has earned through his successful career as a fitness instructor, actor, and comedian.

Is Richard Simmons still alive

Simmons is alive and in good health. On April 19, 2017, following a hospitalization for severe indigestion, Simmons made his first public statement in over a year, posting on Facebook a photo of himself and the message “I’m not ‘missing’, just a little under the weather”. However, the picture that was included in the post was from as far back as 2013, leading to speculation that the person who posted the message might not actually have been Simmons. Since then there have been no reports of him being sick or having any health-related issues.

Where Is Richard Simmons

Since 2014, he has made no major public appearances and stopped appearing in public in February of 2014. Speculation began in March 2016 that his housekeeper was holding him hostage. In response, he gave an audio interview on the Today Show on March 14, denying the rumors.

He sued a private investigator from Los Angeles in June 2018, claiming that over a year earlier he had placed a tracking device on the only vehicle he used for transportation, noting that such tracking is in violation of California law.
