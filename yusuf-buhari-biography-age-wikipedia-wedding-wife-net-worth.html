Yusuf Buhari Biography: Age, Wikipedia, Wedding, Wife &amp; Net Worth

We bring you Yusuf Buhari biography with relevant facts you need to know about President Muhammadu Buhari’s son.

Yusuf Buhari’s wedding became a talk of the town with dignitaries turning up for the colorful event.

Although many don’t know much about the son of Muhammadu Buhari, Yusuf, we have written exclusive information about him.

Yusuf Buhari Biography

Yusuf Buhari, born April 23, is the son of the current Nigerian President, Muhammadu Buhari and the first lady Aisha Buhari.

Yusuf Buhari is originally hails from Katsina State where he was born and raised alongside his siblings.

He is the first and only son of President Muhammadu Buhari surviving eight children.

Yusuf Buhari Age

Yusuf Buhari was born on April 23. He is in his early 30s.

Early Life and Education

Yusuf Buhari was born into a Muslim family in Katsina state, Nigeria. He is the first of President Buhari.

He had his early education in his hometown, Katsina State before he furthered to Kaduna International School and British School of Lome.

Yusuf attended University of Surrey, the United Kingdom where he graduated with a degree.

In 2019, he completed his National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) year.

Yusuf Buhari Net Worth

Yusuf Buhari is the son of President Muhammadu Buhari with an estimated net worth of $1 million.

Yusuf Buhari Wedding

Yusuf Buhari got married to his wife, Zahra Bayero, the daughter of the Emir of Bichi. The marriage took place in Kaduna State.

Yusuf Buhari Wife

Zahra Bayero is the wife of Yusuf Buhari, she is the daughter of the Emir of Bichi.
