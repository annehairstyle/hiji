Hannah Margaret Selleck- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings, Education

Hannah was born on December 16, 1988, in the United States, making her 30 years old. Her zodiac sign is Sagittarius. According to the images on the internet, she has a fit body and takes good care of her appearance, indicating that she invests time and effort into her appearance.

Hannah’s mother is actress Jillie Mack, who has appeared in films such as “Adventures of Nicholas at Roaring Camp,” “The Queen’s Nose,” “Tales from the Darkside,” and “Frasier.” Hannah’s father is the well-known actor Tom Selleck, who was born on January 29, 1945, in Detroit, Michigan, USA. Hannah Margaret Selleck- Professional Career

Hannah reportedly loved horse riding, ice skating, and dancing as a child, and her passion for sports persisted as she grew up, and she went on to become an equestrian. She has competed in a variety of events, including a Silver Team Medal at the Prix de States in 2005 and competing in the Equitation Championship at Capital Challenge in 2006. Hannah is also a jumper, and she has participated at the Grand Prix level as well as in competitions like the Gucci Masters tournament and the Longines Masters in Los Angeles in 2011 and 2015, respectively. She works as a horse breeder as well.

Overall, Hannah’s hard work and talent have allowed her to grow to prominence and acquire more media exposure, and we are confident that she has much more to come in her career.
