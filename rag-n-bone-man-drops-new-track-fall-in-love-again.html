Rag'n'Bone Man Drops New Track &quot;Fall In Love Again&quot;

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, June 10, 2024

As the release of his second studio album Life By Misadventure draws ever closer, Rag’n’Bone Man today (March 23rd) shares another new track set to feature on the record.

Serving as the follow up to All I Ever Wanted, which shot straight to #1 in both the Airplay and iTunes Chart upon its release, Fall In Love Again is a delicate and introspective ballad that lyrically addresses the As You Are singers feelings of apprehension, as he wrestles with his emotions and fear of commitment.

Recorded at Phantom Studios, in Tennessee the beautifully understated track radiates a gentle, yet raw soul-stirring sentiment that permeates straight to the heart, as the 36-year-old sings, “I’ll avoid the conversation/ Say that I need my space, not ’cause I want to / I will make some poor excuse/ Every time that I get close to you.”

Speaking of the track, Rag’n’Bone man says: “‘Fall in Love Again’ is a song Ben Jackson-Cook and I wrote on a boozy afternoon in Leiper’s Fork, Tennessee. He did the melody and I did the lyrics. The song is about sabotage: sabotaging your own relationship for fear of commitment.”

To accompany the tracks release, the multi BRIT Award winner shares with fans a special live performance of the song, filmed at the iconic Abbey Road Studios in London.

Fall in Love Again (Live from Abbey Road)

Life By Misadventure is set for release on April 23rd 2021 via Columbia & is available for pre-order/pre-save here.
