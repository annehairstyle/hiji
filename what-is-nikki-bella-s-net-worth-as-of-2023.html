Stephanie Nicole Garcia-Colace is an American Television star and a retired Professional Wrestler. She is widely known through her wrestling career in WWE during the year 2007-2023 where she performed under the in-ring name Nikki Bella along with her twin sister Brie as the Bella Twins. Nikki started her career as a waitress in Los Angeles and started modeling , acting and promotional work. Nikki made her first television appearance on Fox in a reality show “Meet My Folks” and soon got selected as the WorldCup Twins for Budweiser. The twins then participated in the 2006 WWE Diva Search but did not get selected. Nikki and Brie signed a developmental contract with WWE in 2007 and were said to perform for the Florida Championship Wrestling (FCW). In 2008, Nikki’s twin made her in-ring debut on an episode of SmackDown and defeated Victoria.

This leads to a quick scripted rivalry with Victoria and Natalya, where Brie starts the match and crawls down underneath the ring and Nikki sneaks out to capture the win. Brie and Nikki used this trick to win multiple matches, but were caught after weeks and found to be twins. The twins had then emerged as one of the successful pairs and has featured in multiple segments both in-ring and on the back stage. Nikki Bella has evolved to be one of the top stars of the women’s division both as a face and heel, and has achieved great heights throughout her run.

Some of the accomplishments of Nikki Bella includes, WWE Divas Championship (twice), Guinness World record for the longest WWE Divas Champion reign for 301 days, Ranked No.1 of the top 50 female wrestlers by the PWE in 2015, Diva of the Year and Most improved Wrestler by Rolling Stone in 2015, Choice Female Athlete of the year by Teen Choice awards along with Brie in 2016 and so on. Nikki got inducted into the 2020 Hall of Fame as a part of The Bella Twins. Nikki Bella was not only successful in wrestling, she is equally successful as a television personality. Here is a detailed estimate on the overall net worth of Nikki Bella.
