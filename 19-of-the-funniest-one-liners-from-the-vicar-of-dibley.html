19 of the funniest one-liners from the Vicar of Dibley

Posted by Artie Phelan on Wednesday, June 5, 2024

The Vicar of Dibley was one of Britain's funniest sitcoms - and we're still hoping for more.

The hit BBC comedy followed the life of female vicar Geraldine Granger (Dawn French), an out-going chocoholic who lived and preached in the fictional village of Dibley, Oxfordshire.

Fun fact: the popular series was actually filmed in Buckinghamshire, which (if you've never seen a map) borders Greater London.

The Vicar of Dibley was renowned for closing every episode with Geraldine sharing a naff and rather rude joke with Alice (Emma Chambers) - who usually never got it.

Other characters who became household names include Owen Newitt (played by Only Fools and Horses legend Roger Lloyd Pack), Hugo Horton (James Fleet) and Letitia Cropley (Liz Smith).

Not only did the series boast a whole host (pun intended) of very famous faces, they were also incredibly funny.

So with that said, here's 19 of the funniest one-liners from the Vicar of Dibley.

1. Owen: "I am a great supporter of sex before marriage. Otherwise, I wouldn’t have had sex at all."

2. Geraldine: "I don’t care what the Bible says about girls kissing girls, I’d have snogged her any day."

3. Owen: "I am not a lunatic. I have the psychiatric report to prove it. A slender majority of the panel decided in my favour."

4. David: "You look like an extract in a low-budget remake of a film by Ken Russell."
