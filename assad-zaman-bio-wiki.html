Who is Assad Zaman?

Assad Zaman is a British Actor. Assad is best known for the role of Sathnam in Apple Tree Yard (TV Mini-Series) in 2017, Hasan in Our Girl (TV Series) in 2020, and Rashid in Interview With the Vampire (TV Series) in 2022.

Furthermore, Zaman will appear as Lot in This Is the Night Mail which is in Post-Production.

Assad Zaman: Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, and Education

Assad Zaman was born on 10th May 1990 in Newcastle Upon Tyne, England. On the other hand, He hasn’t disclosed anything about his parents’ or siblings’ identities.

Asides from that, he is of British nationality and holds Caucasian ethnicity.

In addition, moving toward his educational background, Zaman attended the Manchester School of Theater. Later, he graduated in 2013 with a Bachelor of Arts in Acting.

Assad Zaman: Career

Assad Zaman debuted as Tony in Cucumber (TV Mini-Series) in 2015. He played the role of Sathnam in Apple Tree Yard (TV Mini-Series) in 2017. He played the role of Lee Nadella in Vera (TV Series) in 2019.

Moreover, Zaman was cast as Hasan in Our Girl (TV Series) in 2020. In the same year, he played the role of Rohit in The Saint of Southhall (Short). In 2022, he played the role of Anish Sengupta in Hotel Portofino (TV Series).
