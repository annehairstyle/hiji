RHOA: Yovanna Momplaisir Doubles Down On Calling Her Co-Stars Fake Would Like To Be A Full-Time

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, June 13, 2024

Yovanna Momplaisir is making a name for herself both on and off the show. After turning on Nene Leakes, she is sticking with her claims that the ladies of Real Housewives of Atlanta are fake.

While visiting Sister Circle, Yovanna shared her side of the story behind that explosive scene on RHOA. The argument escalated once Momplaisir told Porsha Williams that they aren’t her friend because they’re all fake!

She is doubling down on her claims that the cast is ‘foo foo.’

‘The women on that show? Absolutely [are foo foo]. Y’all watching the same show I’m watching? I’ve watched these women for quite some time now and I just see how they engage with each other. One moment you guys are cool and the next you’re going below the belt and saying the nastiest things to one another, behind each other’s back mind you, then the next they’re cool. That’s just not how I operate with friends, so that’s why I call them foo foo.’

Regardless of her feelings about them being phony, she would accept a peach-holding position if Bravo was to offer her one.

‘I’m open if the paycheck is right.’

https://www.instagram.com/p/B7TWvbvhIiD/

This comes after the CAU alum made a video discussing the dynamics of the Atlanta group of women. She told viewers that they treat Nene differently while easily forgiving one another.

‘It’s crazy that they can have an issue or an argument, something serious. That’s what happened—within an hour they were over it and it’s cool. But why did it take y’all six months to even talk back to NeNe? Was it considered worse? No, it’s just nonsense.’

Yovanna went on to say: ‘I feel like these girls play by two different set of rules. They’ve got a set of rules for how they do things but then certain girls in the group don’t apply to those same rules.’
