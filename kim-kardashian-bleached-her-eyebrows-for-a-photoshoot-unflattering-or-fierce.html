Kim Kardashian bleached her eyebrows (for a photoshoot): unflattering or fierce?

Posted by Billy Koelling on Friday, June 21, 2024

Of all of the stupid crap done for attention, I’ve never understood the no-eyebrow look. Like, I would never shave my head for attention or post really revealing selfies on my social media, but I have some understanding of why people do those things and where those urges come from. I don’t understand the shaved/bleached eyebrow thing at all, and there are SO MANY famewhores doing it these days. Miley did it. Katy Perry did it. Noomi Rapace did it (she wasn’t a famewhore about it though). Lady Gaga did it. And now Kim Kardashian did it.

These are Instagram photos posted by Kim. They were taken Monday night at Kendall Jenner’s 19th birthday party, and Kim posted them yesterday. In the two posted photos, Kim is posing with Cara Delevingne – Cara and Kim hung out during Paris Fashion Week this year and they’re probably posing together for some kind of editorial project or magazine cover. Kim posted one photo with the hashtags: #KendallsBDayParty #DontBeScaredOfMyBleachedBrows #ItsForAPhotoShoot. So maybe Cara and Kim reunited for more editorial work? The thing is… Cara doesn’t need Kim for any kind of magazine work. Cara gets her own work. She’s an It Girl and all of the magazines want her. Cara is hanging out with Kim because she actually likes Kim. Weird, right?

Anyway, the bleached brows… always a terrible look. But at least it’s just for a photo shoot. I’m assuming Kim will go back to dark brows in a day or two.

Photos courtesy of Kim’s Instagram.
