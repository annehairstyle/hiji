Sandra De La Vega - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Kelle Repass on Sunday, June 2, 2024

Entrepreneur and influencer known as the wife of soccer star Andrés Guardado. She served as president of the Andrés Guardado Foundation, which raises funds for young people in shelters in Guadalajara. She has amassed more than 290,000 followers on her sandradlv Instagram account.

Before Fame

She was a model. In 2011, she met her husband at a party in Guadalajara, Mexico. 

Trivia

In 2022, she traveled to Doha, Qatar to watch the FIFA World Cup. Her Instagram page is a hub for photos and videos from her personal life, including some moments with her family.

Family Life

She and her husband have children named Maximo and Catalina.

Associated With

She and Sasha Attwood both went to Qatar to cheer on their soccer-playing partners in 2022.
