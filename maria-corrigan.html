Maria Corrigan is an American model, actress, and Instagram star who is best known as the wife of Canadian actor and model, Nick Bateman.

Her Married Life and Husband

She is married to her handsome husband, Nick Bateman. The couple were dating before they tied the knot.

After being together in a romantic affair for more than a decade, the couple finally decided to exchange the wedding vows on July 17, 2019.

The love-struck couple got hitched on their 11th anniversary at Calamigos Ranch in Malibu. 

The wedding ceremony was an intimate one attended by close family and friends. 

It was a dream come true for both husband and wife. The bride looked gorgeous in a white gown while the groom wore a black tuxedo.
