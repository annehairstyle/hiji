Dynamo (Magician) first learned about magic when he was still in school. According to his biography, other kids constantly bullied him so his grandfather taught him the first magic trick.

Occupation and Career

After moving to London from Wyke, he created a first-ever magic mix-tape in 2004. The filming was done in the streets of London and backstage at events.

He quickly gained momentum by posting those footages on YouTube. Later, he featured celebrities like Ian Brown, Mike Skinner, and Snoop Dogg and shot into stardom.

Hollywood A-list celebrities like Will Smith, P Diddy, Jay Z, Coldplay, Russell Brand, Gwyneth Paltrow and The Black Eyed Peas have acknowledged his talent.

Even the billionaire Richard Branson bowed down to him after watching one of his performances.
