Soulja Boy, Blac Chyna, And Summer Bunni Are In A Love Triangle That Will Reportedly Play Out On

Posted by Billy Koelling on Thursday, June 6, 2024

This actually makes a lot of sense. Soulja Boy, Blac Chyna, and Summer Bunni are all involved in one love triangle that is reportedly coming to a TV near you very soon.

After taking a break from ‘Love and Hip Hop Hollywood’ to work on his business ventures, Soulja Boy is back and is bringing along a big name for a storyline everyone will want to watch.

Soulja Boy and Blac Chyna seemed to be the latest It couple when their romance hit Instagram.

The rapper, who ended things with longtime girlfriend Nia Riley after attending Marriage Boot Camp, and the model who had just split with Kid Buu after a rumored physical altercation were apparently just trying to make Tyga jealous when they first got together.

The ploy allegedly turned into a full-blown romance. However, Soulja was seen with Summer Bunni shortly after kicking it with Chyna. A source says that the three are filming for ‘Love and Hip Hop Hollywood.’

The Summer Bunni relationship had been going on since the beginning of the year leaving the aspiring female rapper to be upset by the news that he has his eyes set on Chyna.

Hollywood Life reported that Bunni’s manager, Antonia Velez, got her the gig with VH1.

They also report: “Soulja Boy had been dating Summer Bunni since the first week of January this year on the low since he wants to keep promoting his relationship with Chyna to upset Tyga.”

Summer isn’t so innocent herself. She rose to fame when it was revealed last year after it was revealed that she was seeing Cardi B’s serial cheating husband, Offset.

She’s used her newfound fame to release music and now get on the Hollywood scene at a bigger scale. Now it seems she will be making her way to ‘LHH.’
