Edward Barber is a Filipino-British actor, TV host, and singer who had come into the spotlight in March 2017 when he had stood 4th in Pinoy Big Brother: Lucky 7, the 7th main season (and the 13th season overall) of Pinoy Big Brother, the Philippine version of the globally popular Big Brother reality game show franchise. He has acted in films such as Loving in Tandem (2017), Da One That Ghost Away (2018), Fantastica (2018), and Princess DayaReese (2021). He has also portrayed characters such as Collin Domingo in the horror-fantasy drama TV series La Luna Sangre (2017–2018), Jake in the legal drama anthology TV show Ipaglaban Mo! (2019), and Francisco ‘Iking’ Castro in the drama TV series Hiwaga ng Kambat (2019). He has also published a solo studio album titled #E0806 (April 2019).

Education

Edward had attended the European School, Karlsruhe (ESK), one of the 3 European Schools (an international school emphasizing a multilingual and multicultural pedagogical approach to teaching) in Germany and one of the 13 such schools across the European Union (a political and economic union of 27 member states that are located primarily in Europe).

He had put his education on hold when he had joined the reality TV show Pinoy Big Brother: Lucky 7 in mid-2016. He has stated that he would like to resume his studies and pursue courses in film, literature, or psychology.
