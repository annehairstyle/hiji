Early Life and Education

Gladwell was born on September 3, 1963, in Fareham, Hampshire, England, to a Jamaican mother and an English father. Career

After graduating from the University of Toronto, Gladwell worked as a reporter for The Washington Post. In 1996, he started working for The New Yorker, where he is still a staff writer.
