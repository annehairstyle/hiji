Drake 21-yeard old fan reveals private messages he sent after throwing the 36G bra onstage in New Yo

Posted by Kelle Repass on Monday, June 24, 2024

Veronica Correira is the 21-year-old Drake fan who threw the famous 36G bra on stage at the Barclays Center in Brooklyn NY and who became an overnight online sensation after Champagne Papi picked it up and asked to know who threw the underwear after he was surprised by its size.

Right after Drake's reaction, the video became viral and Veronica made another TikTok video owning her actions and right away she also went viral.

Veronica Correira got a Playboy deal days after the concert

After the attention she got, Playboy Magazine reached out to Veronica to offer her a modeling deal on their private platform, and this got her even more attention.

Veronica Correira is a Rhode Island native who just opened up her first coffee shop in RI and is trying to navigate this situation to her benefit, as she should.

Recently, Correrira attended the Club Ambition podcast to reveal that Drake got in touch with her after the Playboy deal and shared some good advice.

Drake gave fame advice to Veronica

"He slid up on a Story," Veronica Correia told Club Ambition host, " just simply sent a laughing face. I took a day or two to respond because I didn't know what to say,"

"I wrote out this paragraph basically just thanking him for the experience, like an awesome show," Veronica added, "Then I told him that I own a coffee shop in Cumberland and asked him if he likes coffee."

While talking to Drake, Veronica discovered her favorite artist's coffee preference "iced and sweet."

"He was laughing at all my messages, so I asked him, 'Oh do you think I'm funny?' and he said, 'I think you're really sought-after right now.'" She added.

After that, Veronica asked for Drake's advice on her newfound fame and the negativity that comes with it, "He said just to live your amazing life, like, people are faceless and they would never really say the negative comments to your face,"

"I'm like, yeah, wow, now I'm definitely not paying them any attention," Veronica concluded.
