Amanza Smith Health Updates

Amanza Smith faced a series of health challenges recently. She initially had a cancer scare during Selling Sunset season 6 but thankfully received benign results after a biopsy. Later, in May, she underwent two invasive spinal surgeries due to osteomyelitis, a potentially life-threatening bone infection.

Amanza was in the hospital for nearly two months and returned home in July, facing a six-month recovery period involving rest, antibiotics, and physical therapy. She expressed her eagerness to resume her passions like decorating but noted her current limitations. Her journey has garnered support from well-wishers.

Amanza Smith Personal Life

Amanza has faced challenges in her personal life, including her former marriage to NFL player Ralph Brown, which ended in divorce in 2012. Brown went missing in 2019, and his whereabouts remain unknown, leaving Amanza and their two children, son Braker and daughter Noah, in a difficult situation.

Amanza has claimed that Brown hasn’t paid child support, and her attempts to contact his family have been met with resistance. In 2021, an LA court awarded her full custody of their children, and Brown was not granted visitation rights, highlighting her role as a single mother and her discussions about balancing work and family on the show.
