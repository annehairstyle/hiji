Israel Set to Release Uncensored Bodycam Footage of Hamas Murders to Demonstrate the Grave

Posted by Kelle Repass on Saturday, June 29, 2024

Israel is prepared to unveil unedited, uncensored footage capturing Hamas’ relentless attacks on unsuspecting civilians. This move aims to counter what Israeli authorities perceive as a deliberate denial of the atrocities committed by Hamas, akin to the denial of the Holocaust.

 

 

Last night, government spokesman Eylon Levy informed the press that unaltered clips from the bodycams of deceased Hamas assailants would be presented to the media. This unveiling is intended to depict the ‘brutality inflicted’ upon Israelis.

 

 

This development is a response to the global outcry regarding Israel’s airstrikes on the Gaza Strip, which are reported to have resulted in the deaths of over 5,000 Palestinians since October 7.

 

 

Footage screenings were held for journalists in Tel Aviv this morning, showcasing what Israel asserts as proof of Hamas’ rocket launch sites strategically positioned within civilian infrastructure.

 

 

Israeli Defense Forces (IDF) websites and social media accounts have shared satellite images purporting to reveal these launch sites near mosques, schools, and nurseries, supporting Israel’s claims of Hamas exploiting civilians as human shields.

 

 

Allegedly, two of the identified launch sites were in the vicinity of a mosque and a kindergarten, while another was situated across from a UN building and yet another was located by a boys’ secondary school.

 

 

Geolocation analysis by the IDF confirmed the accuracy of these images, with analysts noting the recent construction of these sites, as previous satellite images from September did not depict their presence.

 

 

IDF spokesman Daniel Hagari emphasized Hamas’ deliberate use of civilian sites, including kindergartens, schools, and mosques, for launching attacks on Israel.

 

 

It remains unverified whether the purported rocket launch sites at these disclosed locations are indeed present.

 

 

According to Gaza’s Hamas-run health ministry, the recent Israeli bombings have led to the deaths of over 5,000 individuals within the besieged Palestinian territory.
