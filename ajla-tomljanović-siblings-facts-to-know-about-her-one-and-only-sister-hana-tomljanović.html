Ajla Tomljanovi Siblings- Facts To Know About Her One And Only Sister Hana Tomljanovi

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, June 17, 2024

Ajla Tomljanovi is an Australian professional tennis player who has won four ITF Circuit singles and three doubles titles.

Ajla previously represented Croatia in 2014. She began representing Australia in the 2014 US Open after receiving permanent residency in Australia.

She had to compete as a Croatian for the next four years before being granted Australian citizenship in January 2018, which allowed her to represent her country at all WTA Tour events.

Likewise, she has won three doubles and four singles on the ITF Circuit. On February 21, 2022, she climbed as high as No. 38 in the world during her singles career. On January 5, 2015, she rose as high as No. 47 in the doubles rankings.

Moreover, Tomljanovic was a talented young player who won the Australian Open girls’ doubles title with Christina McHale in 2009. She achieved a junior world No. 4 rating on March 30, 2009, a combined career high.

Ajla Tomljanović Siblings Include Her One And Only Sister, Hana Tomljanović

Ajila has an older sister named Hanna. She played tennis for the University of Virginia.

On the other hand, her older sibling Hana has also shared many photos with her sister. They can be seen together in a picture she posted on November 28, 2021, wearing a fancy gown. The post shows that the sister has a strong bond.

Furthermore, on June 25, 2015, Hana and Ajla attended the WTA Pre-Wimbledon Party at Kensington Roof Gardens in London, England.
