Niall Horan Announces Canada and US 2018 Tour Dates

Posted by Artie Phelan on Monday, June 24, 2024

Following last night’s release of his new single, “Too Much To Ask,” Niall Horan has finally announced the Canadian and U.S. dates of his 2018 world tour, The Flicker World Tour.

Niall Horan has finally announced some dates for his 2018 world tour. The dates, which are currently only Canada and the United States, were preceded by some other major announcements by the singer.

Just yesterday, September 14th, Horan announced the release date and cover art for his debut album: Flicker. Just hours after that, however, he also announced that his new single, “Too Much To Ask,” was being released at midnight EST.

And now, a mere ten hours post single-release, he has announced some very anticipated tour dates.

US and Canada . The Flicker world tour is coming to you summer / autumn 2018. https://t.co/pKtwFiWBSE pic.twitter.com/MJOT8xCszn

— Niall Horan (@NiallOfficial) September 15, 2017

The announcement of these dates is also linked to the Ticketmaster website, where fans can sign up to receive codes for the fan pre-sale. This method of ticket distribution is very popular amongst artists, so we’re happy to see Niall doing his best to stop ticket scalpers.

Each of the shows will also include Maren Morris as the special guest, too. We’re very excited to see her on the road with Niall.

Tickets for Niall’s fan presale will be released September 19th @ 10 a.m. local time.

Flicker will be released on October 20th.

“Too Much To Ask” is available on streaming services everywhere now. 

Can't wait to get back on the road & play this album for you. And… my good friend the incredible @MarenMorris is joining me for all of it

— Niall Horan (@NiallOfficial) September 15, 2017
