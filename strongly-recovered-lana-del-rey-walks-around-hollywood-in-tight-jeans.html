Strongly recovered Lana Del Rey walks around Hollywood in tight jeans

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, June 24, 2024

Strongly recovered Lana Del Rey walks around Hollywood in tight jeans. And the singer is not at all shy about her rounded shapes. Tthe paparazzi filmed 36-year-old Lana Del Rey while shopping at Melrose Place. The star was accompanied by a friend with whom they were very similarly dressed: both in light skinny jeans and white t-shirts with a deep neckline.

Lana looked cheerful and happy; she didn’t even hide behind big sunglasses. It seems that the singer has already come to terms with the extra pounds which she gained during the quarantine and which she still cannot part with.

For shopping, Elizabeth Woolridge Grant – that’s what Lana Del Rey’s real name is – did a minimalist make-up (a little mascara on her eyelashes, blush on her cheekbones, and gloss on her lips ), and her hair was collected in her signature bun on top. And by the way, she is true to her favorite red lacquer, with which both manicure and pedicure were performed.
