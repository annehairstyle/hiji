What Is Rihannas Zodiac Sign?

Posted by Billy Koelling on Tuesday, June 11, 2024

It is no secret that Rihanna has a big personality and a big heart, which could all be linked to her zodiac sign. 

Rihanna’s zodiac sign is Pisces. Pisces is the fish and although it covers people born between mid-February and mid-March it is the twelfth sign. Pisces are known for being creative, empathetic, and generous. Rihanna has shown her creativity through her music and her empathy and generosity through her charitable work.

You can read more about the mythology surrounding the Pisces zodiac sign, the traits of a Pisces, and how Rihanna shows her Pisces traits below.

Pisces in Mythology & Religion

The pisces sign has been linked to a lot of mythological and religious figures throughout history. The word pisces is literally the latin word for a fish. This zodiac sign is a drawing of two fish tied together most often in an oval shape. This zodiac sign is one of the oldest ones in the world and has been found drawn on Egyptian sarcophagus lids. 

In Greek Mythology, Pisces is thought to be Aphrodite and her son, Eros. The myth goes that Typhon, the father of all monsters was about to attack the gods when Pan (another Greek god) arrived to warn everyone. In order to escape, Aphrodite and Eros both turned into fish and jumped into the river to escape. The sign has also been linked to Typhon as he was a large water serpent god and of course to Poseidon the God of the Seas.

In religion, the sign of pisces has been linked to Jesus Christ. The sign that is widely used by Christians to show their faith in Christ is a fish. The twelve disciples or apostles of Christ were known as the fishers of men. Other religious figures that are associated with Pisces are the Hindu God Vishnu and the ancient Sumerian Goddess Inanna.

Pisces Zodiac Traits

As mentioned above, the three main positive traits of the Pisces horoscope sign are creativity, empathy, and generosity. People with this zodiac sign tend to be so creative that they aren’t restricted by logic and would rather let ideas flow. They are known to be very in tune with other people’s emotions and will often put others’ needs above their own.

On the flip side, Pisces can be overly emotional sometimes and almost too trusting of other people. They can also be closed off to other people when they are in a situation where they need help. According to this Cosmopolitan article, Pisces like water activities, dancing, “being witchy”, writing, and partying.

How Rihanna Shows Her Pisces Side

Rihanna has shown all of the sides of the fish sign over the years. She has shown her more creative side to the public since the release of her 2009 album Rated R. On that album she was credited with not only vocals, but production and art direction. It was the same with her 2010 album Loud, where she was credited with design, creative director, and art director. Through directing music videos, like the one for her song Pour It Up, and collaborating with companies like Fendi to design a bag, she really shows that Pisces creativity.

She has also shown that she is empathetic and generous through the founding of her non-profit Clara Lionel Foundation. The foundation has donated millions of dollars towards causes like climate change, education, disaster relief, HIV/AIDS, and cancer research.

Riri has also shown that overly emotional side of Pisces. Take for example the time in 2016 while performing the song Love the Way You Lie in concert in Dublin. She became so overwhelmed by fans singing the song along with her that she couldn’t continue the song.
