Romane Jolly plays the role of a high school student in the French series ‘Runaway’. Is Romane really a teenager in real life as well? What is her age?

Romane Jolly is a French actress. She is gaining popularity due to her character in Runaway.

In September 2021, Jolly was announced as one of the cast members of Fugueuse: prostitute for love (translated Runaway in English). It is a French drama show that is being broadcast on TF1.

Romane portrays the role of Léa. Léa is a high school student. Long story short, her destiny changes after meeting an older man. She maintains a relationship with that older man.

Léa is passionate about dancing but ends up becoming a prostitue. This series is exciting and thrilling.
