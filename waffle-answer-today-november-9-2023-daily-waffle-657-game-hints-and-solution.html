How to Play Waffle - Daily Word Game?

In the Word Waffle game, you have a board with letters arranged randomly, like a waffle. The letters can be three different colors:

  • Green: This means the letter is in the right place. Great job!

  • Yellow: This means the letter is in the word, but it's in the wrong spot. You need to move it.

  • Grey: This means the letter is not in the word at all. You need to find a different letter.

    • Your goal is to create six correct words using these letters, and you can only make up to 15 moves. To get started, just tap a letter and swap it with another one on the board. The colors of the letters will change to help you figure out if you're on the right track.

    To win the game, you need to make all the letters on the waffle board turn green. You have to do this in 15 moves or less, so try to use as few moves as possible.

    The game board looks like a waffle, and random words are placed on it. You need to drag the letters to form the right words, both horizontally and vertically, as quickly as you can. The goal is to "bake" the waffle by getting all the letters in the right places.

    Remember, each waffle can be solved in 10 moves or fewer. Good luck and enjoy the game!
