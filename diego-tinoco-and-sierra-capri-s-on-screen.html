Diego Tinoco And Sierra Capris On-screen

Posted by Billy Koelling on Thursday, June 20, 2024

Diego Tinoco and Sierra Capri’s chemistry on the television show On My Block led to rumors that they would date in real life. The reality, however, differs greatly from the drama presented on TV. Tinoco claimed to be single and totally dedicated to his professional career.

The Hollywood hottie attracted a lot of attention when he first starred in the supernatural hit series Teen Wolf in season 6. However, his breakthrough performance was in the Spanish-language Netflix series On My Block as Cesar Diaz.

In the series, Sierra Capri’s character Monse serves as his love interest. Because of how closely their characters interacted, many people assumed that they were actually dating. Sadly, Distractify claims that the couple was not dating at the time the cameras ceased recording.

However, as friends, they have a great lot of respect and love for one another. In an interview, Tinoco said that Capri was the best movie companion he could have asked for. Working with her, he claimed, had helped him feel more at ease in front of the camera.

When the romances between their characters ended in the second season of the show, Tinoco and his co-star were both devastated. The actor Notebook Style said he would support Cesar and Monse continuing their relationship and having a happy ending.

I believe I would want to see them live happily ever after together, just like in The Notebook. The actress agreed with the sentiment and went even further in her expression of the same opinions. She said she wanted to watch them grow as people outside of their relationship. She wanted them to get married and have kids together.

Diego Tinoco is putting his efforts toward developing his career

Despite rumors to the contrary, the California native appears to be single at the moment. His remarks suggest that he is just getting started in his acting career. As a result, the talented actor plans to stay away from romances so that he can focus on his budding career in Hollywood. He intends to spend time with his loved ones and pals.

However, he acknowledged in a Seventeen interview that his favorite celebrity was the actress Ana De Armas. He stated his intention to someday work with her. The Netflix star admitted that while De Armas was his first love, Ariana Grande was actually his first crush.

Tinoco’s Debut Movie

Tinoco is currently concentrating on his career. His first movie, R#J, has been signed. Romeo and Juliet, a timeless classic by Shakespeare, is told in the movie in a contemporary setting. It was produced by Timur Bekmambetov and Igor Today, with Carey Williams directing. The On My Block actor shared his excitement for the movie on Instagram in December 2020.
