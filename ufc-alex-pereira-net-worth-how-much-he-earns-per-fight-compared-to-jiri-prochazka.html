UFC: Alex Pereira Net Worth: How much he earns per fight compared to Jiri Prochazka?

Posted by Billy Koelling on Tuesday, June 18, 2024

Brazilian Light Heavyweight Alex Pereira has a short-lived career in the UFC that has already catapulted him to be amongst the highest-paid athletes in the entire sport. Although he still hasn't reached the $1 million mark from base salary and PPV buys alone, winning his upcoming UFC 295 fight against Jiri Prochazka will definitely get him through that threshold. In a previous piece, we established how much of the purse he is getting for tonight's fight. But let's talk about his entire career earnings and total net worth. Pereira has needed only a few fights in order to make an impression and become one of the most entertaining fighters in the UFC today. His fights against Israel Adesanya have been nothing short of spectacular.

How much has Alex Pereira earned as a fighter?

After a long career in the Glory Kickboxing championship, Alex Pereira won two belts and was finally ready to make the transition to the UFC. He always knew tht if he wanted to make a real name for himself, going to the UFC was one of his only options. At 36 years old, Pereira was definitely a late bloomer in the company as he only got his first fight in 2021. But since he made it to the UFC, his progression has been truly impressive. Pereira started his run in the Middleweight division, where he knocked Andreas Michalidis out at UFC 261 and earned himself $154,000 His next fight was against compatrot Bruno Silva and earned $104,000 for his victory.

Not long after that, he earned $244,000 at UFC 276 and knocked Sean Strickland out in the process. 'Poatan' was then ready to fight Israel Adesanya for the title, the same fighter with whom he developed a rivarly during his kickboxing days. Alex won the UFC Middleweight title at UFC 281, which earned him $682,000. For the rematch at UFC 287, 'Poatan lost the belt but won his biggest payday to date at $725,000. However, he ended up earning $1,067,000 in total once you include sponsorship earnings. His most recent payday was against Jan Blachowicz at UFC 291 in the Light Heavyweight division. For that fight, he earned $455,000. Between all his career earnings, Pereira has earned an estimated $5,3 million. Although his net worth is $2 million, according to theufcnews.com.
