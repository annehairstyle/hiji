11 Chic Plus-Size Outfits That Will Level-Up Your Fall Vibe

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, June 9, 2024

Though recent temperatures have fluctuated more than a heart monitor, fall is far from over. And in Fashion Girl Math, this equates to more opportunities to don the season’s snazziest trends (and may possibly warrant a shopping spree or two).

Thankfully, 2023’s remaining months are primed for sartorial peacocking. This season is all about exuding your brightest, most confident self in bold hues, fun prints, and elevated silhouettes.

Like clockwork, academia staples (read: tartan plaid and traditional prep school sensibilities) make their highly-anticipated, seasonal return. Nicola Coughlan and Beanie Feldstein are both fans of the boarding school look, taking the nostalgic style into cool-girl territory.

Apart from fall-worthy patterns, color will also tint wardrobes this season via bright, vivid suits or neutral garments with accent tones.

One hue, in particular, has effortlessly maintained its hold on the zeitgeist — I’m, of course, talking about pink. The vivacious tint continues to be a go-to for celebs and designers alike, taking the Barbiecore trend well through the end of the year.

Ahead, check out some plus-size fall outfits to add to your Pinterest board ASAP, styled by some of the most fabulous, curvy tastemakers in the game.
