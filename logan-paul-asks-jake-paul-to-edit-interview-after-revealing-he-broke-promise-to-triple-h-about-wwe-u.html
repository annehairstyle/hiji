Logan Paul asks Jake Paul to edit interview after revealing he broke promise to Triple H about WWE U

Posted by Artie Phelan on Friday, June 21, 2024

New WWE US champion Logan Paul told brother Jake to cut his latest YouTube video - after breaking a promise to Triple H.

The YouTube sensation, who recently announced he has retired from boxing, will focus his energy on the WWE in the future.

After beating Rey Mysterio earlier in November, Paul landed the WWE US title but has already broken a promise to head of creative for WWE Triple H - by having sex wearing the belt.

In a video with his brother and fellow YouTube star Jake, the two siblings were joking around before he made the revelation.

It led to Logan to tell his brother to cut that out of the video, which subsequently remained and was later published.

Speaking to brother Jake on YouTube, the elder Paul brother said: "I don't think you understand. I just took a shower with this belt on.

"I swear, Triple H. He came up to me and said, 'Logan, congratulations on the belt. One thing we've been really cautious about here in the organisation is making sure our athletes are not f***ing in their belts. So if you could just refrain from that, that'd be great.'
