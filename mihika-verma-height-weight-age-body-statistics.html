    Mihika Verma Facts

  • She grew up in Mumbai.
  • In 2004, Mihika Verma won the Miss India International title.
  • She also represented India in the Miss International 2004 competition.
  • She made her TV show debut by portraying the character of Shreya Raisinghania/Shreya Siddharth Chopra in the Hindi language soap opera, Virrudh, from 2007 to 2008.
  • In 2013, Mihika Verma started playing the role of Mihika Iyer, Madhavi and Vishwa’s daughter, Ishita’s sister, and Mihir’s former girlfriend, in the television drama series, Yeh Hai Mohabbatein (transl. This is Love), starring Divyanka Tripathi Dahiya and Karan Patel.
  • She also appeared as Fiza, a young actress who soon finds herself in a downward spiral, in the television romance anthology series, Yeh Hai Aashiqui, in the year 2013.
  • Mihika Verma made her first theatrical film appearance in the 2019 Hindi-language comedy-drama film, Fraud Saiyaan, whose lead cast comprised of Arshad Warsi, Sara Loren, Flora Saini, Saurabh Shukla, Nivedita Tiwari, and Peeyush Suhaney.
  • She has also garnered a huge social media fan base with more than 500k followers on Instagram.
