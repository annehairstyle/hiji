New Music Friday: A Global Celebration of Fresh Sounds

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, June 20, 2024

In the ever-evolving world of music, Fridays have become synonymous with releasing new tracks and albums from artists across the globe. This week, we’re highlighting various genres and styles, from Nigerian Afrobeats to South African Amapiano and everything in between. Let’s dive into the latest releases from Darkoo x Omah Lay, Kizz Daniel, Chike x Mohbad, Baby Boy AV, Zinoleesky, Zerrydl, Yemi Alade, Barry Jhay, Stefflon Don featuring Victony, Dremo, Azanti & PsychoYP, and Teejay and Davido.

Darkoo x Omah Lay – Whiskey: The British-Nigerian singer and rapper Darkoo has teamed up with Nigerian singer-songwriter Omah Lay for a smooth and soulful Whiskey. The song combines R&B, hip-hop, and Afrobeat elements to create a unique sound that will resonate with listeners.

Kizz Daniel – Twe Twe, Too Busy to Be Bae: Nigerian singer-songwriter Kizz Daniel has released two new tracks, Twe Twe” and “Too Busy to Be Bae. Both songs showcase Kizz Daniel‘s signature blend of Afrobeats and pop, with catchy hooks and infectious beats that will get you moving.

Chike x Mohbad – Egwu: Nigerian singer-songwriter Chike has joined forces with rising star Mohbad for a high-energy track called Egwu. The song fuses hip-hop, Afrobeats, and dancehall elements to create a vibrant and infectious sound perfect for the dance floor.

Baby Boy AV – Kupe: Nigerian singer-songwriter and producer Baby Boy AV has released a new track called Kupe, which showcases his unique blend of Afro-pop and R&B. The song features a catchy melody, smooth vocals, and a laid-back groove, perfect for a chill evening.

Zinoleesky – Sakara: Nigerian singer-songwriter Zinoleesky has dropped a new track called Sakara, which features his signature blend of Afrobeats and hip-hop. The song’s infectious beat and catchy hook make it a must-listen for fans of the genre.

Zerrydl – Danger Zee EP: Nigerian singer-songwriter Zerrydl has released a new EP called Danger Zee, which features tracks showcasing his unique blend of Afrobeats and R&B. The EP is a must-listen for genre fans and will leave a lasting impression.

Yemi Alade – Mamapiano EP: Nigerian singer-songwriter Yemi Alade has released a new EP called Mamapiano, which features a collection of tracks that showcase her unique blend of Afrobeats and South African Amapiano. The EP is a must-listen for genre fans and will leave a lasting impression.

Barry Jhay – Mo: Nigerian singer-songwriter Barry Jhay has released a new track called “Mo,” which features his signature blend of Afrobeats and R&B. The song’s infectious beat and catchy hook make it a must-listen for fans of the genre.

Stefflon Don Ft. Victony – Deadly: British Jamaican rapper and singer Stefflon Don has teamed up with Nigerian singer-songwriter Victony for a new track called Deadly. The song combines Afrobeats, hip-hop, and dancehall elements to create a unique sound that will resonate with listeners.

Dremo – Shey Dey Give: Nigerian rapper and songwriter Dremo has released a new track called Shey Dey Give, which showcases his unique blend of hip-hop and Afrobeats. The song’s hard-hitting lyrics and infectious beat make it a must-listen for fans of the genre.

Azanti & PsychoYP – Clear Road: Nigerian singer-songwriter Azanti and rapper PsychoYP have teamed up for a new track called Clear Road. The song combines hip-hop, R&B, and Afrobeats to create a unique sound that will resonate with listeners.

Teejay and Davido – Drift (Remix): Jamaican singer-songwriter Teejay and Nigerian singer-songwriter Davido have teamed up for a remix of Teejay‘s hit song Drift. The track combines dancehall, Afrobeats, and R&B elements to create a vibrant and infectious sound that will get you moving.

This week’s New Music Friday celebrates the diverse and vibrant sounds of the global music scene. From Nigeria to South Africa and from the UK to Jamaica, artists are pushing the boundaries of their genres and creating fresh and exciting music that will leave a lasting impression.
