Jeanne Ryan Death: Duluth Beloved Radio Personality unexpectedly Passed Away

Posted by Billy Koelling on Thursday, June 13, 2024

In a profound loss for the radio broadcasting community, Jeanne Ryan, a seasoned radio personality with a career spanning over two decades, passed away on Thursday, December 14. Known for her unique voice and humorous wit, Ryan was a beloved figure in Duluth, Minnesota, and beyond.

Who was Jeanne Ryan?

Jeanne Ryan was not just a radio personality; she was a voice that greeted listeners every morning with warmth and humor. At 59 years old, she had spent more than two decades honing her craft and creating a bond with her audience that few could match.

Ryan was most well-known for her distinctive, rich voice and her offbeat sense of humor, both of which made her an endearing presence on the airwaves. Her career began in 2000 in the Duluth-Superior market when she joined 92.1 The Beat, but it was at Mix 108 in Duluth where she truly made her mark.

A Flourishing Career at Mix 108

Ryan’s most rewarding stint came at Mix 108, where she served as the host of the popular morning show for an impressive fifteen years. During her tenure, Ryan guided listeners through their mornings with her engaging personality and infectious laughter, making the station one of the most popular music stations in the United States.

Her ability to connect with her audience made her a beloved figure in the community and an irreplaceable part of the Mix 108 family. Her passion for radio and dedication to her listeners were evident in every broadcast, making her a staple of Duluth’s morning routine.

What was the Cause of Jeanne Ryan Death?

Jeanne Ryan passed away from natural causes, as reported by her family. Her death is a significant loss to the radio broadcasting industry and to the Duluth community where she had become a beloved figure.

Ryan’s family has yet to release an official obituary. As we await further details, it’s clear that her legacy will live on through the countless lives she touched with her unique voice and vivacious personality.
