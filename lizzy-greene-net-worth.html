Lizzy Greene’s net worth stands at $1 million

Lizzy Greene – A Brief Biography

Lizzy Anne Greene, born on May 1, 2003, in Dallas, Texas, USA, is a prominent actress, widely recognized for her portrayal of Dawn Harper in the popular television series “Nicky, Ricky, Dicky & Dawn” aired on Nickelodeon. Her exceptional performance led to nominations for two Kids’ Choice and Young Artist Awards. With her career commencing in 2014, Lizzy Greene has been an active figure in the entertainment industry.

As for Lizzy Greene’s net worth, it is estimated that her wealth amounts to $1 million as of early 2018. TV and film have been the primary sources contributing to Greene’s fortune.

Lizzy Greene’s Estimated Net Worth: $1 million

Lizzy was raised in Dallas and received her education at Fun House Theatre and Film School.

Greene made her debut on the small screen in the comedy series “Nicky, Ricky, Dicky & Dawn” (2014 – present), which was created by Matt Fleckenstein and aired on Nickelodeon. In this series, she portrays Dawn, the oldest sibling among quadruplets. Due to her remarkable role, the actress was nominated for a Young Artist Award, and she was also nominated twice for Kids’ Choice Awards in the category of Favorite TV Actress. Consequently, this show has significantly contributed to Lizzy Greene’s net worth.

Additionally, Lizzy played Young Nicole in the TV movie “Damaged Goods” (2014) and also had a guest appearance in an episode of the series “The Thundermans” (2014), also broadcast on Nickelodeon. In 2015, she starred in the television special “Nickelodeon’s Ho Ho Holiday” and the following year appeared in the television film “Nickelodeon’s Ultimate Halloween Costume Party”. Further adding to her net worth, she participated in the reality competition series “Paradise Run” in 2016.

In 2017, Lizzy appeared as herself in several television specials, including “Nickelodeon’s Not So Valentine’s Special”, “Nickelodeon’s Sizzling Summer Camp Special”, and “Nickelodeon’s Ultimate Halloween Haunted House”. In the same year, she portrayed Barkley in the television film “Tiny Christmas”.

In conclusion, all the aforementioned endeavors have undoubtedly contributed to the growth of Lizzy Greene’s net worth.

Looking at her personal life, at the young age of 14, Lizzy Greene is still single. She is an active user of various social networking sites, including Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram. On the latter, she has amassed over 2.2 million followers. Additionally, she runs a YouTube channel under the name “Lizzy Greene”, which has garnered over 232,000 subscribers and more than nine million views.
