Dog The Bounty Hunter Reveals If He Will Marry Again After Losing Wife Beth Chapman

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, June 21, 2024

Dog the Bounty Hunter has revealed whether or not he will marry again after losing his beloved wife, Beth Chapman.

It has barely been two months since Duane Chapman lost the love of his life. Beth passed away at the end of June after battling throat and lung cancer. The couple spent decades together hunting down the bad guys on reality TV shows like Dog And Beth On The Hunt.

Today Dog spoke with Us Weekly regarding what the future holds without Beth. He even shared whether or not he thought he would remarry.

https://www.instagram.com/p/B0MWUZ7A3s4/

“There will never be another Mrs. Dog. We both said, ‘If I die, you can have a man. 10 men, I don’t care. But don’t marry one. And she said, ‘Don’t take my name off your chest, Dog. Do not get married.’ Don’t worry, I will not get married. I still put the pillows in the middle of the bed. This morning I thought she was still there,” the 66-year-old reality TV star explained.

While Duane has vowed never to get married again, that does not mean he won’t date. He shared with the weekly magazine that life is lonely without Beth, as well as how much he dislikes people calling him a widower. Dog revealed he doesn’t do “single” very well and does have “companionship.”

Earlier this month the Dogs Most Wanted star was spotted out with a mystery woman, sparking speculation Duane was on a date. Dog and Beth’s daughter, Bonnie quickly shut those rumors down. The young woman used Instagram to blast those spreading lies about her father.

“Pissed on my father’s behalf that he can’t go on a business dinner without someone speculating he’s on a date. Leave our family alone, please. Leave my father alone, let him try to continue our business in peace,” Bonnie shared in an Instagram Story.

https://www.instagram.com/p/B1MupIAgFNt/

It is not shocking at all that Dog the Bounty Hunter won’t marry again following the death of his wife, Beth Chapman. They had a rare one of a kind love that many never get to experience in life. Bonnie even echoed the same sentiment following her mother’s passing, Dog would never remarry.
