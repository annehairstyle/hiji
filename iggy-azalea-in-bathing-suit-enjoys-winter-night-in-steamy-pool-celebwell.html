Iggy Azalea in Bathing Suit Enjoys &quot;Winter&quot; Night in Steamy Pool Celebwell

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, June 21, 2024

Iggy Azalea is heating up social media despite the dropping temperatures. The Fancy rapper shared a new photo of herself on social media this week, lounging in a steaming hot swimming pool in her bathing suit. "This is what I'm doing all winter long…" the 31-year-old mother-of-one captioned the snap. How does she keep herself so fit? Read on to see 7 ways Iggy Azalea stays in shape and the photos that prove they work—and to get beach-ready yourself, don't miss these essential 30 Best-Ever Celebrity Bathing Suit Photos!

Iggy's abs aren't just flat—she has a six pack. During an Instagram Q&A, she revealed that her abfab secret is more about what she eats than doing crunches. "For me, diet is what does the trick," she said. " When I eat pretty basic, clean. Whole fruits (foods), meat and vegetables, that's when my abs start to show more." 

After welcoming her first child, Iggy claims she accidentally lost a whopping amount of weight. "Any other moms who can't stop dropping weight after having a baby? (Not a brag, genuinely wanna hear if you had this happen)" she wrote on social media. "I don't try/want to lose weight but it's literally just shedding off me, is it hormonal? Should I be concerned? 20lbs lighter than pre baby and counting."

During an interview with Nova 96.9, Iggy claimed she also lost a lot of weight twerking during rehearsals for her "Mo Bounce" video. "It's a lot-I went on a meal plan before the video because I knew I would have to be dancing fairly skimpily dressed," she explained. "I did a lot of rehearsals actually, and man I lost 15 pounds in a week dancing to that song!"

 In 2018, a source close to the rapper told Hollywood Life that the singer stays away from unhealthy food, especially when she is trying to lose weight, removing processed foods from her diet and carbs and sweets. 

"Iggy has been very committed to fitness and healthy eating, but she still has cheat days and allows herself to indulge once in a while," the source added to Hollywood Life. "She isn't too hard on herself and knows that splurging once in a while keeps her motivated in the long run."df44d9eab23ea271ddde7545ae2c09ec

In 2015 Iggy admitted to Australia's Today Show that she has struggled with self-love. "Some days I just think I want to crawl in my cave and I don't feel confident at all, and other days I feel great," she said. "I think it's like that for all human beings, not just young women … I think maybe feeling confident or 100% every day is something to aspire to, but I don't know if it's humanly possible for anybody."

Obviously, Iggy's fit figure is also a result of rigorous workouts. "Iggy also follows a strict workout regimen which includes hitting the gym several days a week. She loves cardio to keep her waist slim, and lunges and squats to keep a bangin' booty," the source told Hollywood Life.
