Brittany Mahomes' foot tattoo sparks controversy on social media

Posted by Artie Phelan on Monday, June 3, 2024

Brittany Mahomes' foot tattoo caused some controversy during the start of Kansas City Chiefs' training camp this week.

A few weeks ago, photos of Brittany on a boat surfaced and one of the viral pictures prominently displayed her feet, catching the attention of observant fans.

The meaning behind Brittany's tattoo has sparked differing opinions among NFL fans, leading to a back-and-forth debate.

Reactions to this assessment of Brittany's foot tattoo have been highly varied, with some having one perspective on the matter and others holding completely opposite views. Certain users suggested that it resembled the symbol of polygamy, while others believed it could be an infinity heart.

"There's a plethora of different meanings for this tattoo," @StarcadeMediaKC wrote on Twitter.

"The colors are what signify polyamorous and hers is all black. The symbol itself, without the red heart or blue infinity sign color, simply means eternal love or lasting loyalty. Irresponsible post."

Another user added: "Even if your [sic] in a open relationship, why tattoo the damn symbol for it. I'm gonna tattoo the Salvation Army."

Recently, Brittany has been making headlines quite frequently. Her outfit selection for the beginning of Chiefs training camp also caused a stir on the internet.
