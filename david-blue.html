David Blue

Posted by Kelle Repass on Wednesday, June 19, 2024

LOS ANGELES, CA – AUGUST 1: Blue arrives at the premiere of “Star Trek: Renegades” at the Crest Theatre on August 1, 2015 in Los Angeles, CA. photo by cvanwagoner/Bigstock.com

Birth Name: David Cary Blue

Place of Birth: New York, U.S.

Date of Birth: January 17, 1979

Ethnicity: Ashkenazi Jewish

David Blue is an American actor. He is known for his roles on the series Ugly Betty, Stargate Universe, Henry Danger, and Danger Force.

His family is Jewish. He was raised in New York and Florida.

David’s paternal grandfather was Edward/Isador Bernard Blue (the son of Morris Bluestein/Bloestein and Dora/Dorothy Peres/Perres). Edward was born in New York, to parents from Russia. Morris was the son of Isaac Bluestein and Hannah T. Boskenbaum. Dora was the daughter of Samuel “Sam” Peres and Annie Bushlowitz/Bislawitz.

David’s paternal grandmother was Sylvia Wolitzer/Wolytzer (the daughter of William Wolitzer and Ida Geller). Sylvia was born in New York, to parents from Galicia, Austria/Poland.

David’s maternal grandfather was Irving Tarde (the son of Louis Tarde/Terde and Fannie/Fanny Goufon). Irving was born in New York.

David’s maternal grandmother was Barbara Elsie Saltzman (the daughter of Harry Saltzman and Rebecca Goldner). Barbara was born in New York, to parents from Romania. Harry was the son of Jacob Saltzman and Sarah Kahn.
