Days of Our Lives Spoilers: Jason Gerhardt Joins DOOL as Eric Brady Recast GH Alum Fills In For

Posted by Billy Koelling on Monday, June 24, 2024

Days of Our Lives (DOOL) spoilers reveal that Eric Brady (Greg Vaughan) will look a little different soon due to a temporary recast. Vaughan had to step away from the role of Eric briefly, so Days turned to Jason Gerhardt to fill in for him.

DOOL fans can expect Gerhardt to appear as Eric this month, but his stint won’t be a long one. Still, it’s a good idea to know in advance and be prepared for a new face to pop up!

Jason Gerhardt might seem familiar to some soap fans – at least if you were a General Hospital watcher back in 2007-2008.

Gerhardt played Cooper Barrett on GH and appeared in a couple episodes of General Hospital: Night Shift as well.

You also might’ve seen Gerhardt on shows like Mistresses, Bones, Vegas, Chicago Med, NCIS and more.

Gerhardt is a handsome, talented actor who seems like a great temporary recast choice, so Days viewers will be curious to see his version of Eric Brady.

Of course, Greg Vaughan’s absence was resolved quickly, which means it won’t be long until he resumes onscreen Eric appearances after Gerhardt’s short run.

As for what’s coming up in Salem, Sloan Petersen (Jessica Serfaty) will continue to keep huge secrets from Eric regarding the paternity of Nicole Walker’s (Arianne Zucker) baby and the DNA trickery she’s resorted to.

Days spoilers say Nicole will also land in a tight spot thanks to some surprising proposals, so there’ll be plenty of drama to contend with as her pregnancy moves forward.

Eric’s suspicions about Sloan could be on the rise in the coming weeks, especially if Melinda Trask (Tina Huang) makes a mess. Melinda is on the verge of figuring out Sloan’s shocker and could use it to gain the upper hand on her frenemy.
