Tamar Braxton Has The Most Emotional Message For LaShawn Daniels And Tiny Harris Supports Her

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, June 18, 2024

Tamar Braxton is still hurt after the passing of LaShawn Daniels. She posted a message in his honor on her social media account and Tiny Harris is here to support her pal.

‘Look for this AMAZING playlist on @iTunes. #lashawnDaniels EVERY Time I sing this song #Love&War, I PROMISE to dedicate it to you my friend!! EVERY concert, you will be remembered. This is my way of honoring you, for our true friendship And always being reminded of the bond that was created by God!!! Although Many people played a part (other producers, writers music/tv, etc) ONLY YOU and I together shared A #1 tv show, a #1 song AND #1 album at the same damn time ❤️❤️🙏🏼’ Tamar began her post on social media.

She continued and wrote: ‘As SUPER hard as this has all been, somehow, God Chose the 6th year anniversary of #love&war just so me and the world will NEVER forget YOU, & your amazing Gifts of music and laughter 🙏🏼I love u forever my brother And thank you for playing a part in making my dreams come true and all of our miracle memories ❤️💔😢😢😢😢 (go to @jpdesignsart link in bio for the playlist)’

https://www.instagram.com/p/B2R-CNlhuGk/

Tiny commented: ‘Awe friend… u two had something really special, musically & the friendship!! Glad I got to know him thru you!!! 🙏🏽❤️’

A commenter posted this: ‘The whole situation is heartbreaking…but one thing I left his service with yesterday is; life is too short. Live, love, laugh, and forgive. Cherish the people God places in your life, we never know what tomorrow holds. ❤️❤️’

One fan was a bit confused and said: ‘I wonder why no mentions of April 🤷🏽‍♀️ Rest peacefully hitmaker. U made ur mark for sure! This homegoing should be televised…it’s that important.’

Somoene else defended Tamar and posted: ‘She didn’t mention April nor Vince and what’s the problem. She spoke about Lashawn because he was the writer and he’s the one who unfortunately passed away. Y’all need to stop looking for drama. Let her grieve and post him. Y’all cry about everything.’
