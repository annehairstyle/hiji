The Young and the Restless Spoilers: Eric Braeden Says Maybe I Finally Need Hearing Aids!!

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, June 24, 2024

The Young and the Restless (Y&R) spoilers reveal that Eric Braeden (Victor Newman) recently said, “Maybe I finally need hearing aids!!” The Y&R star also shared the hilarious reason why, so let’s talk about Braeden’s confession regarding his taste in music.

On Twitter, Eric Braeden often likes to interact with his fans and open up a bit about his life. In one of Braeden’s latest tweets, he revealed a genre of music that he just can’t get on board with.

“I know I’m in the minority when I say this: I can’t wrap my head around this RAP stuff !” Braeden tweeted. “Maybe I finally need hearing aids!!”

Of course, it didn’t take long for fans to chime in regarding Braeden’s view of rap.

Some Twitter users backed Braeden up about not being able to get into rap while others offered suggestions on specific rap artists he should check out.

Braeden even agreed to give some of those suggestions a try, so he’s keeping an open mind.

Despite not being fond of rap, Braeden admitted he has a “very eclectic taste in music.”

It turns out Braeden listens to everything from opera to Elvis Presley, but rap just isn’t a genre he enjoys.

We all have certain likes and dislikes, so no one will hold that against Braeden!

As for Braeden’s joke about needing hearing aids, plenty of fans piped up to assure him that his ears weren’t deceiving him and that they understood his rap aversion.

Whether you like rap or not, it’s nice to see Eric Braeden reaching out to fans and having a little fun on social media.

Braeden continues to deliver great performances as Victor Newman as well, so he remains one of the most popular soap stars around.

The Young and the Restless spoilers say Victor has more Ashland Locke (Robert Newman) problems ahead, so don’t miss his next round of drama.
