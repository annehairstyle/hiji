Who is Leslie David Baker?

Leslie David Baker is an American actor and television actor. He is popular for his portrayal of Stanley Hudson in The Office.

He has established himself as a versatile actor in his successful career. Moreover, he also stepped his foot into music.

Leslie David Baker: Early life, Childhood, Education

He was born on February 8, 1943, in Los Angeles, and he grew up in Chicago, Illinois, USA. Not much is known except the fact that he showed a keen interest in acting and music from a very young age.

Talking about his education and qualifications, it seems that he also has dedicated himself to studying. So, he graduated from Loyola University Chicago with a degree of B.S. in Psychology.

In addition, he holds an M.S. in Human Services Administration from Spertus Institute for Jewish Learning and Leadership in Chicago.
