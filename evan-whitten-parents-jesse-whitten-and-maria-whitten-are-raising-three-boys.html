Evan Whitten Parents Jesse Whitten and Maria Whitten Are Raising Three Boys

Posted by Kelle Repass on Monday, June 3, 2024

Evan Whitten parents are Jesse Whitten and Maria Whitten. His family consists of his two brothers Levi and Lincoln.

One of the rising child stars, Evan seems to have reached his stardom from a young age. He is now not only working in movies and series in different roles, but he has also gotten his hands on prominent characters.

The child actor was born on January 11, 2010. And he is not the only child in his family. He has two other brothers that he loves to hang out.

When he was a child, he was homeschooled to study. So, he didn’t much interaction with kids similar to his age. Therefore, his mother put him in a theater practice where he could interact with people and learn acting techniques.

And that really paid off for the young star as he is working in big-budget movies such as Years Later, Destroyer, The Resident, Mr. Robot, NEXT, and Chupa.

Evan Whitten Parents

Evan Whitten parents Jesse and Maria Whitten have been supportive of his acting career. Evan was even admitted to a theater by his mother.

Jesse Whitten

Evan Whitten father Jesse Whitten is a lawyer at Campbell Whitten Trial Lawyer.

He always aspired to be a lawyer. So, he studied law at Oak Brook College of Law. He graduated from the college in 2003 with honors. Later he also graduated from the Trial Lawyers College to complete his law education.

After his graduation, he started his own practice, which awarded him a huge reputation in the law field. He was awarded National Trial Lawyers in 2014 for Top 100, California Attorneys for Criminal Justice and the American Institute of Criminal Law Attorneys.

Now, he is working with his friend Clayton Campbell from law school and working together to give people justice.

Maria Whitten

Looking at her photos, Maria comes from a Hispanic community and seems to be a housewife taking care of her sons.

She is also helping her other sons, who seem to follow in their brother’s footsteps in acting. His brother Levi is also acting in some form.
