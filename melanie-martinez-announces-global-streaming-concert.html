Melanie Martinez Announces Global Streaming Concert

Posted by Artie Phelan on Wednesday, June 19, 2024

To end the K-12 era, Melanie Martinez announced that she will be playing a newly conceived one-of-a-kind theatrical presentation of the K-12 Tour, now called, CAN’T WAIT TILL I’M OUT OF K-12!

This show is the LAST CHANCE that fans have to experience the concert, after the second run of the tour sadly got canceled due to the pandemic. Just like her actual show, the concert production will include a full band and dancers. For the show, Melanie is giving the songs from the K-12 Tour a new spin, in addition to performing tracks from her After School EP.

CAN’T WAIT TILL I’M OUT OF K-12 will stream worldwide in four time zones beginning on Thursday, December 17th, airing at 8:00 PM in GMT, EST, and PST, and Friday, December 18th at 8:00 PM AEDT. Complete information can be found now at www.melaniemartinezmusic.com.

For this tour, Melanie included special merchandise in each VIP package as well! The merch in each one is exclusive per package, and only available for purchase is as a part of these packages.

Below are packages options:

GA Ticket – Includes one-time access to the stream.

K-12 VIP package – Includes one-time access to the stream and exclusive t-shirt.

After School VIP package – Includes one-time access to the stream, exclusive t-shirt & tote bag.

The Ultimate K-12 Deluxe VIP package (limited quantities) – Includes one-time access to the stream, exclusive t-shirt, tote bag, signed puzzle and 15 min post-show small group virtual M&G with Melanie herself.
