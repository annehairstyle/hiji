Max Goldwasser Career

Max’s career intensions from a young age were mainly based on acting. But because he grew up in Hidden Hills a neighborhood full of people from the entertainment industry. The community center at the place had a 99-seat theater.

Later on, he decided to venture into screen acting auditioning for commercials, TV Shows and movies. After his experience in acting, max began to realize that his calling is in the world of broadcasting. Therefore, while he studied at USC, he became a field reporter for the student-run news station.

He also served as an actor for a flagship sports show. He also worked as a producer and analyst for another sports talk show and co-hosted a weekly podcast. Max then earned his Cum Laude with a Bachelor’s degree in Broadcast and Digital Journalism and a Sports Media Minor back in May 2018.

Max also states that he has experience working as a researcher for the NFL Network and also as a producer for Spectrum SportsNet. He performed all these roles part-time while he was at USC. He also describes himself as a sports lover. Generally, he has played football, basketball, baseball, lacrosse, martial arts among others.
