Warriors fan Jessica Alba makes surprise appearance at Knicks vs. Heat Game 2 with comedians Chris R

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, June 27, 2024

Jessica Alba was spotted at the New York Knicks' duel against Miami Heat on Tuesday. The famous actress attended Game 2 of the Eastern Conference semi-finals at Madison Square Garden along with comedians Chris Rock, Dave Chappelle and Tracy Morgan, all of whom sat courtside.

After celebrating her birthday last Friday, Alba decided to watch the NBA clash along with her friend Lizzy Mathis. Pictures of her posing courtside went viral on social media, with fans showing their love for the actress.

"All I'm saying is if I was playing in front of Jessica Alba at the Garden I would've dropped 50," the fan wrote.

Another added: "Jessica Alba wasn't on our screens for even 5 seconds and she's trending. Love that for her lol."

A Warriors fan at Madison Square Garden

The California-born actress Alba maintained a close friendship with former Warriors star Baron Davis and was spotted in attendance at Warriors games various times.

"Yes: I'm well aware that [Jessica] Alba and Baron Davis are longtime pals," ESPN's Marc Stein said according to Golden State of Mind.

"But I'll go out on a limb and say that it's still a rather momentous occasion when an actress of Alba's hotness, er, stature, wants to fly in for a courtside seat.

"Alba, by all accounts, only hits Warriors games when they're in L.A."
