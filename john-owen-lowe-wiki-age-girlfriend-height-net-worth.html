“Unstable” Actor John Owen Lowe Wiki | Bio

John Owen Lowe (age: 27 years; born on November 6, 1995) is an American actor, writer, and producer recognized for his work in the 2015 TV series “The Grinder.” His performance in the 2020 thriller “9-1-1: Lone Star” also received wide recognition.

He has also made a mark in the world of cinema, with memorable roles in films such as “Grace Point,” “Holiday in the Wild,” and “Madness in the Hills.” His ability to bring complex characters to life on both the big and small screens is a testament to his versatility as an actor.

These days Owen is turning heads with his work in a comedy series, “Unstable,” which premiered on March 30, 2023, and has already garnered a lot of attention for its star-studded cast and side-splitting storyline.

In this show, Lowe plays the role of a socially awkward son tasked with saving his father’s eccentric and exotic company from disaster. As the show’s plot unfolds, viewers are treated to a hilarious and heartwarming story of family, friendship, and the trials and tribulations of trying to fit in.

How old is John Owen Lowe?

On November 6, 1995, John Owen Lowe (age 27) was born in Los Angeles, California, America, to his father, the famous actor Rob Lowe, and mother, Sheryl Berkoff, who is a renowned makeup artist.

He is the second child of Rob after Matthew Edward Lowe. Growing up in a family of artists, it’s no surprise that John developed a passion for the entertainment industry at an early age. He was inspired by his father’s work and knew from a young age that he wanted to follow in his footsteps.

In 2014, John made his first foray into the entertainment industry with a short film called “A Canine Affair.” However, it wasn’t until 2015 that his career took off when he landed a recurring role in the hit comedy series “The Grinder.”

Despite his early success in acting, John didn’t let fame go to his head. He remained committed to his studies and attended Stanford University to pursue a degree in science and technology. This dedication to education is a testament to John’s intelligence and determination to succeed both on and off the screen.

John Owen Lowe’s Acting Career

In 2019, John starred in the romantic comedy “Holiday in the Wild,” directed by Ernie Barbarash. This movie marked a milestone for John, as he was able to share the screen with his father, the legendary actor Rob Lowe.

Alongside co-stars Kristin Davis, Colin Moss, and Stevel Marc, John gave a standout performance that showcased his talent and versatility as an actor.

John’s career continued to soar in 2020 when he landed a recurring role in the hit TV series “9-1-1: Lone Star.” Once again, he proved he had what it takes to hold his own among a star-studded cast.

In 2023, John earned recognition for his work in the Netflix series “Unstable.” It’s safe to say that the future is looking bright for John, and fans can’t wait to see where his career will take him next. So stay tuned, and get ready to witness more impressive performances from this gifted actor.
