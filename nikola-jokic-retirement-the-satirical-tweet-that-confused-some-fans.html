Nikola Jokic retirement: The satirical tweet that confused some fans

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, June 25, 2024

As Denver Nuggets fans celebrate the franchise's first ever NBA title, some were left worried and seriously confused about the future of basketball star Nikola Jokic, after a tweet suggested his career could be coming to an end.

The internet can be a very unforgiving place and it is very easy to end up believing something that many others know to be completely false.

That is exactly what happened to lots of Nuggets fans and NBA fans in general, who took a joke tweet from a Twitter account called Ballsack Sports at face value.

"Denver front office executives hold 'growing concern' that Nikola Jokic will contemplate retirement following the Finals," the account tweeted.

"'The game is too easy for him. He's been bored for a couple years now'."

That set some fans into a frenzy about whether one of the greats was going to call time on his career despite only being 28 years old.

Other fans, though, were already aware that Ballsack Sports, as the name should suggest, is a satirical account that invents scenarios for comedic effect.

The fact that they often trick serious publications is what has made them so popular.

Jokic is now an NBA champion

Any Nuggets fans who may feel silly after falling for the tweet will not care anymore, as their team are the NBA champions.

Their NBA Finals series was against the Miami Heat, who got this far against the odds, and there was a moment where it seemed an upset may be on the cards.

The Nuggets won the first game fairly comfortably, but then lost Game 2, giving the momentum to the Heat.

However, Jokic and his team soon reasserted their dominance and, on Monday night, the Nuggets won 94-89 at home to make it 4-1 in the series, clinching their first NBA title.
