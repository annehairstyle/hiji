Selena Gomez Flips Off Paparazzi on Set of 'Only Murders in the Building'

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, June 29, 2024

Selena Gomez is in the midst of filming her upcoming Hulu series, Only Murders in the Building, and in the course of production over the last few months, has given us some excellent fashion moments.

Gomez continued to give us major coat envy on set this week — as well as a major vibe, as she appeared to flip off photographers on set. She was seen on the New York City set in an orange fuzzy coat and black turtleneck with a protective face mask, giving the middle finger as paparazzi photographed her.

Of course, the moment could have been part of the show she's filming, but either way, this photo is a real mood. The face mask (because 2021), the middle finger, the facial expression — all of it art.

Gomez is starring in the Hulu series with Steve Martin and Martin Short. According to Deadline, the show follows three strangers who share an obsession with true crime and suddenly find themselves wrapped up in one. Only Murders in the Building is Gomez's return to TV, and she is also executive producing the show.
