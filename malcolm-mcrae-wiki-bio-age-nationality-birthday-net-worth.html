Malcolm McRae: Wiki, Bio, Age, Nationality, Birthday, Net Worth

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, June 12, 2024

Malcolm McRae: Wiki, Bio, Age, Nationality, Birthday, Ethnicity, Net Worth, Wife, Height, Career, Hobbies, and Interests: Malcolm McRae is a renowned actor, singer and is skilled in playing piano as well as guitar. He has garnered attention towards him for his award-winning performance as Young Paul in the short movie Brotherly. Presently, he has shared his part of the spotlight as he is rumored to be married to his sweetheart Anya Taylor Joy. The couple was previously dating.

Malcolm McRae Age:

Malcolm was born on 16 April 1994. Presently, he is 29 years old. He is the son of William McRae and Lesley Mcrae. He is not the only child of his parents. Malcolm has two siblings: Alexander Keene McRae and Charlotte Rainier McRae. He was born and brought up in Alabama, US. His father graduated from Wharton and worked as a venture capitalist while his mother graduated from Pearson’s Paris. She was an interior decorator.

His brother turned out to have an exceptional career as a song artist like him.

Malcolm McRae Early Life and Career:

Not much information is available on the internet about Malcolm McRae’s education. However, several sources have stated that he had a thriving career as an artist since the start.

When he was 12, he was discovered at the AMTC talent competition and won Over All Young Actor. In 2008, he starred in the short film Brotherly, and in 2020, he played Gene Grady in How’dy!

More recently, Malcolm has become interested in music, constantly promoting his rock band, More’s Music on social media, and posting clips of himself playing guitar. According to his IMDB page, he also plays guitar and piano and sings “pop, folk, rockabilly, rock, and musical theatre.”

Malcolm signed with Warner Records with Kane Ritchotte (formerly of Portugal The Man). They’ve been making music for over three years and are even Spotify verified. More released 1/2, the first half of their two-part EP, last summer.

McRae discussed his history with Ritchotte and the creation process for 1/2 in a July 2020 interview with Rain magazine. “The writing process took about three years,” he said. “Kane and I were both working on solo projects and had a few songs written and recorded separately.” We got together and decided it would be fun to write and perform our songs together. So we finished ten songs over the course of three years.”

Interestingly, at the age of 12, he won the AMTC (Millie Lewis Talent Competition) Award for best overall child actor, where he met his manager, Michelle Zeitlin.

Malcolm McRae’s Girlfriend: Anya Taylor Joy

Malcolm McRae is in a relationship with Anya Taylor Joy. She is a rising Hollywood star, having appeared in Netflix’s The Queen’s Gambit and as the upcoming Saturday Night Live host. The couple has not openly stated that they were dating but they have been quite clear about their romantic connection. This can be deduced as the two were often seen around the Big Apple, walking and holding hands. The couple were photographed together kissing in Venice in September 2021.

There were rumors in 2022, that Malcolm has already tied the knot secretly with Anya Taylor. Their relationship was openly revealed as the couple made their first debut together in January 2023 at Golden Globes.

Looking back on his career, it is not shocking that he has accumulated good fortune. His net worth presently stands at 4 to 5 million USD.
