The Bagent Family’s Athletic Legacy

The Bagent family is renowned for their exceptional athletic abilities, leaving a lasting legacy in the world of sports. From arm wrestling to football, their achievements have made them a household name in the athletic community.

Travis’s journey into arm wrestling began during his time as a baseball player at Shepherd University. His dedication and hard work paid off, as he went on to secure an impressive 17 world championships and 25 national titles in arm wrestling. Travis’s remarkable accomplishments have earned him recognition in popular reality series and documentaries, solidifying his status as a seasoned professional in the sport. The Bagent family’s athletic prowess extends even further, with Tyson’s grandfather, Jerry Boyd, also holding the esteemed title of champion arm wrestler.
