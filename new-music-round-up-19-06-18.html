New Music Round Up: 19/06/18

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, June 3, 2024

Welcome to a bumper edition of CelebMix’s New Music Round Up. Over the past seven days, we’ve been inundated with huge new releases from a whole host of our favourite artists and in all honesty we’re still reeling from the surprises, collaborations and bops which we’ve been blessed with.

Leading this week’s round up are Beyoncé and Jay-Z, aka The Carters who casually dropped a surprise joint album after one of their OTR II tour dates at London Stadium, as you do. Everything Is Love was originally available exclusively on Tidal, surprise surprise, however, it has since made its way to all streaming/downloading services.

Nicki Minaj and Ariana Grande graced us with their fourth collaboration, ‘Bed’, ahead of the eagerly anticipated ‘The Light Is Coming’ which is due to drop tomorrow. Ariana also featured on Troye Sivan’s new track ‘Dance To This’, taken from his forthcoming sophomore album Bloom.

DNCE made a welcome return with their new EP People to People, whilst Bebe Rexha gave us another taster from her debut album Expectations in the form of ‘I’m A Mess’. We were also treated to two summer bops this week which are destined to be huge. Luis Fonsi and Stefflon Don went multilingual on us during ‘Calypso’ whilst Sigala teamed up with Sean Paul, Fuse ODG and Kent Jones for the euphoric ‘Feels Like Home’.

New music also came from BLACKPINK, All Saints, Ina Wroldsen, Aly & AJ, FRND, CC Clarke, Steve Grand, Hannah Scott, Picture This, Jason Derulo and Ira Losco, Brendan Murray, Luca Chesney, MwS, Yasiris and Ilinca, Four of Diamonds, Dirty Nano Vs. INNA Featuring The Motans, Alexander Jean x Lindsey Stirling, and Michael Blume.

However, we’re not done just yet. This week saw so many new releases which we’ve yet to tell you about, so sit back, relax and get ready to press play on more new singles and albums from the past week.
