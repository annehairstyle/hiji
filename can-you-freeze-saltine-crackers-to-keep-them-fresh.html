Can you freeze saltine crackers to keep them fresh?

Posted by Billy Koelling on Tuesday, June 25, 2024

Leave unopened crackers and chips in their original wax or plastic packaging. Place opened crackers and chips in plastic freezer bags or wrap them in plastic wrap. Wrap them again in aluminum foil to help protect the crackers from moisture and freezer burn.
