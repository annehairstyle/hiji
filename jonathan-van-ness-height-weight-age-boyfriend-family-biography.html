Jonathan Van Ness is an American hairdresser, podcaster, and television personality known for the popularity of his podcast, Getting Curious with Jonathan Van Ness. He has also appeared on the popular web series Gay of Thrones. Jonathan has a huge social media fan base with more than 1 million followers on Instagram and more than 300k followers on Twitter, and more than 50k followers on Facebook.

Education

Boyfriend / Spouse

Jonathan Van Ness has dated –

  • Wilco Froneman (2018) – Jonathan was in a short-lived relationship with the rugby player Wilco Froneman in 2018. Van Ness made his first public appearance with Froneman at the Creative Arts Emmys in Los Angeles on September 9, 2018. He took to Instagram to announce his relationship with him and later in December, to announce his break up as well. Looking at his post, it appeared that Wilco was cheating on him which was the reason for their separation. He requested his fans not to blame Wilco and that he was broken at the moment but he was good.
  • Antoni Porowski (2019) – Jonathan was then linked with his co-star from Netflix’s Queer Eye Antoni Porowski. Fans started speculating that Jonathan might be seeing Antoni, a restaurateur when both started a joint Instagram account in July and would post pics which showed them displaying affection for each other. However, towards the end of July (to be precise July 31, 2019), Van Ness finally took to Twitter to clear the speculations of him and Antoni Porowski dating. He added that Antoni is a lovely friend with whom he might fall in love someday but not now.
  • Mark Peacock (2020-Present) – In December 2020, he announced that he had tied the knot with Mark Peacock earlier in 2020.
