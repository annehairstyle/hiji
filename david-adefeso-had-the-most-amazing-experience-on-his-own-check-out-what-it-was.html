David Adefeso Had The Most Amazing Experience On His Own Check Out What It Was

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, June 12, 2024

David Adefeso told his fans that he had the most amazing experience on his own. Check out his message here.

‘Had the most amazing experience today. Climbed a mountain all alone. Almost 1,000 feet above sea level. Got lost in the jungle numerous times but always stuck with that old saying “follow the ocean.” 🌊 Then there were the mountain cliffs and the soaring ocean beds intermingled with chocking forest forays through the thick of the jungle, 🌴🌱🌿only to witness an extravagant explosion of natural beauty that truly has to be seen to be believed! The 6.5 mile hike normally takes 4-5 hours because it’s mostly uphill, but I knocked it out in 2.5! And I can say it was one heck of a mental, spiritual and physical mind f8%k! 🌞🧘🏿‍♂️’ David said.
