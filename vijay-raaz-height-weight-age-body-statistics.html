Vijay Raaz is an Indian actor, theatre actor, film director, voice actor, narrator, and comedian. A critically acclaimed artist, he has been cast in a number of movies like Monsoon Wedding, Dil Pe Mat Le Yaar, Run, Bhopal Express, Bombay To Goa, Welcome, Gangubai Kathiawadi, Delhi Belly, Mumbai Matinee, Raghu Romeo, Lal Salaam, Tandoori Love, Delhi 6, Chura Liyaa Hai Tumne, Gully Boy, Stree, Pran Jaye Par Shaan Na Jaye, Dekh Bhai Dekh, Mudda – The Issue, Love in Nepal, Aan: Men at Work, Dhamaal, Anwar, Hari Puttar, American Daylight, Mumbai Xpress, Shabnam Mausi, Deewane Huye Paagal, Yeh Mera India, Mr Joe B. Carvalho, Dedh Ishqiya, Kya Dilli Kya Lahore, Second Hand Husband, Suraj Pe Mangal Bhari, Kya Meri Sonam Gupta Bewafa Hai?, and 36 Farmhouse. Moreover, Vijay Raaz made his directorial debut in the 2014 Hindi-language war film Kya Dilli Kya Lahore.

    Vijay Raaz Facts

  • He shifted from Delhi to Mumbai in order to pursue a career in acting.
  • In 1999, Vijay Raaz made his first film appearance as Badru in the drama film Bhopal Express.
  • He co-starred as Parabatlal Kanhaiyalal ‘P.K.’ Dubey in the 2001 comedy-drama film Monsoon Wedding. This role got him nominated for a Zee Cine Award in the “Best Comedian” category.
  • He was cast as Ganesh Yadav (Siddharth’s friend) in the 2004 Hindi-language action comedy-romance film Run, starring Abhishek Bachchan and Bhumika Chawla. For this performance, Vijay Raaz was nominated for a Screen Weekly Award for “Best Performance in a Comic Role”.
  • In 2019, he played the recurring role of Jauhari in the romantic drama web series Made in Heaven.
  • Alongside Alia Bhatt and Shantanu Maheshwari, Vijay Raaz co-starred as Raziabai in the 2022 Hindi-language biographical crime drama film Gangubai Kathiawadi.
