Unprisoned Season 2: Release Date, Cast, plot, trailer, And Many More Updates

Posted by Billy Koelling on Wednesday, June 12, 2024

Parents are the foundation of a child’s life, and having the best parents determine a kid’s social and romantic relationships. At the same time, shitty parents negatively impact a child’s life and could lower their standard of interaction among men or their peers. Unprisoned is the newly released series exploring parenthood and broken relationship dynamics. The series stars Kerry Washington, the Emmy award-winning actress. The show’s first season was released a while back, and fans are already excited about the second season plot. Here is everything we know about the show’s second season.

Unprisoned Season 2 Release Date: When will it air?

Unfortunately, there is no specific release date for the upcoming series. The show was recently released, and we have not received any renewal updates. If the show gets renewed, then expect season two in 2024.

Unprisoned Season 2 Plot: What will Happen?

Tracy McMillan inspires the series plot, and it revolves around the life of Alexandria, the messy but perfectionist therapist. She is a single mom having it rough, and to add to her other mysteries, her dad is fresh out of prison. Since he has nowhere else to call home, he must live with her as he figures out what to do next with his life. The show’s second season could explore the relationship between Paige and the men in her life. Does she solve her differences with her dad and let him move back in? Only one way to find out.

Unprisoned Season 2 Cast: Who will be in?

Kerry Washington, the Emmy-winning actress, starred as the series protagonist. She plays Paige Alexander, the single therapist who has to live with her dad when he gets released from prison. Washington is an international name and has received several nominations and accolades. She became famous in the Scandal and Save the Last Dance series, where she portrayed Chenille. She has starred in other big shows like Django Unchained, Little Fires Everywhere, and The School of Good and Evil. She stars alongside Jordyn McIntosh as Little Paige, Delroy Lindo as Edwin Alexander, Marque Richardson as Mal, and Faly Rakotohavana as Finn. Other casts include Brenda Strong, Jee Young Han, and Edwin Lee Gibson. Unfortunately, we have yet to be updated on the second season cast, but probably all the casts will return to reprise their roles.

Season 1 Review:

According to Decider, the streamers has some of the best performance from Kerry Washington and Delroy Lindo, and you should certainly stream the show. The series plot is also well written, making the show fun to watch and follow.

Unprisoned Season 2 Trailer: When can we watch it?

A trailer for the show’s second season is yet to be released, and unfortunately, it will not be coming out anytime soon. The show’s first season was released on the 10th of March on Hulu. We are yet to receive the official updates on whether the show will be getting a renewal or not., If the show gets renewed, the earliest you should expect to binge on the trailer is by 2024. If you did not watch the show’s first season and would love to do so, you can stream the series on Hulu. If you would also love a sneak peek of what the series entails, a trailer for the first season already exists on Hulu or YouTube.
