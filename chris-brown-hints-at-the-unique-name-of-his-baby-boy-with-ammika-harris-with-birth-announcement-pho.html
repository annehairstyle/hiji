Chris Brown Hints At The Unique Name Of His Baby Boy With Ammika Harris With Birth Announcement Pho

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, June 13, 2024

Chris Brown is a dad for the second time. Several reliable sources have revealed that Chris’ ex-girlfriend, Ammika Harris, had a baby boy this week.

The name of Chris’ baby boy has not been revealed by some fans who believe it might be Indigo due to the photos he used to announce the birth.

The singer is already dad to 5-year-old daughter Royalty, whom he shares with ex Nia Guzman.

Ammika mentioned the birth of her first child in a simple message that read: “I was in love when I first saw you.”

The crooner posted a photo of himself with the caption, “11-20-2019,” which is believed to be the baby’s birth date. He also shared another with the word “BORN” on it.

The supposed girlfriend of Chris, Ammika, recently made news after she shut down suggestions by a fan that she was trying to imitate actress Karrueche Tran, one of Chris’ exes.

It all began with a new post that the 26-year-old shared on Instagram.

https://www.instagram.com/p/B5He54Hhhpn/

The post, consisting of a couple of photographs of Ammika, shows the model in a more natural look. It seems that Ammika wanted to skip the makeup this time, as she wore almost none, except for lipstick.

The media personality explained in the description that she wears her favorite lipstick from MAC Cosmetics, Hover.

The hair of the model is also done simply, just straightened and split in the middle.

https://www.instagram.com/p/B5He54Hhhpn/

Unsurprisingly, the model post quickly gathered a lot of likes and praises from her many followers, with just one standing out from the rest with his question if the model was trying to change her style to look more like Chris’ ex, Karrueche, by the way she did her lips.

https://www.instagram.com/p/B41-P3oggF5/
