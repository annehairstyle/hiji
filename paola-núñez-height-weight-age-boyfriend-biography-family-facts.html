    Paola Núñez Facts

  • At the age of 12, she started acting in theatre. Then when she was 16 years old, she started her full-time acting career.
  • From 2005 to 2006, Paola Núñez appeared as Bárbara Bazterrica in the television drama titled Amor en custodia and this role won her the Premios Bravo Award for “Best New Actress”.
  • Alongside Will Smith, Martin Lawrence, Vanessa Hudgens, Alexander Ludwig, Charles Melton, Kate del Castillo, Nicky Jam, and Joe Pantoliano, Paola co-starred as Lieutenant Rita Secada, head of A.M.M.O. and Mike’s ex-girlfriend, in the 2020 American action comedy film titled Bad Boys for Life.
  • She has served as a spokesperson for NGOs like Greenpeace, Save the Children, and Animal Heroes.
  • Paola Núñez has shared a friendly bond with Chilean-Mexican actor Andrés Palacios. They have co-starred in the telenovela Amor en Custodia.
