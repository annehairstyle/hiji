Television Career

Silvija was a popular journalist, editor, tv presenter, and television personality. According to Croatian Journalists Association, she belongs to the category of tv workers who can rightfully be said to have been doyens of journalism. Luks was an active member of that association. Since high school, all the time, she was engaged in journalism. 

At the age of 20, Silvija worked as a journalist, editor, and presenter at Radio Zagreb. Later, she moved to Television Zagreb. She was a reporter from Angola, then served as an author in the 1970s. In addition, she participated in the creation of cultural, political, and documentary shows like Culture of the Heart, Screen on the Screen, and My Heart. 

In the early 1990s, she was deputy general director and program director at Yutel, and then spent a portion of her career in diplomacy. From 2000, Luks was again at HRT, where she was a program editor, mentor, and commentator. She also spent one year in the USA as a special correspondent. 

After that, she became a permanent journalist for HRT from France. Silvija returned to HRT as president of the Supervisory Board. Aside from this, in 2022, she appeared in the documentary titled Od Dubrovnika do Goe
