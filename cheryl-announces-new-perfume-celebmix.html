Cheryl Announces New Perfume - CelebMix

Posted by Artie Phelan on Wednesday, June 19, 2024

After more than two weeks away from social media Cheryl today came back with a bang.

The gorgeous Geordie who is currently recording her fifth studio album took to Twitter this afternoon to confirm the release of her new perfume.

The latest fragrance from the 33-year-old is called StormFlower Platinum and follows the release of her previous three best selling perfumes; StormFlower EDP, StormFlower EDT and StormFlower Noir.

The newest addition to Cheryl’s perfume brand has yet to be given a release date but Cheryl posted a stunning commercial for the upcoming fragrance via her Twitter account along with the caption: Coming soon…. #StormFlowerPlatinum. You can watch the gorgeous advert by visiting Cheryl’s Twitter account @CherylOfficial.

StormFlower Platinum comes in a stunning silver bottle with the now famous StormFlower flower lid.

Are you excited for Cheryl’s new perfume? Let us know over on Twitter @CelebMix.
