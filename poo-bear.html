Poo Bear

Posted by Kelle Repass on Friday, June 28, 2024

Jason Boyd – 25th Annual ASCAP Rhythm and Soul Awards – Arrivals – Beverly Hilton Hotel – Los Angeles, CA, USA, 2012 – Keywords: Poo Bear, Pooh Bear, 1/2 Length Shot Orientation: Portrait Face Count: 1 – False – Photo Credit: Aaron J. Thornton / PR Photos

Birth Name: Jason Paul Douglas Boyd

Place of Birth: Connecticut, U.S.

Date of Birth: September 4, 1979

Ethnicity: African-American, as well as about 1/32 Muscogee Native American

Poo Bear is an American is an American songwriter, singer, and music producer. He is also known as Poo Bear and MDMA.

Poo Bear is the son of Dolores and John Boyd. He spent much of his childhood in Atlanta, Georgia. He is married to Ashley Joi Sadler, with whom he has a son.

Poo Bear’s maternal grandfather was Frederick Douglas “Fred” Funderburg (the son of Robert Lee Funderburg and Leela Johnson). Frederick was born in Anniston, Alabama. Robert was the son of George Washington Funderburg and Ophelia Blassingame Hugley, who was of half Muscogee Native American descent.

Poo Bear’s maternal grandmother was Annie Laura Ball (the daughter of Eli Whitney Ball and Cora Curry). Annie was born in Oxford, Alabama. Eli was the son of George Ball and Lela Richie. Cora was the daughter of Richard Curry and Idella “Ida” Westbrook.

Poo Bear’s matrilineal ancestry can be traced back to his third great-grandmother, Recie, of Alabama.
