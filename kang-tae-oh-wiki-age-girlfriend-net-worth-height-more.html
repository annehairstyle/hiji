Extraordinary Attorney Woo Actor Kang Tae Oh Biography

Kang Tae Oh (born on June 20, 1994) is a 28 years old actor famous for his appearance in Korean tv shows. He first came on television in 2013, playing a significant role in the serial “After School: Lucky or Not.” In 2016 Tae rose to fame after portraying the role of Choi Young-Gwang in a series titled “The Best Lover.” Kang has also played a few cameo roles in movies and was also in a short film titled “Wake Up.”

This Korean actor’s career achieved new heights after “Extraordinary Attorney Woo” was released on June 29, 2022. The story of this new Tae Oh show is about a super-intelligent girl who tops his law college. As per sources, a few more Kang’s projects are in the pipeline, and we can expect their release by the end of 2022.

Career

In his debut year, Tae was approached by the makers of “Drama Festival – Save Wang Jo-Hyeon,” and they gave him the role of Nam Nam-Cheol in their show. One after the other, projects from more prominent production banners started getting offered to Oh. His status as an actor increased so much that he no longer had to give auditions; instead, he used to choose which show he wanted to do and which one did not.
