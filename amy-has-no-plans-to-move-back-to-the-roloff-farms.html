Amy Has No Plans to Move Back to the Roloff Farms

Posted by Artie Phelan on Sunday, June 2, 2024

Little People Big World spoilers, news, and updates tease that there are rumors about Amy Roloff moving back to the Roloff farms with her husband Chris Malek.

Amy and Chris have been living in a 4,000-square-foot property in Hillsboro, Oregon. Amy revealed that she was actively looking for a new house. Tori even showed her some houses near her home in Washington, and Amy was actually impressed.

Little People Big World Spoilers – Where Will Amy And Chris Move?

Fans initially thought Amy and Chris would move to Washington near Zach and Tori, but Amy has finally addressed all the speculations.

During a Facebook live about donations for the Little People of America Athletic Association with her friend Lisa, Amy answered fans’ burning questions.

She revealed that she has started liking the house she and Chris are currently living in, although she wished there was a little more space to make it better. She shared that the house is in a nice area.

She added that in other areas, the properties are quite expensive these days, so it’s hard to choose a new house. Moreover, since all her kids and grandkids are close by, she doesn’t want to leave Pacific Northwest.

Little People Big World Updates – No Plans To Move Back To The Roloff Farms

When a fan asked whether she and her kids would ever want to go back to the Roloff farms, Amy said she no longer plans to move back to the property. She also shared that the farm has already changed a lot, and the price is also beyond her budget and what her kids can afford.

Lisa was quick to add that the farm isn’t for sale right now, but Amy added that she wouldn’t be surprised if her ex-husband put it back on the market. But as it seems now, the farm has lost its charms for Amy and her kids ever to want to move back.

Is LPBW Ending In Season 25?

Meanwhile, fans are still waiting for concrete news regarding the 25th season of Little People Big World. In a recent Q&A session with fans, Tori Roloff hinted that her little family with Zach might be quitting the reality show, just like Zach’s siblings did.

Amid the feuds with Matt Roloff, Zach, and Tori have moved to Washington with their kids and hinted that they are looking for ways to earn income other than their paycheck from the TLC show. When fans asked about the future of their family on the show, Tori said their time on the show is approaching its end and that they are just enjoying it while they’re still part of it.

Could it be the end of LPBW? We’ll have to wait and see.
