Celeste Dring is an English comedian and actress best known for portraying Lauren Richards in the BBC television series Wanderlust and Princess Eugenie in Channel 4’s The Windsors.

Celeste Dring is an English actress from Wolverhampton.

Celeste graduated from the University of Cambridge with a degree in English in July 2010.

Dring contributed an essay to the campus magazine Varsity, which appeared in issue 729 on November 12, 2010.

Dring began her career as a dance director at Wolverhampton’s Central Youth Theatre. She directed Glamour and Romance for Everybody Dance Now for the Central Youth Theatre’s Cultural Olympiad Project in 2011.

At the Edinburgh Festival in 2013, she made her debut appearance on screen in her own co-written comedic pieces.

“Lazy Susan” was nominated for the comedic rookie category after a second appearance at the 2014 Edinburgh Festival Fringe.

Dring won a significant part as Princess Eugenie in Channel 4’s The Windsors in 2016 and later said that her portrayal of Eugenie was inspired by characters from Made in Chelsea rather than real-life events.

In 2018, Dring starred as Kayleigh Hudson in the BBC3 comedy series This Country as a recurring character. In the same year, Dring played Laura in BBC’s Wanderlust.
