Kylie Jenner Is Planning To Expand Her Makeup Line

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, June 15, 2024

Kylie Jenner has recently launched her own lip kit line and it was a huge success! She is now planning to expand the line and produce other products as well.

The Kylie Lip Kit sold out in less than a minute when it was first released. Earlier this week the website had restocked on products but they quickly sold out again. The 18-year-old reality star is overwhelmed by her success and she says that she plans to expand the lip kit line by adding more colours to it. Jenner is also hoping to branch out and release other beauty products in 2016. She wants to do a complete makeup line for her brand.

Teen Vogue interviewed Jenner and asked her where she saw herself in five years. Jenner responded:

“I see myself with a really successful makeup line. I want to expand it a lot. For now, I’m going to keep adding different lip colors. I’d also maybe do an eyeshadow palette, an eyebrow kit, or a contour kit, depending on what people want.” 

In the interview with Teen Vogue Jenner also gave beauty and makeup tips. She revealed that she uses Nip + Fab Dragon’s Blood Fix Serum every morning after washing her face with a face wash. This makes sense as Jenner is  Nip + Fab’s newest spokesperson. She also told the magazine that her nighttime routine consists of taking off her makeup with wipes and then washing her face. Then, she uses a toner, moisturizer, and under-eye cream. This, and the fact that she’s one of the lucky people who’s faces don’t break out, is how she gets perfect skin.

If you want to look flawless on camera, then Kylie Jenner suggests that you use the following foundations: Armani Face Fabric, Make Up For Ever HD, Rodial Skin Tint or Charlotte Tilbury. This is how she looks good in her Instagram photos, although she says she gets help from good lighting, nice angles and the VSCO Cam app.

With the popularity of the lip kit, Kylie Jenner is sure to be able to expand her makeup line. We’re rather excited to see what she plans to do with her brand in 2016.
