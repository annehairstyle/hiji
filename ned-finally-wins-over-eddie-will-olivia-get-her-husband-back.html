Ned Finally Wins Over Eddie, Will Olivia Get Her Husband Back?

Posted by Artie Phelan on Monday, June 10, 2024

General Hospital Spoilers reveal that Ned Quartermaine/Eddie Maine (Wally Kurth) has been perfectly happy having no responsibility and a lot of time on his hands.

Eddie has no desire at all to be Ned. However, Ned has some information that could certainly get Nina Reeves where it counts, even if it does blow Michael Corinthos’s (Chad Deull) plan out of the water.

Eddie Maine Is About To Be surprised

General Hospital Spoilers reveal that even though he doesn’t want it, Eddie is about to be bombarded with a flood of Ned’s memories as they all come crashing back at once.

Eddie already had a major flash when he remembered the wedding on the Brooklyn Bridge. What will be the next thing that sparks a memory?

Ned isn’t concerned with what Eddie wants, He wants to come home and get his life back on track. Ned also needs to clear his name after being blamed for the call to the SEC.

Can Ned finally clear his name and get everyone off his back? Will Ned bring Nina down finally and let everyone know who made that call and why?

GH Spoilers: Ned Quartermaine Needs To Come Home

Ned needs to return home and expose Nina’s phone call. Sonny Corinthos (Maurice Benard) who made that SEC call.

Nina may have gotten away with it for this long but Sonny has been determined to learn the truth. Since Michael has withheld that information, Ned just may have to give it to him. Will Ned tell Sonny who called the SEC?

Ned could certainly clear his name if he wanted to by just telling everyone that Nina made sure that call was made. Ned’s whole family thought that he was the one who made this call even though Drew Cain (Cameron Mathison) is family.

Ned deserves an apology and his memory would certainly get him that. Will he tell the truth?

General Hospital Spoilers: Fans Are Over Eddie Maine

Fans are just about over the whole story of Eddie these days. Fans also aren’t happy with Michael using his knowledge to control Willow Corinthos’ (Katelyn McMullen) relationship with Nina.

Will Ned regaining his memory put a stop to all of this? Could Willow hear Ned telling Nina what he knows now that his memory has returned?

Willow finds out that Michael is blackmailing his mother somehow, so why not Ned tell Nina what he knows and go from there?

Willow could confront Michael over keeping this secret and demand to know if that is what he is using against her mother. Either way, Olivia Quartermaine (Lisa LoCicero) will finally have her husband back.
