The Wedding Of The Year - Congratulations Janya!

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, June 23, 2024

They’re two of the worlds best known YouTubers and they’re finally married! Jim Chapman, 27, married long time fiancée Tanya Burr, 26, on Thursday after their eight year relationship. The service was held at Babington House in Somerset and the happy couple were joined by YouTuber friends Zoe Sugg and boyfriend Alfie Deyes, Niomi Smart and Marcus Butler amongst other friends and family.

The two got engaged in 2012 whilst holidaying in New York, an event that Jim filmed and posted on his vlog channel EveryDayJim; the video has since reached over 1.2 million views.

The two met at a house party in 2006 and were completely unaware that the two went to the same school. The two then got talking at the rest is history!

Their life has been captured on both of their blogs and YouTube channels with most of the world falling in love with them and their utterly adorable relationship.

The happy couple looked stunning on their special day, with Tanya dressed in a traditional white gown and Jim in an incredibly smart suit.

The two have kept a lot of details about their big day secret to their viewers, with Tanya wanting to keep it privet and teasing fans by telling them she was going wedding dress shopping during her Vlogmas series last year.

We wish Mr and Mrs Chapman all the happiness in the world and are so happy to see two of the nicest people happy together.
