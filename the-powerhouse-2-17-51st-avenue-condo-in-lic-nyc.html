The Powerhouse, 2-17 51st Avenue - Condo in LIC NYC

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, June 2, 2024

Condo located in Long Island City, between 50th Avenue & 51st Avenue

Description of The Powerhouse at 2-17 51st Avenue

The Powerhouse is a full-service condominium with amenities including a 24-hour concierge and maintenance staff, common roof-deck with BBQ grills, fitness center, game room, residents lounge, live-in super, and attached garage. The building is located at 2-17 51st Avenue, just a block away from the beautiful Gantry State Park and very close to various forms of public transportation including the East River Ferry landing and the subway, one stop from Grand Central Station.
