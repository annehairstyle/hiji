The Vamps Release 'Rockabye' Cover

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, June 8, 2024

A new year, a new set of brilliant covers of our favourite artists own take on some of our favourite hits. The Vamps are leading the pack with their take on the Clean Bandit (Ft. Anne-Marie and Sean Paul) number one smash ‘Rockabye’.

The quartet’s own take on the track sees Brad Simpson taking on keys and delicately beautiful lead vocals with the dream team of Connor Ball and James McVey soulfully serenading us on guitars whilst Tristan Evans drums entwine perfectly between these other elements. Directed by the talented Dean Sherwood this has become a visual and musical treat.

We love the twist of adding a verse of Clean Bandit’s earlier hit ‘Rather Be’ which effortlessly fits into the track. We’d have been happy with a like for like cover, but The Vamps have made this something even more special!
