Lil Uzi Vert Gives Joseph Grazi An Anarchy Tattoo

Posted by Artie Phelan on Saturday, June 8, 2024

Lil Uzi Vert has learned a thing or two while getting tattooed over the years, and recently turned the tables and gave artist Joseph Grazi some ink.

In a clip shared to social media on Wednesday (April 9), Uzi can be seen tattooing an anarchy symbol on Grazi’s chest.

AD LOADING...

The pair were at 6 Skulls tattoo shop in New York City – a spot the “Just Wanna Rock” artist often frequents for their many piercings and tatts.

In addition to being a music artist and now a tattoo artist, Uzi was looking to add wrestler to their resume as well over the weekend when they performed at Wrestlemania 39 in Los Angeles.

On Saturday (April 1), the Philly rapper performed “Just Wanna Rock” as the Usos — who were the reigning WWE Tag Team Champions — walked to the ring for their big match at the popular two-night Pay-Per-View event at the SoFi Stadium in Inglewood, CA.

AD LOADING...

After their performance, Lil Uzi Vert gave an exclusive interview to WWE, where they shared how excited they were to perform at such a prominent wrestling event — and as it turned out, they’re a big wrestling fan.

“I can’t believe I’m here — I can’t believe I got to see so many people that I always wanted to see for all my life,” they said to host Cathy Kelley backstage after the performance. “It’s crazy.”

AD LOADING...

They also exclaimed that they were “ready to get in the ring” when they saw the Usos walking into the ring.

“But you know, you’ll see me — April. Philadelphia. 2024. So, you know — I don’t want to give up all the surprises, but you’ll see me there,” they teased, insinuating that they’d be performing at Wrestlemania 40, which is slated to be hosted in their hometown of Philadelphia on April 6, 2024.

In other Uzi news, they recently revealed that new music with Playboi Carti could finally be on its way.

“Me and my luv about 2 take over the world,” Lil Uzi Vert said while tagging Playboi Carti in a post on Instagram in March.

In a Twitch interview with Adin Ross in February, Uzi revealed that they and Playboi Carti have recorded over 40 songs together for their long-awaited collab album 16*29.

“He’s another one that stay in his lane, nobody can jump in his lane,” Uzi said. “Anyboody who try to jump in Carti lane kill thyself.”

Both Lil Uzi Vert and Playboi Carti have an extensive collection of songs they’ve collaborated on over the years. These songs include “Of Course We Ghetto Flowers,” “wokeuplikethis, “Shoota,” “Left, Right,” and many more.
