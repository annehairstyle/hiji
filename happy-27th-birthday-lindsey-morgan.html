Happy 27th Birthday Lindsey Morgan

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, June 19, 2024

American actress Lindsey Morgan turns twenty-seven today.

The actress has had a number of roles since her career started, her most notable being in General Hospital and The 100. Lindsey got the part of Kristina Davis in General Hospital in 2011. After leaving the show, she landed a part in The 100 playing Raven Reyes. Lindsey is currently still playing the part of Raven in the fourth season of the popular show.
