WWE legend turned porn star Tammy 'Sunny' Sytch involved in fatal accident

Posted by Billy Koelling on Monday, June 3, 2024

Tammy 'Sunny' Sytch, a WWE Hall of Fame Hall of Fame inductee in 2011, has been involved in a car accident in Ormond Beach, Florida, in which a 75-year-old man was killed.

According to police reports published by TMZ, the former wrestler, who redirected her career to become a porn actress, may have been under the influence of alcohol in the accident involving three vehicles.

Sytch, 49, was unable to stop her Mercedes from crashing into the back of a Kia Sorento, seriously injuring its driver, Julian Lasseter Jr.

The New York Post reports that the driver was rushed to hospital, but nothing could be done to save his life.

The US Sun adds that witnesses to the accident claim that the former wrestler, who was also taken to hospital after the accident, was travelling at a very high speed.

She was arrested for trying to kill her partner with a pair of scissors

Sytch has been arrested six times for drink driving. In January 2022, Sunny was arrested by police for attempting to kill her "intimate partner" with a pair of scissors after using drugs and alcohol.

According to the court report "Tammy Sytch was involved in a domestic dispute with a man that could have proved fatal".

Tammy 'Sunny' Sytch: The most downloaded celebrity on the internet

In 1996, Sytch was the most downloaded celebrity on the internet, and she has been in serious trouble with the law since 2012 (with several arrests for DUI, theft, probation violation, and a restraining order violation) and has been in jail several times.

At the height of her career Sytch turned down a million-dollar offer from Playboy to pose nude, but years later she ended up working for cash for an adult content website from 2001 to 2003.

In 2016 she made the leap as an actress into the world of pornographic films.
