Wrexham AFC vs Boreham Wood: How to watch on TV and live stream from the US

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, June 18, 2024

In-form Wrexham are set to play what could be their last home game of the season, as they host Boreham Wood on Saturday.

The National League leaders have been engaged in a tight title race with second-placed Notts County, but they could clinch promotion to the Football League with a win in their upcoming clash.

Ryan Reynolds and Rob McElhenney's team are top of the table with 107 points, followed by Notts County, who sit four points behind the leaders with two games to go. This means that should Notts County lose to Maidstone United away from home earlier on Saturday, Wrexham will have mathematically won the title before their kick off.

Wrexham AFC vs Boreham Wood: Predicted line-ups

The two Wrexham stars, Ben Foster and Paul Mullin, are expected to start in goal and up front, respectively. However, Jacob Mendy is highly doubtful through injury.

As for Boreham Wood, coach Luke Garrard is not expected to make any changes from his starting XI after his team secured a 1-0 win over Wealdstone last weekend.

Wrexham's predicted XI (4-4-2): Foster; Tozer, Forde, O'Connell, Cannon; Lee, Jones, O'Connor, Barnett; Palmer; Mullin.

Boreham Wood's predicted XI (3-4-1-2): Ashmore; Agbontohoma, Evans, Bush; Sousa, Payne, Broadbent, Ilesanmi; Brunt; Ndlovu, Marsh.

Wrexham AFC vs Boreham Wood: When does it start?

The crucial National League duel between Wrexham AFC and Boreham Wood is scheduled to take place on Saturday, April 22, at 18:30 BST, which is the local time in Wales.

This is a 13:30 EST / 10:30 PST kick-off in the United States.

Wrexham AFC vs Boreham Wood: Where can you watch it?

Fans in the United Kingdom and Ireland can watch the game between Wrexham AFC vs Boreham Wood live on BT Sport 1, BTSport.com, BT Sport App and National League TV.

Meanwhile, viewers in the US and Canada can find the Wrexham game on National League TV.
