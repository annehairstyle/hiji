Cyrus Convinces Judge He's A Changed Man

Posted by Billy Koelling on Monday, June 24, 2024

General Hospital (GH) spoilers suggest that Cyrus Renault (Jeff Kober) convinces a judge that he’s a changed man. He suggests that he found God and that he’s been reborn. However, fans don’t trust his intentions. Keep on reading to learn more.

According to the General Hospital spoilers, Cyrus convinces the judge that he’s changed. He believes that he won’t go back to his old ways.

The user 24Kasie76 posted on the SoapCentral.con Message Boards: “Cyrus’s phony expressions trying to convince the judge he is a brand new man is silly.” Some fans don’t believe that he’s a changed man.

Even the original poster noted; “If I am a judge, I would still say No. JMHO Cyrus has NOT changed. Some can in life, other inmates, crazy bad men just can’t.”

GH Spoilers: Cyrus Convinces Judge He’s A Changed Man

GH fans took to the thread to share their thoughts. Most of them believe that Cyrus is already working on his next scam. He already conned the judge into thinking he was innocent.

One fan noted: “Of course, this hardship release is a Cyrus scam. He continues to plot with Mason, the warden, and whoever else helps on his vendetta against Drew and Sonny!”

The General Hospital spoilers suggest that Cyrus faked a heart attack. His life flashed before his eyes. Cyrus claims that he saw God. Now, he feels that he’s changed for the better because he was given a second chance at life.

“This entire Cyrus heart attack scenario is ridiculous to me. No MRI, no talk of a heart catheter, no diagnosis, what caused it? Does he need a stint or a valve replacement, etc?” one fan asked.

The General Hospital Spoilers: Cyrus Goes Back To His Old Ways

The General Hospital spoilers suggest that Cyrus went back to his old ways. He dropped the Jesus act the moment he got out of prison. Fans have a feeling that he will go back to causing trouble in no time. Others wonder what Cyrus will do now that he’s been released.

“Where will Cyrus live? How will he support himself, too old and sick to work, and all his money gone? Wouldn’t the judge want to know these things before turning him loose?”

Cyrus claims he’s no threat to the public because of his heart condition. However, some fans argue that he can pick up a gun and shoot someone. Plus, he always hires someone to do his dirty work for him. He could get released to a halfway house unless he’s financially stable.

What are your thoughts? Do you believe that Cyrus has changed? Or, do you think it’s all an act? Sound off below in the comment section. As always, check back for more GH spoilers, news, and updates.
