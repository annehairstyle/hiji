Sukhe is an Indian singer, songwriter, and music producer who has mainly worked on a number of Punjabi and Hindi-language songs. He rose to acclaim with the release of his 2015 single Jaguar (ft. Bohemia). Over the years, he has also worked on songs like Chadd Gayi, Sniper, Munda iPhone Warga, All Black, Suicide, Janni Tera Naa, Superstar, Insane, Gallan Teriyan, BAMB, Mill Lo Na, Pasand Jatt Di, 20 Saal, Nikkle Current, Morni, I Need Ya, Jaani Ve Jaani, Wah Wai Wah, Duji Vaar Pyaar, Oye Hoye, Where Baby Where, DJ Thoda Bass Bada Do, Patli Kamariya, and Mere Warga.

    Sukhe Facts

  • He is such a big fan of Bohemia that he has got his signature inked on his right forearm. Bohemia had himself signed for him.
  • He and Preet Hundal (Hundal Mohali Wala) are the founding members of the musical band, Muzical Doctorz.
  • He once said, “All through my college life, I had a great inclination towards music. I used to participate in various music competitions and perform in college fests. I had full support from my friends and girlfriend and that gave me the confidence to build a niche for ‘Muzical Doctorz’. I enjoyed my college life in proper ‘Punjabi-style’ and that fun is reflected through my songs.”
  • He later started using his Muzical Doctorz as a firm that supports talented and deserving new artists.
  • After completing his school education, he moved to Chandigarh to become a part of the Punjabi underground music movement.
  • The first fee which he received from a client for musical collaboration, he had to return it back as the client came the next day and told him that he didn’t want to work with him.
  • After the super success of his single Jaguar, he wasn’t able to record any music as he wasn’t sure whether he could create anything better. He managed to get back to the studio after rapper Badshah encouraged him to continue working.
