KUWK: Kourtney Kardashian Posts Adorable Picture Of Her And Daughter Penelope Cuddling In The Mornin

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, June 10, 2024

Kourtney Kardashian took to social media to share a super cute picture with her 7 year old daughter, Penelope. The mother-daughter duo were just snuggling together in the morning and it looked really comfy and relaxing!

Such adorable bonding time! It’s safe to say that the Keeping Up with the Kardashians star’s followers loved the sweet snap.

The caption simply read: ‘Morning cuddles,’ and also included a strawberry emoji.

The oldest of the Kardashian sisters looks as pretty as ever without any makeup on as she pouts for the camera.

She was wearing blue collared pajamas with some cute pink flowers on it while her daughter, who looked like she was still half-asleep when her mom took the photo, rocked white pajamas with baby pink horizontal stripes that also had a ‘P’ embroydered on the tiny front pocket!

Penelope, just like her cousin, North, has already developed a great sense of style at the age of just 7 so she somehow always looks like she’s a children’s model – even when she sleeps.

Furthermore, just like her entire reality TV family, she already loves luxury brands!

Penelope was filmed by her aunt Kim during dinner with family and her dad Scott Disick at Nobu last month and Penelope was wearing a Gucci trench coat.

It was a great fashion moment that Kim Kardashian could not help but capture and share on her Instagram Stories.

After they all enjoyed their dinner, Penelope showed off her coat even more while hand in hand with her dad as Scott and her mom Kourt were walking to the car.
