Morgan Hudson a.k.a. ‘Morgz’ is one of the UK’s most promising young YouTubers who is known for producing a variety of content which includes posting comedy and lifestyle videos on his YouTube channel named Morgz. Morgan has launched his own clothing line named Morgz in the past. He has a huge social media fan base with more than 1 million followers on Instagram and more than 200k followers on Twitter. He also has a YouTube channel with more than 13 million subscribers.
