Maxine Medina is a Filipino designer, actress, model, and beauty pageant titleholder who rose to fame after she was named Miss Universe Philippines 2016. From there, Maxine went on to make several well-known appearances in films and shows including Lisette in Spirit of the Glass 2: The Hunted, Tanya in Hello, Love, Goodbye, and Lorraine Prado in First Yaya.
