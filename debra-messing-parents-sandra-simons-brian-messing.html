Debra Messing parents: Sandra Simons, Brian Messing

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, June 24, 2024

Debra Lynn Messing is an American actress born on August 15, 1968. In 1993, Messing received critical acclaim for her portrayal as Harper in Tony Kushner’s pre-Broadway workshop production of Angels in America: Perestroika. She then starred in many episodes of the television show NYPD Blue in 1994 and 1995.

In 1995, Messing made her cinematic debut as the adulterous wife of main character Paul Sutton in Alfonso Arau’s A Walk in the Clouds. (Keanu Reeves) As a result of her exposure, Fox cast her as a co-star in the television sitcom Ned & Stacey. From 1995 to 1997, the show aired for two seasons.
