Sam Callahan Releases Acoustic Single &amp; Video For &quot;Stitches And Scars&quot;

Posted by Billy Koelling on Thursday, June 20, 2024

Quite possibly our favourite Sam Callahan track to-date, “Stitches & Scars” is proving to be a hit with his fans, and following suit from his previous singles “Burns Like Fire” and “Say Something“, he has released an acoustic version of the track. Not only that, but he’s also released a video to accompany the acoustic version.

Sam Callahan has been in the music industry for a number of years and we’ve been following him ever since. He’s best known by many as being a contestant on the tenth series of The X Factor, where he placed seventh. He has dipped into various music genres and has since dropped some amazing tracks. We love the direction he’s been heading in the past few years with “Burns Like Fire“, “Say Something“, and this incredible single “Stitches & Scars“; we cannot wait to see what he releases next. We hope this is all leading up to a debut album.

This song was written by Sam Callahan whilst it was produced by Jamie Sellers. This acoustic version sees him record at the venue Run Wild Music recording studio. The video really shows off how talented Sam Callahan really is as he throws everything into this song. We already know the song means a lot to him, but seeing him sing the song live in the studio amplifies the emotion he throws into the song.

Stripped back, this song is raw and full of heart. We didn’t think it was possible to make this song any better and any more emotional, but Sam Callahan proves us wrong with this acoustic version.

The acoustic version of “Stitches & Scars” is available to download and stream, right now. We adored the original track, but this acoustic version is even better, believe it or not.
