The 2019 MTV VMAs Nominees are Here!

Posted by Kelle Repass on Tuesday, June 4, 2024

This year, the MTV VMAs will take place on Monday, August 26th at the Prudential Center in Newark, NJ. Earlier today the nominees were revealed, with Ariana Grande and Taylor Swift leading the pack with 10 nominations each.

Here are the nominees for the MTV VMAs 2019 in full. :
