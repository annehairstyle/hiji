Did Carrie Underwood And Jessica Simpson Really Feud Over Tony Romo?

Posted by Kelle Repass on Thursday, June 13, 2024

Prior to Jessica Simpson and Tony Romo's romance, Romo dated Carrie Underwood back in 2007, according to Livingly. This former relationship reportedly caused some tension between the two singers from the beginning, when Simpson started dating Romo in 2008. It ramped up when Underwood and Chace Crawford also broke up that year; Simpson was supposedly worried about what Underwood's single status could mean.

This wasn't helped by the fact that Underwood told Allure that she and Romo still talked on the phone. "[Jessica] is really upset that [Tony's] still communicating with Carrie," a source told Today, who said the NFL quarterback and the "Before He Cheats" singer would email and text even after Simpson "had specifically asked Tony to stop." After Underwood made this public knowledge, Simpson responded on a San Francisco radio station, saying, "I don't understand why she would say that ... I respect Carrie, and I would never say anything like that."

In response to the growing rift, Underwood told Elle she didn't mean for anything she said to blow up. "It was just something that was said in passing ... I would never mean to say anything to hurt anybody or stir up anything, because I'm just not about drama â€” at all," Underwood said.
