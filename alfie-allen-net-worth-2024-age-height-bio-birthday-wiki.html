Alfie Allen Biography

Alfie Allen is one of the most popular and richest TV Actor who was born on September 12, 1986 in London, England, United Kingdom. Dramatic supporting actor who is best recognized as Theon Greyjoy on HBO’s Game of Thrones.

He had a small role with Natalie Portman in the 2008 historical drama The Other Boleyn Girl.

Allen’s first professional appearance was in a one-off Channel 4 comedy, You Are Here in 1998, co-written by Matt Lucas and David Walliams. The same year, Allen and his sister Lily appeared in the 1998 film Elizabeth, which was produced by their mother.
