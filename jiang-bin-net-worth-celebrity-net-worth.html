What is Jiang Bin's Net Worth?

Jiang Bin is a Chinese entrepreneur who has a net worth of $3.1 billion. Born in 1965, Bin earned his wealth as the founder of GoerTek Inc, a producer and supplier of electronic components. A graduate of Beijing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics who also holds an MBA from Tsinghua University, Bin launched GoerTek Inc in 2001, together with his wife Hu Shungmei. Thanks to the healthy cooperation with Sony, Lenovo, Apple, and Samsung, GoerTek's shares have increased more than double which brought an amazingly 44% to $599 million growth of company's revenue in 2013.

Last December, the company has stated its producing joint venture with South Korea-based Travice with a $3.2 million worth deal to get 80% of the shares of the factory that will be placed in Vietnam. Moreover, GoerTek works on its research expansion in Qingdao. Since 2007, Bin serves as company's Chairman, while his wife is the Deputy Manager and his brother Jiang Long holds the Vice Chairman position. In addition, he also started a charitable foundation supporting employees' family and helping people in disaster areas.
