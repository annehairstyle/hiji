Peregrine Pearson (born June 15, 1994) is the heir to the Cowdray Estate in West Sussex, England. He is the son of Michael Pearson, 4th Viscount Cowdray, and Marina Rosemary Parker. He has two older sisters, Eliza and Emily, and a younger brother, Lucas.

Pearson studied business, management, and marketing at the University of West London and graduated in 2016. He later became a director at Cowdray Estate, a historic property that includes a polo club, a golf club, a farm store, a café, and several cottages. He also works as the director of Weetman Developments, a property development company that focuses on strategically placed rentals.

Peregrine Pearson is an ardent polo player who plays for the Cowdray Park Polo Club. He is also interested in art, music, and fashion. He has attended several events and parties with celebrities and royals, including Princess Maria-Olympia of Greece and Denmark, whom he dated for two years before their split in September 2023.

They were relaxed and happy as they hugged and joked together. They also checked their phones and had an active conversation. Sophie Turner made her Instagram debut with Peregrine Pearson in January 2024, posting photos of the two on a ski vacation with friends.
