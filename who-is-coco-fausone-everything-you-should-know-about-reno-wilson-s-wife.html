Who Is Coco Fausone? Everything You Should Know About Reno Wilsons Wife

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, June 2, 2024

Coco Fausone is a celebrity wife and well-known yoga instructor. She is also known as Reno Wilson’s partner.

Wilson, her husband, is well-known for his roles as Howard in the sitcom The Cosby Show, Officer Carl McMillan in Mike & Molly, Stan Hill in Good Girls, Wes in The Chronicle, and Detective Tom Selway in Blind Justice. He has also appeared in other television shows.

The relationship between Fausone and Wilson has lasted for almost 20 years, during which time they have welcomed two children into the world. Find out how the pair has been able to keep their relationship going for so long.

The 29th of March is the day Fausone was born. She spent most of her childhood and teenage years dancing and choreographing, which she did while growing up in Silver Lake, LA, in the state of California.

Later in life, Patsi Simon’s daughter found employment instructing children in dance and cheerleading choreography, as well as movement. Gamynne Guillotte, who is Fausone’s sister, is a nomadic architect who received her education from the Southern California Institute of Architecture.

Coco is the first to admit that her desire for an active lifestyle stems more from her natural disposition than from any particular vocation. Running marathons, surfing, and snowboarding are three of her favorite activities, which she enjoys doing since they allow her to hone both her physical and mental strength.

In addition to that, Ms. Wilson is a swimming instructor. She enjoys nothing more than getting the spin room bouncing with hard-hitting sounds and R&B grooves. In addition to that, she is completely proficient in Spanish.
