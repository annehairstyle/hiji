Latto Put It On The Floor Again Lyrics Ft. Cardi B

Posted by Kelle Repass on Saturday, June 8, 2024
Looking for Latto Put It On The Floor Again Lyrics? Hip-hop fans are in for a treat with the release of "Put It On Da Floor Again," a dynamic collaboration of Latto and Cardi B.

Latto Put It on the Floor Again Song

If you've been scrolling through TikTok or any social media platform lately, you can't escape the infectious bop of Latto's "Put It on Da Floor." This catchy tune has taken over the internet, with its irresistible lyrics like "rip me out the plastic, I've been acting brand new" providing the perfect backdrop for stunning prom outfits, dazzling nail sets, and flawless hairstyles. People are literally breaking free from plastic, and it's a spectacle to behold.

Since its release on April 21, Latto's single has become an unstoppable force, dominating social media feeds everywhere. The rapper herself has been fueling the hype with hilarious videos of her friends rapping along to the lyrics. But it doesn't end there—Cardi B couldn't resist joining the viral trend, and she did it in the most exciting way.

On May 31, Latto dropped a teaser for the "Put It on Da Floor" remix, now titled "Put It on Da Floor Again," featuring none other than Cardi B. And guess how they announced this epic collaboration? By participating in the TikTok trend, of course. Latto shared a clip across her social media platforms, declaring, "'Put It on Da Floor,' now I'm about to 'Put It on Da Floor Again.' Remix this Friday." She then playfully motioned towards someone hidden under a black plastic bag and exclaimed, "Rip her out the plastic, she's been acting brand new." Moments later, a glammed-up Cardi B appeared.

True to their word, the dynamic duo unleashed their remix on June 2, accompanied by an iced-out music video that exudes pure swag. The visual extravaganza features surprise appearances from Offset, Cardi B's husband, and Angel Reese, a star from LSU's women's basketball team. The video opens with Latto dramatically breaking free from a plastic-wrapped package, and before you know it, Cardi B joins the scene with her explosive verse. She confidently raps, "Put a ribbon on me, I've been acting brand new / I'm not smoking on just anything, I'm smoking on you / Your bestie can join the pack, I'll smoke her too / I've been balling so hard, could've gone to LSU."

Latto's TikTok moment and the remix are the latest highlights in her already stellar year. After dropping her sophomore album, "777," in early 2022, she kicked off 2023 with the hit single "Lottery" featuring Lu Kala. She's been making waves with collaborations alongside rising talents like Mello Buckzz, Lola Brooke, and TiaCorine. As if that wasn't enough, Latto is currently rocking stages on the second leg of Lizzo's "The Special 2our" and bagged two Grammy nominations for Best New Artist and Best Melodic Rap Performance this year. A press release teasing Latto's "Put It On Da Floor Again" promises more new music, vibrant visuals, and big energy from the unstoppable artist as she embarks on the next chapter of her remarkable career. Get ready for even bigger things from The Biggest!

Explore the Dark Side of Success with Latto Put It on the Floor Again Song

The lyrics of "Put It on the Floor Again" by Latto featuring Cardi B convey a message of confidence, success, and dominance. The song is filled with braggadocious lines and assertive statements from both artists. In the first verse, Latto asserts her independence and resilience. She dismisses those who doubted her and emphasizes her financial success. She mentions spending money without hesitation and highlights her ability to maintain a luxurious lifestyle. Latto also mentions her influence and how people may lose their way trying to reach her level of success.

The chorus reinforces the theme of achievement and compares Latto's accomplishments to those of the late rapper Shawty Lo. She laughs at her financial success, emphasizing that it is no laughing matter. Latto dismisses any imagined threats from others, describing them as "imaginary smoke." She challenges anyone who claims to be up for the challenge to prove themselves on the floor, meaning they should back up their words with actions.

The post-chorus line suggests that Latto and Cardi B are confident in their status and that others should be aware of their accomplishments. It hints at the idea that people should already know about their success, and if they don't, they are out of touch. In Cardi B's verse, she joins in with her own brash and bold lines. She asserts her own confidence and likens herself to a gift, deserving of attention and adoration.

Cardi B boasts about her extravagant jewelry and criticizes other women for not doing enough. She mentions only providing support and resources to her close friends, implying that others have to earn her generosity. She also addresses haters and detractors, implying that she blocks them on social media to avoid engaging with their negativity. The chorus and post-chorus are repeated again, reinforcing the themes of success, dominance, and dismissing any challenges from others.

In the outro, Latto and Cardi B celebrate their confident and assertive personas. They refer to themselves as "real bitches" and emphasize their powerful presence. The phrase "put it on the floor again" is repeated, suggesting a continuation of their dominance and success. The lyrics of "Put It on the Floor Again" display a sense of self-assurance, financial success, and a dismissive attitude towards anyone who questions or challenges the artists' achievements.

About Latto and Cardi B

Latto and Cardi B are two powerhouse female rappers who joined forces for the hit song "Put It on the Floor Again." Both artists have made significant contributions to the music industry and have garnered a massive following.

Latto, formerly known as Mulatto, is an American rapper hailing from Atlanta, Georgia. She gained recognition after appearing on the reality TV show "The Rap Game" in 2016 and has since made a name for herself with her bold and confident lyrical style. Her music often explores themes of empowerment, success, and female empowerment. With her debut album, "777," and numerous collaborations, Latto has established herself as a rising star in the hip-hop scene.

Cardi B, born Belcalis Marlenis Almánzar, is a Grammy-winning rapper and songwriter from the Bronx, New York. She rose to prominence with her breakout hit "Bodak Yellow" in 2017 and has since become one of the most influential figures in contemporary rap music. Cardi B is known for her unfiltered lyrics, infectious personality, and captivating stage presence. Her music often reflects her life experiences, struggles, and triumphs, resonating with audiences worldwide.

The collaboration between Latto and Cardi B on "Put It on the Floor Again" showcases their incredible talent and chemistry as they deliver captivating verses with their signature flows. The song's catchy hooks, energetic production, and fierce delivery highlight the individual strengths of both artists. With their unique styles and charismatic personas, Latto and Cardi B create a dynamic and unforgettable track that appeals to fans of both artists and the wider hip-hop audience.

Their collaboration on "Put It on the Floor Again" not only showcases their musical prowess but also symbolizes the empowerment and unity among female artists in the industry. Together, Latto and Cardi B bring an unapologetic and powerful energy, celebrating their success while challenging societal norms and stereotypes.

With their combined star power and immense talent, Latto and Cardi B continue to make waves in the music industry and inspire aspiring artists worldwide.
