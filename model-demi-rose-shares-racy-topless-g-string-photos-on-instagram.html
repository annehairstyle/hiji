Model Demi Rose shares racy topless, G-string photos on Instagram

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, June 22, 2024

Demi Rose has flaunted her incredible curves in a series of racy new snaps on Instagram.

The model and influencer shared the risque shots – one in which she’s wearing a tiny white thong bikini while posing on an ocean swing – overnight.

Another of the throwbacks, which Demi Rose shared with her 18.6 million Instagram followers, saw her go topless, with only a book to preserve her modesty.

The 26-year-old, who hails from Birmingham in the UK, has been showing off her figure all week while on holiday in California.

In one post, she posed by a fire, flashing her toned legs and wearing a zebra print leotard and black floral shawl.

The beauty, who has been modelling for eight years, made news earlier this year for revealing that she’d been bullied as a kid.

“I always wanted to get into modelling and when I eventually got there I classed it as a blessing because I grew up being bullied and didn’t have many friends at all,” she told the BBC’s Radio 1 Newsbeat in June.

She also opened up during the interview about losing both of her parents in 2019, just seven months apart.

“I use [my platform] to uplift people because after what I’ve been through, I want people to have a more positive outlook on death and not see it as a bad thing,” she said.
