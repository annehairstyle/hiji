WHEN IS MOTHER’S DAY THIS YEAR?

In 2023 Mother’s Day will fall on Sunday, May 14. In Australia and the United States Mother’s Day always falls on the second Sunday of May.

But in countries like the United Kingdom, Mother’s Day was already celebrated this year on Sunday, March 19.

This is because the UK celebration of mums follows Christian traditions that date back to the Middle Ages.

More Coverage

“Mothering Sunday” takes place on the fourth Sunday of Lent and was traditionally when servants who had left their families for domestic work were allowed to return home for the day and see their loved ones, the Evening Standard reports.
