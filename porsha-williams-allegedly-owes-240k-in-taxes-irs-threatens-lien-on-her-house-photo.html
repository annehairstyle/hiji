Porsha Williams Allegedly Owes $240K In Taxes IRS Threatens Lien On Her House (Photo)

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, June 11, 2024

Porsha Williams is basking in the sweet scent of motherhood but it seems like there is trouble looming in the distance. The ‘Real Housewives of Atlanta’ star allegedly owes over $240,000 in unpaid taxes spanning the course of eight years!

The majority of the chunk of owed income tax is from 2017 — sitting at a whopping $197,907.58. The rest spans out between 2009-2016.

In the court documents obtained by the Daily Mail, Uncle Sam is warning the reality star that they will be taking her properties as payment.

“We have made a demand for payment of this liability, but it remains unpaid. Therefore, there is a lien in favor of the United States on all property and rights to property belonging to this taxpayer for the amount of these taxes, and additional penalties, interest, and costs that may accrue.”

Yikes. This isn’t the first time that Porsha has run into financial trouble.

[media-credit name=”Source: Daily Mail” align=”alignnone” width=”247″][/media-credit]

After divorcing Kordell Stewart in 2013, Porsha was hung out to dry and even had to move back in with her mom to save money. In addition to not receiving alimony, health insurance, or any of Kordell’s properties, the ‘RHOA’ star owed $18,000 in homeowner’s fees.

The court documents were filed in January of this year. However, it doesn’t seem like the future Mrs. Mckinley is the least bit worried about it.

Since January, Porsha has had a lavish gender reveal, baby shower, and is even planning a New Year’s Eve wedding.

These days, the new mom is concerned with all things baby. Pilar Jhene’ made her way into the world two weeks ago and has been slaying Instagram without even having her face shown.
