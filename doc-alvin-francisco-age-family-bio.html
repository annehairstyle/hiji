Doc Alvin Francisco - Age, Family, Bio

Posted by Billy Koelling on Sunday, June 16, 2024

Filipino TikTok star famous for his educational content in which he explains various medical topics. He is the host of the podcast Doc Alvin: The Podcast. A vlog he posted about taking the Sinovac vaccine received over 1.5 million views.

Before Fame

His August 7 2019, YouTube debut consisted of a video of him performing in a talent competition.

Trivia

His Doc Alvin YouTube channel has gained 850,000 subscribers and 51 million views. His medical content has led to his docalvinfrancisco Instagram page gaining 190,000 followers. He is a consultant and board-certified specialist.

Family Life

He was born in the Philippines. 

Associated With

 A video of him explaining a health issue that Kris Aquino was suffering from received over 2 million views.
