Dominic Thiem is an Austrian professional tennis player who is best known for being one of the most popular Austrian players in history. He achieved his highest ATP singles ranking of No. 3 in March 2020. Dominic has participated in numerous tournaments such as US Open, Australian Open, French Open, Open de Nice Côte d’Azur, ATP Tour Masters 1000, Indian Wells Masters, and many more. He has endorsed brands like Red Bull, Adidas, Tecnogym, Kia, Sky Sport, and Duravit. During the course of his career, he has been the recipient of several honors such as “Austrian Sportsman of the Year” award.
