Latavius Murray Parents: Meet Tawanna Wright, Paul Murray

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, June 9, 2024

Former running back for the Oakland Raiders Latavius Murray was born to his parents, Tawanna Wright and Paul Murray.

Not much information is available about them.

Who Is Latavius Murray?

Latavius Murray is a footballer who plays as a running back for the Buffalo Bills of the National Football League (NFL).

Born on January 18, 1990, in Florida, United States, Murray played high school football Onondaga Central High School in New York, where he was an All-league and All-Central New York selection for three seasons.

Murray had a successful collegiate career at the University of Central Florida (UCF), where he finished with 132 rushing yards and three rushing touchdowns in his freshman year, and was an All-C-USA First-team selection as a senior.

Also in college, Murray made it to the to the “College Football Performance Awards All-Purpose Trophy Watch List, the College Football Performance Awards Special Teams Watch List, and the Doak Walker Award Watch List.”

In 2013, Murray was drafted by the Oakland Raiders as 181st overall pick in the sixth round of the 2013 NFL draft, and signed a four-year contract worth $2.26 million with the team.

Consequently, he played for the Minnesota Vikings, New Orleans Saints, Baltimore Ravens, New Orleans Saints, and the Denver Broncos.

On May 1, 2023, Murray was signed by the Buffalo Bills to a one-year contract.

His current NFL statistics shows a record of 6,252 rushing yards, 4.2 rushing average, 55 rushing touchdowns, 222 receptions, 1,501 receiving yards, 2 receiving touchdowns.
