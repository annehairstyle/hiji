Peter Porte And Miranda Wilson Talk Mother-Son Scheming In Salem

Posted by Artie Phelan on Monday, June 3, 2024

Days of Our Lives (DOOL) spoilers hint that mother-son duo, Dimitri Von Leuschner (Peter Porte) and Megan Hathaway (Miranda Wilson) are set to cause a tremendous amount of chaos in Salem. Joining their “team” to take over DiMera Enterprises is Kristen DiMera (Stacy Haiduk) and it sounds like the trio will unleash some major drama that is sure to shock the rest of the family and town.

Days Of Our Lives Spoilers – Son & Mom Will Plot, Scheme, & Plan

Miranda Wilson and Peter Porte recently sat down with Michael Fairman’s channel to talk about what their characters have in store for the land of Salem. The duo notes there will be a lot of “plotting”, as they plan out a “pretty complicated” scheme that they have spent months on.

Still, neither Wilson nor Porte got into the details of their characters’ scheme (or even what the end goal is); however, from the sounds of DOOL spoilers, they won’t be afraid to ruffle some feathers and go to the extreme to get what they want.

Days Of Our Lives Updates – Dimitri Von Leuschner Is A Momma’s Boy & Will Follow Instructions

Days spoilers for Monday, June 5th imply that Megan will push her son to marry Kristen. That’s more than a little gross considering that she is his aunt. Still, digging into the DOOL history vault, fans know that they aren’t blood-related as Kristen is adopted and to further justify things (although it still seems uber-creepy), Kristen never grew up with Megan around and Dimitri never grew up with Kristen around. With that being said, still mighty awkward, but the DiMera family has been known to go to extremes to get their way.

While it is an over-the-top suggestion, Porte notes during the interview that “at the heart” of it all, his character is willing to do “anything” for his mom. Sounds like a real momma’s boy; however, while Megan is “pushing” Dimitri on the idea, will Kristen be sold? She still has her limits!
