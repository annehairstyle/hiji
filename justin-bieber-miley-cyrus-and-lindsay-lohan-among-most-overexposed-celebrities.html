Justin Bieber, Miley Cyrus and Lindsay Lohan Among Most Overexposed Celebrities

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, June 30, 2024

You couldn’t go a day in 2013 without seeing a news headline from either Miley Cyrus, Justin Bieber or Lindsay Lohan. Lately, Cyrus and Bieber have been making Lohan look like an angel. Regardless, there’s no question that the threesome is easily the most overexposed group of celebrities in the United States of America.

According to E! Online, in a recent poll released by Forbes Miley Cyrus, Justin Bieber and Lindsay Lohan were amongst the top overexposed celebrities this past year. The list ranks more than 6,600 celebrities based on a 46-part criteria based on public opinion. Number one on the list? Justin Bieber, who received 67% confirmation that he is overexposed by the public, by far the most out of any celebrities.

Certainly, Bieber, Cyrus, and Lohan live in infamy is the driver of their overexposure. However, not all celebrities on the list were misbehaving troublemakers. In between Bieber and Lohan at number two was Kim Kardashian. Those three were of course followed by Miley Cyrus, who made more news this year for explicit nonsense than Kardashian… go figure.

Coming in at number five was Honey Boo Boo – who can argue that one? I think this list is just confirmation that we’ve had too much Bieber, Cyrus and Lohan this past year. Unfortunately, it doesn’t appear as if we’re getting a break anytime soon.
